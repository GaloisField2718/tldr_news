 STUDY FINDS SIZE REALLY DOES MATTER WHEN IT COMES TO HOME SCREEN ADS
(2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Connected TV home screen ads deliver the strongest attention.
Audiences view 76% of these ads, and 59% of that viewing counts as
active attention, which means eyes on both the screen and the ad. They
also generate 2.5x more active attention than linear TV and avoid the
steep drop seen on social platforms, where 80% of attention
disappears. The viewing device drives attention more than the platform
or creative. As measurement improves, CTV home screens will become
even more valuable for high engagement. 

 HOW VINE'S SIX-SECOND VIDEOS BUILT THE BLUEPRINT FOR TIKTOK (3 MINUTE
READ) [14] 

 Vine created the core format that powers TikTok, Instagram Reels, and
YouTube Shorts. Its 6-second-long looping videos became the foundation
of the modern short-form ecosystem. Vine's technical innovations
shaped today's platforms, and its role in launching major creators
showed the potential of the format. Its failure came from weak creator
monetization, a problem that still affects platforms. The platform's
cultural and design impact remains central to how billions consume
content, even as new startups try to revive its original appeal.
