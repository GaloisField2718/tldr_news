 HOW TO GET CITED IN AI ANSWERS (5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 AI search rewards consistent authority and authentic brand signals.
To increase visibility in AI answers, prioritize publishing
high-quality, expertise-driven content on your own site or in

pick up over time.

 Aldi has shifted its social media strategy to presenting itself as a

 10 B2B NEWSLETTER TIPS (1 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 B2B newsletters work best as short-form, native content that teaches
or inspires rather than just linking to blog posts. Send them
consistently, monthly or bi-monthly, and from a real person, not a
generic address. Avoid generic titles, focus on the reader, and keep
copy skimmable. Reserve pitches for the PS, use images or videos
sparingly, and publish on a newsletter platform for extra reach. Split
dense content across multiple issues to keep them readable and
engaging.

 PULL VS. PUSH MARKETING (4 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Push marketing usually shows linear or diminishing returns and
requires sustained spend to maintain reach. Pull marketing, by
contrast, builds momentum over time but depends on audience discovery
and organic engagement. Push efforts can risk brand fatigue and
low-quality interactions when overused, while pull tactics may
underdeliver without a clear path to distribution.
