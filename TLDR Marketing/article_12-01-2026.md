 SHOULD LINKS BE UNDERLINED? IT'S WORTH A/B TESTING (4 MINUTE READ)
[12] 

 Underlining links improves usability and accessibility, even if it
affects visual appeal. Google's tests showed that removing underlines
reduced clicks, slowed interaction, and lowered revenue. Hover
underlines had little impact and were shipped for competitive reasons.
Details matter because hover states and larger targets can help
recognition, while color-only links hurt performance. Accessibility
standards warn against relying on color alone, since many users are
colorblind and hover cues fail on touch devices.

 Streaming is expanding sports marketing reach by bringing in younger
