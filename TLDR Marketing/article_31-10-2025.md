 TUBI STUDY REVEALS STREAMING'S GROWING ROLE IN HOLIDAY SHOPPING (3
MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Streaming is now a major driver of holiday shopping. Nearly half of
viewers make a purchase after seeing an ad. 71% shop while streaming,
often influenced by the content or ads they watch. Authenticity
matters, with 77% more likely to buy from independent creators and 43%
drawn to brands that feel real or high-quality. Viewers also engage
spontaneously, with 47% shopping impulsively and 60% buying
immediately when an ad catches their attention. 

 WHY SO MANY PUBLISHERS ARE LAUNCHING BRAND MARKETING CAMPAIGNS RIGHT
NOW (6 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Publishers like Reuters, NBC News, and Wired are running their first
brand marketing campaigns, moving into subways and social feeds with
awareness ads. They are doing it now because referral traffic is
declining, AI is threatening search visibility, and Gen Z doesn't know
these brands. With AI platforms scraping publisher data, brand equity
now sets negotiation leverage. These campaigns aren't just for vanity,
they're pre-activation plays for subscriptions and future-proofing.

 HOW TO GET 50% INCREASE IN LEADS W/ 1 LINE OF TEXT (13 MINUTE READ)
[6] 

 A top hospitality school increased lead form submissions by 50% by
placing a single authentic testimonial directly above the inquiry
form. The quote was short, specific, and paired with a real student
photo. This small shift addressed user hesitation at the moment of
decision. Rather than investing in a website redesign or new traffic
sources, the team focused on trust-building where and when it mattered
most to potential leads. 

 UDEMY'S FROM PLATFORM TO POWERHOUSE MARKETING STRATEGY (5 MINUTE
READ) [7] 

 Udemy built its growth by turning its business model into its
marketing engine. It created a marketplace where instructors promote
their own courses, driving organic traffic and lowering acquisition
costs. Its SEO-focused strategy and 200,000+ course catalog capture
long-tail search demand, while localized pricing and campaigns across
180+ countries make the platform feel personal and accessible. Udemy
for Business added a strong B2B arm that targets companies through
thought leadership, subscriptions, and success stories, creating
sustainable long-term revenue.

 INTRODUCING FIGMA WEAVE: THE NEXT GENERATION OF AI-NATIVE CREATION AT
FIGMA (2 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Figma has acquired Weavy and rebranded it as Figma Weave, a new
AI-native platform for image, video, animation, and motion design.
Weave combines model flexibility with professional editing on a
browser-based canvas, giving users full control over lighting,
masking, and color grading. The acquisition supports Figma's strategy
to pair AI with human craft, enabling creatives to refine outputs
beyond first prompts and build more distinctive content pipelines.

 BETH O'MALLEY WANTS EMAIL MARKETERS TO STOP BEING HARD ON THEMSELVES
(14 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 A good email strategy starts with intention. The â€œwhyâ€ behind
each message matters as much as what's sent. Standout emails sound
human, use thoughtful segmentation, and prioritize meaningful
communication over volume. Focus on impact rather than vanity metrics,

 ARIELLE NISSENBLATT ON CURATING PODCASTS AND EXPANDING THE CREATOR
ECONOMY (5 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 EarBuds is a weekly podcast newsletter that helps listeners find
high-quality shows and helps creators reach engaged audiences. Each
issue features 5 episodes tied to a theme, including one from the
curator's own show and others they recommend. Founded in 2017 to solve
discovery challenges and give independent creators more visibility, it
has grown into a platform with a podcast, website, and active social
presence. The focus is on building lasting listener relationships and
giving creators tools to present their content thoughtfully.
