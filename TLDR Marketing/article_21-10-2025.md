 THE GUIDE TO PRICING PAGE STORYTELLING (4 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Good pricing pages use storytelling to differentiate a company and
guide customers toward decisions. Many companies make mistakes, such
as focusing on product details instead of pricing philosophy,
overloading pricing menus, or neglecting meaningful social proof.
Fixes include leading with a clear pricing philosophy, showcasing only
the most relevant features, and adding context-rich testimonials or
interactive tools that clarify plan value. 

 THE REAL META AD MIX (2 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Leading advertisers are balancing video and static assets to support
both demand creation and conversion. Static ads are effective for
urgency and personalization, while video builds awareness and intent
over time. Attribution systems often misrepresent video's contribution
to performance by over-crediting lower-funnel conversions. A portfolio
approach that includes both asset types aligns better with long-term
growth and platform diversity.

 HOW TO USE THE USER ID FEATURE IN GOOGLE ANALYTICS (2 MINUTE READ)
[10] 

 The User ID feature in GA4 lets you track individual users across
sessions and devices by linking an internal identifier from your CRM
or database, but it requires a login system. To implement it
correctly, assign each user a unique, consistent, non-personal ID,
send it only after login, and reset it to null on logout. Avoid empty
or invalid values, ensure user consent, and don't use User ID as a
custom dimension to prevent data issues. When configured with the
correct reporting identity, GA4 can retroactively connect pre-login
events and enable more accurate tracking and segmentation.
