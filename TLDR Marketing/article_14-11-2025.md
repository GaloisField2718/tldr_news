 MARK ZUCKERBERG WANTS ALL OF ADVERTISING. ANDY JASSY MAY HAVE A
STRONGER CLAIM (4 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Amazon appears to be in a stronger position than Meta to control more
of the advertising ecosystem due to its growing AI tools, unmatched
commerce signals, and major expansion into streaming TV. It claims it
can help brands reach 90% of the streaming market through its data
partnerships, and it has 80 million authenticated US households
feeding its targeting systems. Its new AI tools for creative,
planning, and campaign management suggest it could replace many
functions handled by agencies, even as some marketers question how
fast these tools will be adopted. Meta continues to push its own AI
vision, but Amazon's deeper footprint in TV, ad tech, and first-party
data gives it an advantage in this emerging battle. 

 WOMEN ARE DISCUSSING THEIR NEXT CAR PURCHASE ON REDDIT, IS YOUR BRAND
ON THE SHORTLIST? (3 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Half of Reddit's 185M weekly US users are now women, many planning
car purchases. Three in five expect to buy within two years, and one
in four within 6 to 12 months. Reddit is the top platform female car
buyers trust, with 68% preferring real people's insights over
comparison sites. Two-thirds want brands to share product details, and
many engage in auto and life-planning communities where intent is
high. Providing useful content and joining discussions through AMAs
and Conversation Ads helps brands connect with buyers as they decide.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 ANALYZE WHAT CHATGPT USERS ARE ASKING USING YOUR WEBSITE'S BOT
TRAFFIC (4 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Analyzing server logs for ChatGPT-User bot visits can reveal what
topics real ChatGPT users are prompting about. This provides a
data-driven alternative to unreliable third-party prompt volume
estimates. Websites can identify which subjects attract the most
AI-driven attention by filtering logs for ChatGPT-User activity and
clustering pages by topic using LLMs or vector embeddings. Comparing
bot visits with human referral traffic highlights whether content is
informative but unengaging or blocked from bot access. 

 HOW THOUGHT LEADERSHIP ADS DRIVE PLG GROWTH ON LINKEDIN (2 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 About 80% of the LinkedIn budget goes to Thought Leadership ads that
drive strong PLG signups with minimal CTAs. Edited posts with clear
next steps and tested objectives perform best, with Engagement
outperforming Reach. Diverse voices, a creator engine, and persona
targeting keep content relevant, while frequent rotation prevents
fatigue. Next steps include adding visuals for brand recall and
featuring more employees, customers, and micro-influencers. 

 WINNING WITH PINTEREST ADS: HOW TO INCREASE YOUR B2C SALES (12 MINUTE
READ) [10] 

 Pinterest advertising presents a cost-effective alternative to Meta
and TikTok, especially for businesses targeting planners and DIY
consumers. Its visual search structure supports long-lived campaign
assets, enabling ongoing reach well beyond the paid window. Brands
should lead with high-performing offers, repurpose content with
Pinterest-specific formatting, and structure campaigns to guide users
from discovery to purchase. Strategic use of the Pinterest tag,
interest targeting, and act-alike audiences increases effectiveness
and creates retargeting opportunities.

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 SOCIABLE.AI (TOOL) [11] 

 Sociable is a social media automation tool that helps brands identify
trending content, place timely top comments, and automate replies
across platforms. It filters content relevant to a brand, predicts
viral potential, and tracks impressions and interactions. The platform
supports outbound community engagement, allows for on-brand
AI-generated responses, manages DMs, and provides analytics. 

 LET AI DO THE HARD PARTS OF YOUR HOLIDAY SHOPPING (4 MINUTE READ)
[12] 

 Google is expanding its AI-driven shopping capabilities to simplify
purchase journeys and reduce friction during the holiday season. Key
upgrades include conversational search in AI Mode with dynamic product
comparisons, integration of shoppable insights in the Gemini app, and
agentic tools that automate local inventory checks and price-based
checkouts. These features leverage the Shopping Graph and Duplex
technology to increase conversion, enhance user efficiency, and
support both online and offline commerce.
