 AI PROMPT ENGINEERING TO WORK SMARTER, NOT HARDER (5 MINUTE READ)
[11] 

 Meta prompting helps you reverse-engineer effective prompts to save
time, while basic prompting rules, such as assigning roles and giving
clear instructions, improve output quality. Teaching AI a specific
voice and tone allows for more authentic communication, and asking it
to slow down produces more accurate and thoughtful responses. Other
methods include the AI sandwich, where humans draft, AI expands, and
humans edit, and fill-in-the-blank prompting to complete unfinished
writing. 

 FROM GHIBLI TO NANO BANANA: HOW AI TOOLS BECOME CULTURAL PHENOMENA (4
MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Nano Banana went viral after its August launch, gaining 10M users and
powering 200M image edits within weeks. It runs 2-3X faster than
GPT-4o. Growth came from frictionless onboarding, share-optimized
outputs, and cultural timing. The trend highlights how consumer AI
adoption pushes enterprise uptake and raises expectations for speed,
simplicity, and virality in product design.
