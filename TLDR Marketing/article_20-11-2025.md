 THE FUTURE OF MEDIA IS BEING BUILT ON YOUTUBE (6 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 YouTube is becoming the hub for new media. It's drawing legacy
publishers and independent creators. The Daily Beast and Complex are
shifting to video to reach younger audiences and protect against AI
disruption. Its monetization tools, high-quality attention, and low
production barriers make it more profitable than text or short-form
social platforms. Vodcasts, live-streams, and AI-assisted production
are fueling growth. Creators now treat it as their main distribution
point, building audiences before converting fans to subscriptions or
donations.

 HOW I RUN A LINKEDIN B2B GROWTH ENGINE (2 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 This author uses a simple LinkedIn playbook to drive over $1M+ in
pipeline while spending only $300 per month on ads. It starts with
consistent founder-led content that helped the startup reach nearly

 8 MARKETING TACTICS THAT WORK ON BOOMERS, BUT YOUNGER GENERATIONS SEE
THROUGH (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Marketing tactics that still appeal to Boomers, such as expert
endorsements, emotional testimonials, and physical catalogs, often
miss the mark with younger generations who expect transparency and
verification. Boomers respond to traditional signals of trust like
phone access, scarcity messaging, and authority figures, while Gen Z
and Millennials rely on peer reviews, independent research, and
digital convenience.

 A/B TEST RESULT: PAGE LOAD TIME (PERFORMANCE) PATTERN (2 MINUTE READ)
[9] 

 Talabat A/B tested a decrease in its homepage load time, which led to
a measurable lift in revenue and orders. The time-to-interactive
decreased from about 2.1 seconds to about 1.0 second, a drop of about
58%. As a result, revenue per user increased by 0.36% and orders per
user rose by 0.34%. The results align with prior evidence that even
small performance gains can drive meaningful business impact. 

 MORE WAYS TO SPOT, SHAPE, AND UNDERSTAND AI-GENERATED CONTENT ON
TIKTOK (2 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 TikTok is expanding its tools to help users control, identify, and
understand AI-generated content. This includes a new setting that lets
users choose how much AI-generated content appears in their feed. It
is also adding invisible watermarking to improve labeling reliability,
which supports its existing systems that have already labeled over 1.3
billion videos.

 YOU CAN'T DISMISS AI ADS AS SLOP WHEN THEY'RE WINNING IN TESTING (7
MINUTE READ) [11] 

 AI-assisted ads outperform traditional ones, scoring higher on
emotion, attention, and distinctiveness. Even when recognized as

 BECOMING THE CREATIVE DIRECTOR OF YOUR CAREER (3 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Think of a career as a creative project rather than a ladder to
climb. Own the professional narrative, shape what work matters, and
design how presence is perceived in every interaction. Treat roles,
projects, and skills as parts of a larger body of work. Optimize for
growth and enjoyment rather than just titles or salary. Cultivate
instincts for when to pivot, pause, or fully commit, and bring
intentionality and play to daily work. This mindset transforms work
into a living composition, where each project contributes to a
purposeful career. 

 US USERS WANT TO QUIT INSTAGRAM MORE THAN OTHER SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS
(6 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 US search data shows that nearly 2M users per month are seeking ways
to delete or deactivate social media accounts. Instagram leads the
list, followed by TikTok, Snapchat, Facebook, and X. Instagram's high
user dissatisfaction is tied to a growing recognition of addiction
patterns driven by algorithmic design and self-comparison loops.
TikTok also draws concern for its time distortion and high
reactivation rate, while Facebook sees regret-driven account
recoveries despite privacy concerns. Platforms like Reddit, Pinterest,
and Threads generate minimal exit interest due to stronger perceived
value, niche use cases, or integration barriers.
