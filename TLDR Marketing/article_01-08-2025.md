 HOW TO GET QUOTED IN THE PRESS (6 MINUTE READ) [7]

smart workflow. Start by identifying the reporters and platforms that
influence your audience, then follow and engage with them
authentically. Use tools like HARO or Qwoted to find opportunities,
and send short, relevant pitches with insightful, jargon-free quotes.
Once you're featured, amplify and repurpose the coverage to extend its
impact.
