 THE OPEN WEB IS SHIFTING FROM AN AUDIENCE ECONOMY TO A CONTEXT
ECONOMY (5 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 The next phase of ad tech is shifting from knowing who someone is to
understanding what's happening around them right now. Instead of
relying on fixed audience segments built from past behavior, new
bidding infrastructure lets ad auctions read signals like timing and
nearby activity in real time. Containerizing that decision-making
inside the auction itself cuts response times by roughly 80%, giving
algorithms enough time to weigh context instead of just matching an ID
to a segment. Advertising stops chasing who people are and starts
responding to the moment they're actually in.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 SEO & AI TIP: IN AI OVERVIEWS, BEING RECOMMENDED IS THE GOAL, NOT
BEING CITED (2 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 AI Overviews recommend brands based on commercial prompts, not
informational content. Ranking for informational queries rarely pays
off downstream in commercial visibility. Build content around
high-value commercial queries and support each page with pricing,
comparison, methodology, and brand information that helps AI justify
recommending your business. 

 HOW TO USE CONTENT PILLARS TO BUILD YOUR SOCIAL STRATEGY (7 MINUTE
READ) [11] 

 The best-performing content pillar systems in 2026 work as concentric
circles instead of a flat list of themes. A narrow core pillar builds
algorithmic and audience authority faster than a broad one, while
adjacent pillars share that same audience and add new angles without
diluting it. A pillar only counts as adjacent if it still reinforces
why someone followed the account in the first place, since anything
broader just makes the feed generic. 

 HOW TO MEASURE THE IMPACT OF AI SEARCH THE RIGHT WAY (8 MINUTE READ)
[12] 

 AI search is driving real demand, but most teams still measure it
through referral traffic, which rarely shows up. Only around 1% of
users click AI citations, and much of that traffic is misattributed or
shows up as direct, making last-click models unreliable. Instead, use
an вҖңAI visibility ladderвҖқ that tracks leading indicators like
crawl rate and citation share. It should also look at quality signals
like how a brand is positioned in answers, and lagging indicators like
self-reported attribution and win rate.

 A SUBTLE GOOGLE ADS BIDDING CHANGE (2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Campaign targets now matter more than past overperformance, so review
every target-based bidding campaign before Google changes bidding on
August 17. Campaigns that currently exceed their target ROAS may lose
efficiency because Google will optimize toward the set goal instead of
continuing to outperform it. Raise targets in small steps when recent
performance is more than 20% above the current goal, or set a higher
custom target if stronger performance is realistic. Advertisers that
care more about conversion volume than efficiency should consider
switching to a maximize bidding strategy. 

 BRANDOM: WHY FANDOM IS THE FUTURE OF AI SEARCH (52 MINUTE VIDEO) [14]


 AI search is moving away from brand-owned content toward third-party
voices on Reddit, YouTube, and LinkedIn. LLMs tend to trust sources
that sit further from brand control, which makes customer, employee,
and community advocacy more important than blog content. Reddit is
emerging as a key offsite channel, similar to what blogs were for SEO,
but requires real participation instead of spam. The shift is also
blurring lines across SEO, PR, and community, while measurement relies
on signals like share of voice and citations.

 COULD EA'S NEW ADVERTISING PLATFORM OPEN THE FLOODGATES FOR IN-GAME
ADS? (3 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Six years after apologizing for intrusive ads in UFC 4 and disabling
them, EA is betting that real-time, targeted placements can succeed
where static pop-ups failed. The new platform is built to enhance
gameplay rather than interrupt it, giving brands the ability to adjust
messaging in real time and measure impact for the first time. However,
most gaming ad spend still lives on mobile, where casual play
tolerates interruption more easily. Console and PC audiences treat
games as a more focused experience that ads have historically
struggled to enter without friction. 

 BUILDING THE NATION'S LARGEST SOCCER LEAGUE (7 MINUTE READ) [17] 

 Community creates stronger long-term growth than paid acquisition
when the customer experience gives people a reason to return and
invite others. The Footy League built scalable operations behind its
community, including customer service, registration systems,
sponsor-ready media, and local experiences that support national
expansion without losing its identity. It uses paid marketing to
attract first-time players, but newsletter marketing and word of mouth
create higher retention and stronger referrals. Brand comes from the
customer experience instead of visual identity, so values need to
appear in everyday interactions such as onboarding, training, and
support.
