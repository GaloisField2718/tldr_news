 INTRODUCING THREADS COMMUNITIES: FIND YOUR PEOPLE (2 MINUTE READ)
[11] 

 Threads is launching public communities that let users join
topic-based spaces to share and discuss interests such as basketball,
TV, and K-pop. The feature builds on custom feeds and topic tags and
is currently being tested across more than 100 popular topics. Each
community includes personalized elements like custom Like emojis and
public membership listings that appear on user profiles. Threads also
plans to add contributor badges and ranking features to highlight top
members and surface the most relevant posts.

 DOES X PREMIUM REALLY BOOST YOUR REACH? AN ANALYSIS OF 18M+ POSTS (5
MINUTE READ) [12] 

 X Premium posts get far more visibility and engagement than regular
user posts. Premium accounts average 10X more reach per post, while
Premium+ users see over twice the reach of standard Premium.
Engagement rates are higher across Premium tiers at 0.49% to 0.55%,
compared to 0% for regular accounts. Text and video posts perform
best, while link posts are penalized, especially for non-Premium
users. 

 HOW TO BECOME A BETTER COPYWRITER (11 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Focus on writing that drives revenue, not on sounding eloquent. Talk
to customers or sales teams to understand pain points, objections, and
language. Study competitors' ads to find proven messages and keep a
swipe file of strong examples. Specialize in one platform, learn its
algorithm, and track ROI. 

 THE GAP MARKETING: A DEEP DIVE INTO THEIR REVIVAL (8 MINUTE READ)
[14] 

 Gap rebuilt its brand identity by shifting from a mall staple to a
cultural storyteller. New leadership focused on creativity and
cultural relevance while campaigns with Tyla, Troye Sivan, and KATSEYE
connected emotionally and visually with Gen Z. Gap also co-created
products with influencers like Julia Huynh and made social content
feel organic rather than promotional. The results showed up in sales
and earned media.
