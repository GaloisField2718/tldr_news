 OPTIMIZE CAMPAIGNS BY PAIRING TRADITIONAL AND DIGITAL MEDIA
EFFECTIVELY (2 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 A study analyzing over 1,000 global advertising campaigns found that
pairing traditional media, such as TV and outdoor ads, with digital
channels like YouTube and Facebook can increase key brand metrics by
more than 50%. Successful campaigns adapt their media mix to specific
objectives, using TV with YouTube to build broad awareness and
combining Facebook with in-store promotions to drive local engagement
while prioritizing shifts in brand perception and consideration over
simple reach. 

 HOW THE FCC'S POTENTIAL OVERHAUL OF A 20-YEAR-OLD RULE COULD AFFECT
TV ADVERTISING (5 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 With TV ad revenue falling and streaming ad dollars surging, the FCC
is weighing whether to scrap a rule that caps broadcast ownership at
39% of the national audience. Broadcasters and industry groups say
loosening the limit would allow networks to become more modern,
attract younger viewers, and better compete with tech platforms like
Google and Facebook. Opponents caution that such a move could give
more power to large corporations or politically aligned owners,
reducing competition and media diversity.

 HOW TO RECLAIM AI SEARCH TRAFFIC FROM HALLUCINATED URLS (1 MINUTE
READ) [6] 

 Hallucinated URLs are web addresses generated by AI that look real
but don't exist on a site. To reclaim AI search traffic from them,
filter analytics to show only AI assistant visits, export the data to
Google Sheets, and use an Apps Script to check each URL's HTTP status.
Identify URLs with a 404 status and significant traffic, then set up
301 redirects to relevant existing pages. This approach turns
non-existent, AI-invented URLs into traffic drivers while revealing
the kinds of content LLMs expect your site to have.

 THE PROS & CONS OF EVERY AUTOMATED BIDDING STRATEGY IN GOOGLE (11
MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Google Ads offers eight automated bidding strategies, with some
falling under Smart Bidding, which uses auction-time optimization for
conversions or conversion value. Each strategy serves different goals,
such as Maximize Conversions for lead volume, Maximize Conversion
Value for high-value leads, Target CPA for cost control, and Target
ROAS for profitability. Successful automated bidding requires accurate
conversion tracking, realistic targets, and enough recent conversion
data. Advertisers can mix strategies across campaigns, ad groups, or
portfolios based on goals and data availability. While automation
saves time, performance should be monitored regularly to ensure
campaigns meet objectives.
