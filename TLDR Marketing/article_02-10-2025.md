 THE MECHANICS OF NEUROBRANDING: BRAND STRATEGY THAT STARTS IN THE
FEELS (10 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 This article describes how to use neuroscience to build real brand
preference. The first mechanic is immersion. Identify where users
interact with the brand and make those moments emotionally rich. This
could be a scented store, tactile packaging, or a sensory-rich waiting
experience like Disney's Rise of the Resistance queue. The second
mechanism is neural coupling. Brands that tell stories that reflect
customer identity build loyalty without selling. The third mechanic is
subconscious priming. Repetition of a distinct color, sound, or phrase
wires emotional associations over time. 

 A CRASH COURSE ON LAGGING AND LEADING METRICS (4 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Lagging metrics measure outcomes but are slow to respond. Leading
metrics give directional signals but need interpretation. Most teams
over-index on lagging metrics like NPS or revenue per user because
they feel more concrete and definitive. However, these often come too
late to guide action. Marketers can build better dashboards by pairing
each lagging metric with 2 to 3 leading ones. For example, tie
conversion rate to DAUs or time to first value. This dual tracking
helps steer strategy in real time and spot issues like churn or
underused features quickly.
