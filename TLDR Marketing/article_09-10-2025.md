 THE RECENCY BIAS THAT'S RESHAPING AI SEARCH (8 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 AI search models show a strong bias toward newer content, reshaping
how visibility works across platforms like ChatGPT. A study by Waseda
University found that timestamps alone can shift top-10 results by up
to 5 years, move pieces up to 95 ranks, and reverse 8-25% of relevance
judgments. Older, high-quality content is systematically buried, while
newer, lower-quality material often ranks higher. GPT-4o and
Qwen2.5-72B are the most resistant to this bias, while LLaMA3-8B and
GPT-3.5-turbo are the most affected. To maintain visibility, brands
should update content regularly and use strategies like timestamp
management and temporal context signals.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 MAKE YOUR DISCOUNTS A BIT HARDER TO GET (3 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Requiring customers to complete a small task to access a discount can
significantly increase sales. Across eight experiments with over
100,000 participants, redemption rates rose by up to 59% when people
had to enter a promo code or solve a CAPTCHA instead of receiving an
automatic discount. The effect is strongest for moderate discounts and
small tasks but weakens when effort or discount size increases. The
research suggests that people feel more rewarded when they earn a
discount, making them more likely to redeem it and complete a
purchase. 

 HOW TO TURN CHATGPT INTO YOUR BEST PIPELINE SOURCE (6 MINUTE READ)
[8] 

 Docebo, a public enterprise learning management system, attributes
12.7% of its high-intent pipeline to AI discovery, a 5x increase YoY,
driven by a one-person content operation focused on answer engine
optimization. The team prioritizes branded search and monitors
share-of-voice within AI queries, using tools like Xfunnel to
benchmark visibility. Branded terms now account for 85% of Docebo's
search traffic as non-branded clicks decline with the rise of AI
overviews. Success is rooted in late-funnel keyword targeting, direct
attribution from demo forms, and AI-optimized content structure that
includes FAQs and comparative formats preferred by LLMs.

 Brands need clean, consistent data before the holiday season to make
accurate decisions and avoid wasted ad spend. Use a single verified

 WHAT ELECTRONIC MUSIC FORETELLS ABOUT GENERATIVE AI (3 MINUTE READ)
[12] 

 This piece uses the history of electronic music to explain concerns
around gen AI in creative fields. New technology often sparks fear,
first that machines replace human artistry and later that democratized
tools flood the market with mediocre content. Moby's Play shows how
cheap, accessible production can create music that becomes ubiquitous
but often serves as background noise, foreshadowing today's AI slop.
AI will greatly increase content output, but human attention is
limited. The challenge will be selection, not creation, and success
will depend on systems that highlight meaningful work amid the noise. 

 HOW TO CREATE A CRISP, USEFUL BUYER PERSONA (10 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 The process of building a persona must focus on identifying the
actual concerns, motivations, and objections expressed by customers
during the buying process. Personas should capture the triggers that
prompt engagement, the goals buyers seek, the barriers they encounter,
and the criteria used in selecting a solution. Interviews can reveal
deeper insights than surveys or CRM data alone, especially around
issues like cost, where surface-level responses may obscure root
causes such as ROI misalignment. A complete persona includes profile
context, path to purchase, and representative quotes to inform sales
tactics and product development.
