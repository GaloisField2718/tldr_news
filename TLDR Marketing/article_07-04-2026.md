NEWS & TRENDS

 WHY APPLE'S APP STORE IS SUDDENLY BEING FLOODED WITH NEW APPS (4
MINUTE READ) [4] 

 The App Store is seeing a surge in new apps, with launches up 30% in
2025 and 84% in early 2026. AI-powered coding tools now let anyone
create functional apps quickly. Most new apps focus on productivity,
photo, video, and weather, though Apple has begun restricting apps
that let users build other apps on iPhones. The influx makes it harder
to find high-quality apps and has slowed some reviews. 

 SEARCH MOVES BEYOND KEYWORDS AS AI RESHAPES AD TARGETING (3 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 Search behavior is shifting from short keywords to longer,
conversational queries that trigger AI-generated responses, changing
how ads are matched and served. Google reports that improvements in
language models have reduced irrelevant ads by roughly 40% by
interpreting context and intent rather than relying on exact keyword
matches. Overall traffic trends have remained stable year over year,
but click quality has improved as users arrive with clearer intent.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 GUEST POSTING PROCESS ON LINKEDIN (2 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Identify relevant sites using searches like “best [your category]
after:2025” and review existing partners for high response rates.
Begin with small placements to build trust, then pitch a guest post
that serves the host audience and covers BOFU topics they haven't
addressed. Write content optimized for SEO, support it with backlinks
if possible, and reach out from a company email with a multi-step
sequence emphasizing mutual benefit. This process builds backlinks and
AI search visibility. 

 SEO A/B TESTING: 5 EASY EXPERIMENTS TO DRIVE TRAFFIC (7 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 Title tags can be tested with more compelling variations to boost
CTR. Content can be optimized for Google AI Overviews by adding
concise summaries, FAQs, and clean HTML structure. Internal link
placement can be adjusted from high-authority pages with keyword-rich
anchors. Content length and depth can be expanded with additional
sections, visuals, and supporting data. Schema markup can be
implemented via JSON-LD, CMS plugins, or Google Tag Manager to enable
rich results and increase CTR. 

 WIN REPORT: HOW REUSING WINNING COPY INCREASED CONVERSIONS BY 82% (2
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Reusing proven homepage copy in an exit-intent lightbox increased
conversions by 82% by capturing visitors about to leave. Many users
engaged with the site like a travel magazine without entering the
booking funnel. The lightbox used a tested headline, popular activity
links, and a clear call-to-action to guide users toward planning a
trip. This approach worked by delivering a timely prompt without
altering existing content.

 HOW GROW & CONVERT HELPED LEVEL AI APPEAR IN AI SEARCH RESULTS FOR
100+ BOFU PROMPTS (7 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Grow & Convert helped Level AI appear in over 100 AI search prompts.
They targeted 50+ high buying-intent keywords with detailed content.
The content highlighted Level AI's unique features, like semantic
understanding, emotion detection, and automated QA scoring. They used
a Prioritized GEO strategy to ensure LLMs found Level AI through top

 MAKING DATA SEXY: THE KNEAD THAT DOUGH STORY (4 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Most analytics tools fail because employees feel overwhelmed by data
and avoid using them. Knead That Dough addresses this by turning
messy, multi-source reporting into a single, easy-to-use dashboard
with real-time insights accessible on a phone. The strategy centers on
the “Joy of Data”, shaping a brand that feels human, practical,
and engaging. The design avoids industry clichés and uses minimal
visuals and real-world context to make data easier to understand and
more appealing to interact with. 

 AUTHORITIES NEED TO ESTABLISH CLEARER RULES ON SOCIAL MEDIA AGE
CHECKING (4 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Teen social media restrictions are failing because no universal,
effective age-checking system exists, and enforcement varies by
platform. Australia's under-16 ban shows 70% of children still access
social apps, with no drop in online harm. Proposed solutions include
app store-level verification or a universal digital ID system to
standardize checks and prevent teens from switching to less secure
platforms. Without a scalable approach for all platforms, these
restrictions will remain ineffective.
