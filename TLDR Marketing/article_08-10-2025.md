 THE ART OF DESTINATION MAKING (6 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Branding a place into a global destination requires a clear identity,
accessible infrastructure, and consistent buzz. Iceland and Banff
succeeded by defining unique icons, improving access, and creating
shareable moments that drove global attention. Iceland tripled its
annual tourism within a decade after launching its â€œInspired by
Icelandâ€ campaign and expanding airline stopover programs, while
Banff became Canada's most visited national park through strategic

 VENT NOW VS VENT AI: WHO WINS? (4 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 This piece compares two AI therapy apps, Vent AI and Vent Now. It
shows how Vent Now quickly overtook its rival through aggressive
social tactics. While Vent AI relies on paid ambassadors and
low-performing videos, Vent Now floods TikTok with thousands of
similar slideshow posts using viral hooks about detachment and mental
health, likely generated with AI tools. This formulaic, low-cost
strategy has driven massive engagement and revenue, proving that
efficiency and volume now outperform authenticity in the AI wellness
app race. 

 FREE OR PAID SUBSCRIPTION? FOR ONLINE PLATFORMS, IT'S A DANCE (5
MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Research shows that the choice between free, paid, or hybrid models
on digital platforms is less about pricing pressure and more about the
strength of network effects and the commercial value of user data
captured. When platforms like Meta or Google can collect detailed
behavioral data and depend heavily on network-driven features, the
free model is the most profitable. Subscription models become more
viable when data is less valuable or network effects are weaker, as
seen with streaming services like Netflix or Spotify. The study also
finds that hybrid models, while seemingly flexible, can intensify
competition by placing platforms in direct overlap for all users. This
structure can reduce margins and open markets to new entrants.
