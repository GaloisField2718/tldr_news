 TIKTOK WORLD 2026: IS THE MARKETING FUNNEL FINALLY DEAD? (4 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 TikTok is collapsing the traditional funnel as discovery,
consideration, and purchase now happen in the same content experience.
64% of users buy after discovering a product on the platform, often
without leaving it. TikTok Shop and creator-led formats embed commerce
directly into content through episodic storytelling and in-feed
purchase loops. Winning brands use native, culturally fluent videos
that fit the feed rather than polished ads. 

 REASONING LIFT: WHAT HAPPENS TO AI VISIBILITY WHEN AI THINKS HARDER
(5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 High reasoning mode changes AI visibility and should be treated like
a separate search environment. GPT 5.2 increased citation rates from
50% to 68% and nearly doubled citations per response, with 4.6X more
fan-out searches. Impact is strongest in mid and lower funnel
comparison queries that trigger deep research across pricing, docs,
compliance, APIs, and product details. Early funnel content also
matters since early citations persist through buyer journeys. Brands
need content that answers specific sub-queries and consistent
visibility across reasoning modes.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 THE 1 SECOND THAT DECIDES IF YOUR VIDEO GOES VIRAL (2 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 Most captions are added as an afterthought and miss what holds
attention. The first subtitle should appear at second 0 and stay under
6 words. Key phrases should be enlarged 1.5 to 2X for emphasis.
Background contrast like a semi-transparent bar or drop shadow
improves readability. Platform auto-captions should be turned off to
avoid duplicate text. Subtitles should sit within the middle 60% of
the frame to avoid TikTok, Instagram, and LinkedIn UI overlays. 

 SWATCH SHOWS THAT CO-BRANDING IS MARKETING'S MOST UNDERRATED GROWTH
LEVER (5 MINUTE READ) [8]

 WE ALL NEED SOMEBODY TO LEAN ON: REMEDIES FOR FEELING ALONE ON A TEAM
(7 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Only 20% of the global workforce feels engaged at work, while 44% of
US employees report burnout. Under-support is a structural issue that
often hits during role changes, unclear leadership, or AI-driven
workflow shifts, which forces employees to be their own coach or
feedback loop. The remedy is to build lower friction support systems.
This means setting clearer priorities, recurring peer check-ins, and
more actionable manager communication. 

 IC WORK IS THE NEW CAREER FLEX (8 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 AI is turning senior marketers and operators into high-impact
individual contributors who can ship cross-functional work without
large teams. Companies should reward business impact over headcount as
one person can now do work that once required a PM, designer,
engineer, and marketer. Faster execution changes decision-making by
reducing the need for layers of approvals and coordination. Teams
should hire people who can connect growth, product, and distribution
end to end and give them direct access to business context and core
metrics. At Lovable, 90% of time now goes to building instead of
meetings and alignment.
