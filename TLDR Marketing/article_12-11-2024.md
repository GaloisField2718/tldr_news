 THE STATE OF MARKETING OPERATIONS PROFESSIONALS IS OK, BUT IT'S GOING

earning six-figure salaries and a growing pool of experienced talent.

with 81.3% of professionals prioritizing it. Despite advances, many
companies still have low digital maturity, creating significant
opportunities for growth.

 HOW TO WRITE A HOOK: 7+ BOLD WAYS TO INSTANTLY GRAB ATTENTION (8
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Techniques for grabbing attention include promise hooks that offer
benefits, proof hooks that establish trust, and empathy hooks that
connect emotionally. Statistic hooks use surprising data, while
massive mistake hooks build trust by sharing relatable failures. Story
hooks engage with compelling narratives, and question hooks spark
curiosity to keep readers interested. Each approach aims to create
intrigue and sustain reader engagement. 

 7 BLACK FRIDAY MARKETING IDEAS (3 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Ideas for Black Friday include personalizing email campaigns based on
customer behavior, running gratitude-focused social media contests,
and collaborating with local businesses for joint promotions to
strengthen community ties. Offering thank-you giveaways to loyal
customers provides a meaningful alternative to discounts, while
sharing authentic gratitude stories on social media adds warmth. Fun
holiday experiences like scavenger hunts or trivia make shopping more
enjoyable. 

 INSIDE STATSIG'S PRICING PHILOSOPHY (3 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Statsig's transparent pricing strategy, charging only for features
that incur costs or add customer value, has fueled its growth since
its 2021 founding. The company reached $25M ARR through product
experimentation and positioning itself as a comprehensive growth
platform. Statsig offers three main plans: Developer (free), Pro, and
Enterprise. Its 3-Part Tariff structure, based on event volume,
ensures affordability and scalability. By offering core features for
free and scaling with customer needs, Statsig encourages seamless,
cost-effective upgrades.

 YOU DON'T NEED TO BUILD A PERSONAL BRAND (5 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 The priority early in a career should be on producing great work
rather than building a personal brand. A strong reputation develops
naturally from genuine contributions and results as personal branding
is a "lagging indicator" of success. Sharing valuable insights
publicly can enhance reputation but it should stem from real
experience, not attention-seeking. Delivering impactful, value-driven
work ultimately creates an authentic and lasting brand. 

 WHY GREAT MARKETING IS RISKY AS HELL (7 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Modern marketing often follows algorithms, leading brands to
replicate successful patterns instead of taking bold risks. Ahrefs
broke this mold by launching a children's book on SEO based on a
simple idea: connecting with industry parents in an unexpected way.
This gut-driven move provided a unique gift and refreshed the brand's
image.
