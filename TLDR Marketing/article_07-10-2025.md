NEWS & TRENDS

 FEWER ONLINE ADS ARE GOOD FOR EVERYONE (2 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Reducing online ads boosts reader trust, satisfaction, and brand
favorability. In low-ad environments, 73% of readers trusted
publishers versus 56% in high-ad ones, and 63% felt more favorable
toward brands. Over half showed facial confusion in high-ad settings,
signaling frustration. Fewer intrusive ads improve user experience and
long-term value, even with lower short-term revenue. 

 BAD BOT REPORT: THE RAPID RISE OF BOTS AND THE UNSEEN RISK FOR
BUSINESS (30 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 AI tools have made it easier to create malicious bots, increasing
both the volume and sophistication of automated threats. In 2024,
automated traffic surpassed human traffic at 51%, with bad bots making
up 37%. Advanced and moderate attacks accounted for 55%, while simple
high-volume attacks also rose. Key targets include financial services,
telecom, healthcare, and retail. Data scraping, payment fraud, and
account takeover attacks are the most common.

 LINKEDIN'S EGC AD STRATEGY (1 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Employee-generated content is created by staff rather than the
company itself. LinkedIn has been sponsoring employee posts, not just
company page content, to increase ROI. This post shares 3 examples of
LinkedIn staff doing this, including the Head of APAC posting
thought-leadership content, product marketers using creator-style
content to discuss the power of LinkedIn videos, and content
strategists pushing LinkedIn Ads. 

 PERSONALIZED SUBSCRIBER OUTREACH (1 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Identify your most engaged email subscribers who consistently open
and click your emails, then research who they are from their email
addresses. Send a personalized email to learn about their needs and
interests, aiming to build a closer relationship. Even without a
response, this outreach shows care and strengthens trust.

 Top voice note apps for creators include Otter.ai for transcription,

 BETTER AUDIENCE TARGETING IN GOOGLE ADS (2 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Google Ads is shifting from keyword-based to audience-focused formats
like Performance Max and Demand Gen. Its expanded Display tools now
mirror Meta and LinkedIn, letting advertisers create detailed custom
audiences from site visitors, searchers, or users with specific
interests or purchase intent. This flexibility allows testing and
combining segments to identify top performers and scale campaigns
effectively.

 MICRO-MOMENTS OF MISTRUST: THE EREWHON EDIT (2 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Even the most curated brands can lose control of the conversation
with a single overlooked word. Erewhon sparked online confusion when
an IG post initially suggested new city openings, then quietly changed
to say the brand was shipping instead. The small edit created a
micro-moment of mistrust, showing how quickly narratives can shift.
