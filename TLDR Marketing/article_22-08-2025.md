 IS LINK BUILDING DEAD? (15 DATA STUDIES BEHIND THE CLICKBAIT) (9
MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Backlinks and referring domains still correlate strongly with higher
Google rankings, with the top-ranking page having 3.8 times more
backlinks than results in positions 2 to 10. Traditional link-building
methods, such as guest posting and link insertions, have declined in
value, as only 4.6% of sites offering such services were found to be
high quality. Digital PR has become the most effective strategy,
earning authoritative links and unlinked mentions that strengthen
brand authority and visibility. AI Overviews cite top-ranking pages in
over 90% of cases, but unlinked mentions are often more impactful than
backlink volume for AI visibility. Link building is not dead, but it
has shifted toward authority-focused digital PR and brand strategies
rather than raw link quantity.
