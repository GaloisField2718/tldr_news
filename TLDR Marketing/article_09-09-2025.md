 CLICKUP'S SLIDING INTO DMS OF THEIR UNHAPPY CUSTOMERS (1 MINUTE READ)
[10] 

 ClickUp's early growth strategy relied on a scrappy, low-cost tactic
that involved tracking 1-star reviews of competitors on sites like
Capterra, G2, and TrustPilot and then reaching out to frustrated users
directly on LinkedIn. The personalized message framed ClickUp as a
solution to the problems they faced with rival products. This approach
cost nothing but time, generated the company's first 100+ customers,
and helped drive $1M in ARR. 

 PINTEREST SHARES TIPS FOR HOLIDAY CAMPAIGN PLANNING (2 MINUTE READ)
[11] 

 Holiday campaign planning on Pinterest should start now to capture
rising gift-related searches that begin in October and peak by
December. With 55% of users preferring personalized recommendations
and saves strongly tied to purchase intent, brands should create
curated festive boards by recipient or interest and prompt users to
save Pins. Pinterest recommends early activation, save-driven design,
and personalized content to influence gift planning behavior and drive
seasonal sales.
