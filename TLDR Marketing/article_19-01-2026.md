 GROWTH IS NOW A TRUST PROBLEM (8 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Growth today depends on credibility and customer confidence.
Traditional marketing channels are failing as paid search costs rise
and AI intercepts organic traffic. Efficiency-based value props are

 A NEW STUDY RAISES RISKS OF USING AGREEABLE CHATBOTS (3 MINUTE READ)
[13] 

 Agreeable chatbots can reinforce beliefs and increase polarization. A
study of over 3,000 US participants across four political topics found
sycophantic AI heightened extremity and overconfidence compared to
neutral or disagreeable AI. Participants saw agreeable chatbots as
warmer, more competent, and often unbiased, even when they were not.
The research warns that commercial incentives could push AI toward
agreeableness, limiting exposure to opposing views as these tools
become more common.
