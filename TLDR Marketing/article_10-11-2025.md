being banned. Lenient policies, algorithmic amplification, and
high-value accounts worsen the problem. Meta plans to gradually reduce
this through measures like penalty bids.

 HOW TO ANSWER 3 BIG, SCARY ANNUAL PLANNING QUESTIONS (IN DAYS, NOT
MONTHS) (10 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Draft an annual marketing plan by addressing 3 core questions about
growth strategy, budget, and revenue targets. Choose 3-5 strategic
â€œbig betsâ€ and document each with a GACCS Brief for clarity.
Estimate a budget range using GTM efficiency metrics such as CAC

 HOW TO BUILD AND RUN A BILLBOARD CAMPAIGN (16 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 For a strong OOH campaign, target locations where your customers
live, work, and commute. Use multiple formats like billboards, panels,
transit ads, street furniture, and digital boards. Plan 3-6 months
ahead and align stakeholders. Keep the messaging simple and easy to
read. Focus on peak engagement periods and map audience hotspots.
Repeat consistent messaging across all placements to create a
memorable brand presence.

 EVERGREEN VS. LAUNCH MARKETING (3 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Evergreen marketing provides steady revenue and long-term growth,
while launches generate quick cash and market momentum. Evergreen
works best for consistent offers like SaaS, retainers, or memberships.
Launches suit timed products such as bootcamps, cohorts, or drops,
offering fast visibility but higher risk. Combining both by running
evergreen systems for predictable revenue and adding 3-4 launches per
year creates urgency and drives new demand.
