 AI BRANDS ARE PUMPING AD DOLLARS INTO LINEAR TV (3 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Major AI companies like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Meta are rapidly
increasing spending on linear TV ads. Some are close to matching or
surpassing their 2025 totals in just a few months. The goal is to
shape public perception and drive AI adoption. Consumer sentiment is
mixed and trust in AI remains low. Big campaigns, including Super Bowl
spots, position AI as useful and accessible. Trust building is
becoming a central competitive focus. 

 THE BALANCE OF POWER IN INFLUENCER MARKETING IS SHIFTING (4 MINUTE
READ) [6] 

 Spending on boosting influencer posts is on track to overtake what
brands pay creators for sponsored content by 2028. The trend is driven
by shrinking organic reach on major platforms, which has made paid
promotion essential for extracting ROI from influencer campaigns. Some
agencies now operate on a performance model where brands promote
creator content and track downstream actions like customer acquisition
costs. Creators still hold leverage as the source of content brands
need to amplify, but the economics increasingly favor those who can
demonstrate consistent organic engagement.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 IMPLEMENT вЂњAGE VS. SERPвЂќ INTO YOUR CONTENT PROCESS (1 MINUTE
READ) [7] 

 Content freshness in SEO is relative, not absolute. Google compares a
page's age to competing results rather than its publish date. Older
pages can rank as fresh if rivals are older, while newer pages can be
seen as stale if competitors are more recent. Track the last updated
age of the top results for key queries and compare it to your own
pages. When you fall behind the SERP median, make substantial updates
like rewrites, new data, or intent shifts, not minor edits. 

 YOUR EMAIL A/B TEST WINNER MIGHT BE LYING (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Email A/B tests are often interpreted too narrowly, with marketers
rolling out "winners" based on a single metric without examining what
happened further down the funnel. One example showed a "winning"
subject line with higher click-through rates actually produced lower
conversion rates, lower average order values, and less revenue per
recipient than the losing version. Test results are shaped by timing,
audience composition, inbox conditions, and competing messages, making
them unreliable as a permanent strategic direction. The recommended
shift is to use testing as a learning system focused on behavioral
patterns rather than isolated wins. 

 TARGET'S DIGITAL CHIEF BREAKS DOWN THE RETAILER'S CREATOR OVERHAUL (3
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Target is overhauling its creator strategy after winding down its
2023 affiliate program, replacing it with two distinct offerings. Club
Target is a gamified platform for smaller creators with as few as 500
followers, rewarding them for completing weekly challenges on TikTok
and Instagram. It already has around 8,000 participants after its
pilot. Target Ambassadors is an invite-only program for established
influencers with tiered commissions and campaign opportunities.

 HOW TO IMPLEMENT WEBMCP: A BREAKDOWN OF GOOGLE'S LIVE DEMO (3 MINUTE
READ) [12] 

 WebMCP lets you expose structured site actions directly to AI agents,
which creates a new surface for discovery and conversion. Google's
demo shows that implementation relies on defining clear вЂњtoolsвЂќ
such as search and filtering, each with strict inputSchema and
outputSchema that control what an agent can request and return. This
structure turns site functionality into machine-readable actions,
which makes it easier for AI systems to interact with and recommend
your offerings. Registering these tools through navigator.modelContext
is the key step that makes them callable, which effectively opens your
site to agent-driven experiences. You can use this to shape how AI
agents surface products, control outputs, and guide user journeys
through predefined actions instead of relying on traditional page
crawling. 

 YOUR MANAGED WORDPRESS MIGHT BE BLOCKING AI BOTS AND YOU CAN'T SEE IT
(10 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Some managed WordPress hosts may be silently blocking AI crawlers at
the platform level, preventing sites from appearing in AI-generated
search results. An investigation into one site hosted on WP Engine
found that bots like ClaudeBot and GPTBot were receiving 429
rate-limit responses on cache-miss requests, while browser and
Googlebot requests passed through normally. The block operates below
both customer Cloudflare dashboards and WordPress security plugins,
making it invisible to standard SEO audits.

 LINKEDIN IS QUIETLY KILLING VIDEO REACH, AND THE DATA PROVES IT (5
MINUTE READ) [14] 

 LinkedIn is pushing short-form video, but performance is dropping.
Video views are down 36% year-over-year while engagement per post is
only up 7%. At the same time, overall reach has fallen sharply,
especially for company pages, as organic visibility shrinks and
personal profiles dominate the feed. The 360Brew ranking system
prioritizes dwell time and depth of engagement, which favors documents
and carousels over video. Despite platform messaging, video is
becoming less efficient while text and carousel formats consistently
outperform. 

 META WILL USE AI TO ANALYZE HEIGHT AND BONE STRUCTURE TO IDENTIFY IF
USERS ARE UNDERAGE (2 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Meta is expanding AI systems to detect underage users on Facebook and
Instagram by analyzing photos, videos, and profile activity for cues
like height, bone structure, captions, and interactions. The goal is
to estimate age, not identify individuals. Accounts flagged as under
13 may be deactivated until age verification is completed. The system
is already live in select regions and will expand globally alongside
stricter Teen Accounts with added safety controls for younger users.
