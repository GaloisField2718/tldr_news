later usage increased 8.9% and helped drive $747.5 million in
spending.

 THE MARKET ENTRY TIMING EQUATION: WHEN BEING EARLY WINS AND WHEN IT
KILLS YOU (25 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Pioneers often fail, while early leaders who enter during the growth
phase tend to win. The T-Score framework turns timing into a
measurable decision by combining market readiness, defensibility, and
CAC volatility. Companies with low scores are too early and burn out,
mid-range scores rely on perfect execution, and high scores lead to
fast-follower dominance with efficient growth. The framework helps
founders cut guesswork and validate timing with data. 

 SUBVERTING THE AUTONOMOUS IMAGE (6 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 AI has created autonomous images that operate independently from
reality and human creative processes. Drawing on Debord's concept of
the Spectacle, these visuals reinforce existing social and economic
structures while presenting themselves as unquestionable and complete.
The black-box nature of the systems, the speed and scale of
production, and the resulting alienation from creation highlight the
shift in image autonomy. Techniques now exist to subvert or critically
engage with AI, using its mechanisms to expose biases and underlying
material and social conditions.
