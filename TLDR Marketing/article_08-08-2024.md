 BENDING REALITY: TIKTOK WHAT'S NEXT 2024 SHOPPING TREND REPORT (3
MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Rising inflation and economic uncertainty in 2024 have transformed
shopping behaviors, with consumers embracing "fun-flation" and
buy-it-for-life communities. TikTok's "What's Next" trend report
explores these new shopping trends, emphasizing the significance of
emotional connections, community, and transparent brand relationships.


 REDDIT CEO HINTS THAT SUBREDDIT PAYWALLS ARE ON THE WAY (2 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 In a recent earnings call, Reddit CEO Steve Huffman suggested that
certain subreddits might be paywalled to boost revenue post-IPO. The
platform has stayed notably consistent over the years, lacking the
excessive monetization found on other social media sites, but recently
the site has aggressively pursued new revenue sources. The most
controversial change was to hike up its API fees, which many
third-party apps couldn't afford, and more recently the company signed
a licensing deal with OpenAI.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 CREATE A PROBLEM-SOLUTION EMAIL (2 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 A problem-solution email is an effective pitch for problem-aware and
solution-aware prospects in your funnel. It should start by
identifying a specific pain point that users might be experiencing,
then frame the product as the solution by showing the benefits and
additional details. This post includes 3 brief teardowns of
problem-solution emails from haircare brands. 

 LINKEDIN AD TIP: SAVE AUDIENCES (1 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 In LinkedIn Ads, save an audience and open it under Plan > Audiences
> Saved. Refine it by excluding irrelevant job titles on the left and
applying them as exclusions on the right. Re-add the updated audience
to any campaigns using it to ensure your ads target the right
audience. 

 8 EASY, CHEAP, LOW-LIFT WAYS TO DO CUSTOMER RESEARCH (1 MINUTE READ)
[8] 

 For cost-free customer research, spend time helping with support

design team's UX work and moderate webinar chats for insights.

TRAFFIC (1 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Ahrefs' new "Content Changes" feature shows you the exact changes
another website used to improve its organic traffic. The tool detects
when changes were made and charts them alongside organic traffic over
time, which helps you identify the updates that resulted in improved
performance. You can then leverage these insights to improve your own
page.

 VISUAL CONTENT STATISTICS FOR 2024 (16 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Visual content is set to captivate more in 2024, with 62% of
marketers planning to integrate interactive elements and 85%
maintaining or increasing their video budgets. The shift to mobile is
significant, as 54% of website traffic is now mobile-driven, making
mobile optimization crucial. With visual content getting 94% more
views than text-only content, marketers should focus on leveraging
images, videos, and interactive formats to boost engagement. 

 HOW TO WRITE A SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY + EXAMPLES (8 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 A social media policy is a document written by your organization to
provide guidance to employees about their actions and behaviors
online. Such a policy can help you avoid potential PR crises or
information leaks, create brand advocates, and facilitate brand
cohesion. It should include expectations based on relevant factors
such as an employee's role, the social platform, and the type of post.
This article includes examples of social media policies from Mass
General Hospital, The New York Times, and more.
