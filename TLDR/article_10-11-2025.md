 APPLE PLANS MAJOR NEW SATELLITE-POWERED FEATURES FOR IPHONES (17
MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Apple is working on new satellite features, including a satellite
framework for third-party apps, satellite-powered maps, richer
messaging capabilities, 'natural usage' improvements, and satellite
over 5G. Bringing most of these new features to market will require
major upgrades to Globalstar's satellite infrastructure. SpaceX
acquiring Globalstar would force Apple to rethink its business model
and long-term strategy for satellite services. However, it could also
bring opportunities - Apple could collaborate with SpaceX on premium
satellite features. 

 TESLA IS LAUNCHING A CRAZY NEW RENTAL PROGRAM WITH CHEAP DAILY RATES
(4 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Tesla's in-house Rental program lets people rent vehicles for between
three to seven days, starting at $60 per day. It includes free
Supercharging and free Full Self-Driving for the duration of the
rental. There are no limits on mileage or charging, but renters are
not allowed to leave the state from which they rented the vehicle.
Customers who purchase their own Tesla within seven days of using the
Rental service will receive up to a $250 credit towards their
purchase.
