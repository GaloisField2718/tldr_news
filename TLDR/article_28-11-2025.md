 NASA ROVER MAKES A SHOCKING DISCOVERY: LIGHTNING ON MARS (6 MINUTE
READ) [10] 

 Scientists have observed centimeter-scale electrical discharges on
Mars. The sparks could play an important role in chemical reactions in
the Martian atmosphere. They do not pose any danger of electrocution
for humans. However, they could disrupt or damage electronic devices
over time. Lightning has also previously been observed in the
atmospheres of Jupiter and Saturn. 

 STUDY CLAIMS TO PROVIDE FIRST DIRECT EVIDENCE OF DARK MATTER (3
MINUTE READ) [11] 

 The first evidence of dark matter may have been discovered.
Scientists have spotted a pattern of gamma rays that appears to match
the shape of the dark matter halo that spreads out in a sphere from
the heart of the galaxy. It closely matches the properties of
gamma-ray radiation predicted to be emitted by dark matter. More work
is needed to rule out other astrophysical processes and background
emissions that could explain the signals.
