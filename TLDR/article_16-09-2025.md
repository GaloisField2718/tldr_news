(4 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Retro Biosciences is gearing up for a clinical trial of a drug aimed

potentially restore cognitive function in ways that could redefine

 NEW ULTRASOUND HELMET REACHES DEEP INSIDE THE BRAIN WITHOUT SURGERY
(4 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Researchers have created an ultrasound helmet that could offer an
alternative to invasive procedures. It can modulate deep-brain
circuits without surgery with unrivaled precision. The helmet offers a
safe, reversible, and repeatable method for both understanding brain
function and developing targeted therapies. It has potential clinical
applications for neurological disorders like Parkinson's disease,
where deep-brain regions are especially affected.
