 THE DISAPPEARING MIDDLE OF SOFTWARE WORK (3 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 The center of software work is moving. Previously, the model of
software work - turning intent into something real - absorbed most of
the time, attention, and craft of software teams. AI agents can now
produce working code from goals, context, and tasks. As systems
improve, the middle will get thinner, and less time will be spent
manually translating intent into implementation. Developers will still
need to understand the problem, gather the right context from
customers and internal teams, and shape the work. Directing and
managing agent work will become the craft in this era.
