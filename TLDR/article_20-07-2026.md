BIG TECH & STARTUPS

 META IN TALKS TO LEASE COMPUTING POWER TO ANTHROPIC IN POTENTIAL $10
BILLION DEAL (5 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Anthropic proposed a computing deal with Meta in June that could be
worth as much as $10 billion over two years. Meta is considering the
deal, which would involve monthly payments and the option to opt out
of any agreement early. The talks show how demand for more computing
power is still high. The deal could open up a new line of business for
Meta and provide Meta with a new revenue stream until demand for its
own AI services catches up. 

 SPACEX IN TALKS TO PROVIDE COMPUTING POWER FOR PENTAGON'S AI PUSH (3
MINUTE READ) [6] 

 SpaceX is in talks to provide the Defense Department with computing
capacity at a cost of up to several billion dollars. Some national
security officials have raised concerns that the Pentagon is becoming
too reliant on Elon Musk's services. Pentagon officials have said they
want to reduce reliance on individual tech companies. The Pentagon is
seeking $30 billion in funding for an initiative that will focus on
securing high-end AI chips.

SCIENCE & FUTURISTIC TECHNOLOGY

 INDIA'S FIRST PRIVATELY-DEVELOPED ROCKET REACHES ORBIT ON DRAMATIC
DEBUT LAUNCH (12 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Skyroot Aerospace's Vikram-1 rocket, India's first fully commercial
satellite launcher, launched into a 280-mile-high orbit from an island
spaceport in the Bay of Bengal on Saturday. The launch was delayed for
more than half an hour to resolve a last-minute technical problem, but
the flight was deemed a grand success. The Vikram-1 is India's first
private orbital rocket to reach orbit on its very first attempt. A
second Vikram-1 launch could happen before the end of the year. 

 CHINA JOINS RUSH TO RETHINK THE SMARTPHONE FOR THE AI ERA (5 MINUTE
READ) [8] 

 Several companies are now racing to develop devices with built-in AI
agents. ZTE Corp. has unveiled a lineup of co-designed smartphones
with built-in AI services. The NaviX Ultra, which it calls the world's
first agentic smartphone, pulls up ByteDance's AI agent Doubao with a
voice command or the press of a button. It comes in black, pink,
white, and blue.

 THESE AI-NATIVE COMPANIES HAVE TINY STAFFS AND FEWER BOSSES (7 MINUTE

 The newest generation of AI-infused companies offer a vision of how
