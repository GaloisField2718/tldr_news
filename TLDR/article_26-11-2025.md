 ELON MUSK'S BORING COMPANY REVEALS PRUFROCK TBM'S MOST DISRUPTIVE
FEATURE (3 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 The Boring Company's Prufrock-5 tunnel boring machine (TBM) will
begin tunneling by December 1. One of the key features of the Prufrock

with improvements. Standard TBMs are often left underground at the end
of projects because retrieving them is usually too expensive and
impractical. TBMs from the Boring Company could eventually be as
reusable as SpaceX's boosters.

 Interoception is a sense that supplies the brain with a remarkably
rich picture of what is happening throughout the body. It shapes our
emotions, behavior, decisions, and feelings. A growing body of
research suggests that many psychiatric conditions may be caused in
part by errors in our perception of our internal environment.
Scientists still need to develop a firm understanding of how
interoception works, but someday it may be possible to treat some
conditions by returning a person's internal sense.
