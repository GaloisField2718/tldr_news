 OPENAI UNVEILS PRESENCE, A NEW PLATFORM THAT LETS ENTERPRISES LAUNCH
AND MANAGE REALTIME VOICE AGENTS AND CHATBOTS (12 MINUTE READ) [6]

 Presence is a new enterprise product from OpenAI for deploying and
managing voice AI agents and chatbots across customer-facing and
internal business workflows. It is designed for eligible enterprise
customers who want agents that operate under company-defined policies,
permissions, and evaluation standards. Presence is now available
through a limited general availability program led by OpenAI
forward-deployed engineers and select global systems integrators.
OpenAI has not disclosed pricing, geographic limits, contractual
terms, or expected costs for integration.

 SCIENCE CORPORATION'S VISION-RESTORING CHIP WINS EU APPROVAL (5
MINUTE READ) [8]

 Science Corporation has won approval from Europe's medical device
regulator to begin selling its PRIMA device, which restores vision
lost from age-related macular degeneration. The device has also
received a designation from the US FDA, the first step toward an
expedited regulatory review. It requires an hour-long outpatient
procedure that plants a small chip in the back of the eye. The product
gives functional vision through camera-equipped glasses that transmit
a view of the world to the chip.

 TRAVIS KALANICK'S ROBOTICS COMPANY RAISES $1.7B, LED BY A16Z (3
MINUTE READ) [9]

 Travis Kalanick's robotics company, Atoms, has raised $1.7 billion in
a funding round joined by Uber, reconnecting Kalanick back with the
company he founded. Atoms is a rebranded holding company created from
a ghost kitchen project Kalanick has been working on since he resigned
from Uber. Kalanick has yet to go into specifics about what he wants
to build with Atoms, but he has indicated he wants to build a
wheelbase for robots and make everyone more productive. A large
portion of the funding will likely go toward hiring.

 THE STATE OF WEBMCP (16 MINUTE READ) [11]

 WebMCP lets web pages register typed JavaScript functions that
browser AI agents can call. While there is strong industry support
behind the spec, site adoption is close to zero. No mainstream agent
consumes the tools yet, but Google says Gemini in Chrome will be the
first. The API changed locations in a recent draft, which means early
adopters already carry migration debt.

 DURABLE OBJECTS ARE MADE FOR AGENTS (11 MINUTE READ) [12]

 Cloudflare Durable Objects are a primitive that combines a serverless
V8 isolate for running code, a paired SQLite instance, and request
routing to a particular isolate. They allow developers to start
thinking about their service as a set of many event-driven objects
rather than as a bunch of services talking to a database. Durable
Objects run explicitly in a single-threaded manner and come with
native support for WebSockets. They are cheap and extremely
token-efficient.

 Technology should enable rather than subjugate. Gaining more

 THE PROBLEM WITH HYPERGROWTH AI STARTUPS (7 MINUTE READ) [14]

 Startups that have had explosive AI-driven revenue growth may be in
trouble. Most of these companies are scaling revenue by reselling
inference at very low (and sometimes negative) margins. The value of
the products or services they're providing isn't adding anything on
top of the intelligence they are reselling. While VCs seem excited
about this growth, the business model is unsustainable.
