 WHY LOCAL TECH SCENES HAVE CHANGED (19 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 AI has changed the relationship between local tech scenes and San
Francisco. The opportunity cost of being anywhere other than San
Francisco is now at an all-time high. However, it is also much easier
for players who want to stay local to start a one-person business and
grow it themselves. AI will enable local tech scenes to produce
promising startups and select for promising talent in very different
ways than they used to. 

 THE JUNIOR HIRING CRISIS (12 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 AI is accelerating the erosion of the traditional apprenticeship
model in tech. This puts the 10- to 20-year talent pipeline at risk.
Students and early-career professionals need to start building
relational intelligence now. Relationship skills have always been a
differentiator, but now they're a necessity.
