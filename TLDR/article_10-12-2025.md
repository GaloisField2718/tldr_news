 THE BUBBLE IS LABOR (4 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Companies only hire people because they can't do all of the work
themselves. If every founder could handle all of the tasks themselves,
there wouldn't be anywhere near the number of jobs we have today. The
only reason the current labor market exists is because there's a group
of founders/owners who need help producing their goods and services.
These people aren't required to hire anyone, and the moment they can
do the work themselves, they will.
