BIG TECH & STARTUPS

 NVIDIA DETAILS ITS NEXT-GENERATION VERA CPU FOR AI, SETTING UP
CHALLENGE TO AMD AND INTEL (6 MINUTE READ) [8]

 Vera is the first server CPU that Nvidia has designed from the core.
It was specifically designed to address bottlenecks in AI agents.
Nvidia claims the chip has 50% better performance for AI agents than
x86 chips. Vera chips were delivered to clients, including OpenAI,
Anthropic, and SpaceX, for evaluation in June.

 JACK DORSEY IS TAKING ON SLACK WITH BUZZ, A GROUP CHAT PLATFORM FOR
TEAMS AND THEIR AI AGENTS (3 MINUTE READ) [9]

 Buzz is a group chat platform for the workplace that puts humans and
AI agents in the same conversations. It is model-agnostic,
decentralized, self-sovereign, and open source. The app looks a lot
like Slack, but with native AI agents and the ability to manage GitHub
projects from one interface. Developers can customize Buzz instances
to the needs and workflows of their teams. The app is available for
macOS, Windows, and Linux, and the code for the app has been uploaded
to GitHub.

 OPENAI MODELS ESCAPED AND HACKED A COMPANY IN CYBERSECURITY TEST GONE
WRONG (4 MINUTE READ) [10]

 OpenAI claims that two AI systems that it was testing broke out of
their test environment and hacked into Hugging Face. Hugging Face
discovered the breach early last week, saying there had been
unauthorized access to internal data sets and company credentials. Its
employees suspected that the attack was carried out by a
top-of-the-line frontier AI model due to the sophistication of the
attack. OpenAI's AI apparently targeted Hugging Face as it was the
quickest route to answering a benchmarking question.

 INSIDE ROBLOX'S BET ON WORLD MODELS (25 MINUTE READ) [11]

 Roblox is exploring the use of video models to create photorealistic
experiences without compromising scale or multiplayer performance. An
early version of Roblox Reality is expected later this year or early
next. This post looks at how the company is bringing this vision to
life. Roblox's approach doesn't require a video model to solve
everything on its own. It gives the model an engine that remembers the
world, enforces its rules, and keeps it true for everyone playing.

 SOFTWARE FACTORIES, LIGHT AND DARK (21 MINUTE READ) [13]

 A software factory is harnessed loops at scale. Running the loop with
humans (light factory) increases judgment and concentration while
lowering speed and breakage. Using only AI agents (dark factory)
allows agents to handle the whole process, but risks a lack of
understanding of the produced software. The hardest job now is knowing
which checks to build and how much autonomy to delegate.

 MODELS ARE WORSE AT REVIEWING THEIR OWN CODE (13 MINUTE READ) [14]

 Claude Code and Codex are both better at finding bugs in each other's
code than their own. Models produce the same types of bugs that they
are most likely to miss in review. Developers can take advantage of
this finding by routing reviews to different models.
