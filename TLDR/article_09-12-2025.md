information. The glasses will be built on top of Android XR in
collaboration with Samsung, Gentle Monster, and Warby Parker.

 CURRENT FUEL MIX AND USES (12 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 We're in the middle of a period of rapid transition in energy. Solar
is decosting at an accelerating rate, coal is shrinking, and nuclear
is flat. Primary production will soon be mostly solar. Batteries will
be everywhere, and the grid will shrink. Datacenters, which are
already building their own captive power plants, will become power
sources for their communities. Substantial cost improvements are still
needed to make space AI competitive.

 LLM WEIGHTS VS THE PAPERCUTS OF CORPORATE (5 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Model weight first companies are companies where the majority of the
software was code-generated. These companies do not require as much
context engineering and do not need to adhere to classical principles
as much as corporate companies. They achieve much higher
velocity/productivity/success rates using AI because they are not
forcing models to follow pre-existing dogma. Companies having problems
with AI are likely trying to bend the technology to their whim rather
than working with it.
