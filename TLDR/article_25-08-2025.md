 THE NEXT STARSHIP FLIGHT WILL TEST MUCH MORE THAN HARDWARE (4 MINUTE
READ) [7]

 META'S NEW 2MM LASER DISPLAY COULD ENABLE TRUE AR GLASSES (4 MINUTE
READ) [8] 

 Meta researchers have found a new use for photonic integrated
circuits (PICs), chips that can receive, modulate, and direct light.
Their PICs can receive RGB light from laser light sources integrated
into a panel, process it, then shine light onto a liquid crystal on a
silicon panel for final display. The overall complex is just two
millimeters thick and has a dramatically better color gamut over
similar displays. These PICs are manufactured using a CMOS-compatible
process, which means they should be affordable to create at scale.
