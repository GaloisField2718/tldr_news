 COMPREHENSION DEBT вҖ” THE HIDDEN COST OF AI GENERATED CODE (8 MINUTE
READ) [13] 

 Comprehension debt is a hidden cost to human intelligence and memory
that results from excessive reliance on AI and automation. It is the
growing gap between how much code exists in a system and how much of
it any human being genuinely understands. It can lead to teams not
being able to explain why design decisions had been made or how
different parts of the system are supposed to work together. The debt
has to be paid sooner or later, and interest accrues rapidly. 

 THE "NIGHT SHIFT" AGENTIC WORKFLOW (10 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 This post outlines an agentic workflow that allows developers and
agents to work together around the clock. The developer takes the 'day
shift', while AI agents work overnight. The developer prepares as much
as they can during the day shift for the agents to work autonomously
as they rest, finishing tasks by the next morning. The workflow has
helped the author spend less time babysitting and much more time
thinking about the problems they need to solve.
