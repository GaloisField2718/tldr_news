BIG TECH & STARTUPS

 OPENAI TOUTS AMAZON ALLIANCE IN MEMO, SAYS MICROSOFT HAS ‘LIMITED
OUR ABILITY' TO REACH CLIENTS (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 OpenAI's revenue chief, Denise Dresser, recently sent a memo to staff
saying that the company's alliance with Amazon was a key growth driver
for its enterprise business. The memo said that OpenAI's partnership
with Microsoft was foundational to its success, but it limited the
startup's ability to meet enterprises' needs. OpenAI's enterprise
business makes up 40% of its revenue and is expected to meet parity
with its consumer business by the end of the year. The company has
been trying to convince investors of its strengthening position as it
is gearing up for an initial public offering as soon as this year. 

 META EXPECTED TO UNSEAT GOOGLE AS WORLD'S LARGEST DIGITAL-AD PLAYER
(3 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Meta is projected to surpass Google in net ad revenue this year. The
company is seeing a lift in ad revenue thanks to new offerings and the
broader boost that AI has provided. It invested significantly in
establishing substantial user habits for its products before
eventually introducing advertisements. Google's global growth is
projected to remain flat this year.

SCIENCE & FUTURISTIC TECHNOLOGY

 AMAZON LEO SHOWS OFF IN-FLIGHT WI-FI ANTENNA THAT WILL TAKE ON
STARLINK (3 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Amazon's Leo Aviation Antenna is a satellite dish designed
specifically for in-flight Wi-Fi in planes. The flat, low-profile
antenna is designed to be installed on the exterior of the plane to
receive radio signals from orbiting low-Earth satellites. Amazon
promises a single-day installation for its antenna - SpaceX's
terminals require a downtime of 10 to 14 days. Amazon's airline
satellite service promises to deliver up to 1 Gbps in downloads and
400Mbps in uploads simultaneously. 

 VIDEO: CHINA'S HUMANOID ROBOT REACHES 10 M/S SPRINT, EDGES CLOSER TO
USAIN BOLT'S RECORD (4 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Unitree Robotics' H1 humanoid robot can sprint up to 10 meters per
second. The robot weighs around 136 pounds and features a combined
height and calf length of 80 centimeters. A video of the robot's
record-breaking run is available in the article. Unitree recently
revealed plans to launch its sport-ready R1 humanoid globally via
AliExpress for $4,000.

 GITHUB STACKED PRS (3 MINUTE READ) [14]

 META SPINS UP AI VERSION OF MARK ZUCKERBERG TO ENGAGE WITH EMPLOYEES
(3 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Meta is building an AI version of Mark Zuckerberg that can engage
with employees in his stead. Zuckerberg is personally involved in
training and testing the animated AI. The character was being trained
on the billionaire's mannerisms, tone, and publicly available
statements. It is designed to make employees feel more connected to
him through interactions with it. 

 BEYOND THE SKY (62 MINUTE READ) [17] 

 Hyperliquid, a blockchain and cryptocurrency trading exchange, was
created by Jeffrey Yan, who believes that all of finance needs to be
rebuilt from scratch. Before Hyperliquid, Yan ran one of the largest
anonymous trading operations in crypto, Chameleon Trading. This
resulted in impressive returns, but for Yan, it's not about the money.
This article discusses Yan's past, how he built Hyperliquid into the
most profitable startup per employee on earth, and Yan's plans to
shape the future of finance.
