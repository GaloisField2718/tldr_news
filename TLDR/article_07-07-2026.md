 NVIDIA'S NEXT-GEN AI RACK SYSTEM DELAYED TO 2028 ON MANUFACTURING
SNAGS (4 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Nvidia's Kyber rack-scale architecture has been delayed by more than
12 months to 2028. The server cabinet is designed to house 144 of the
company's 2027 Rubin Ultra chips so they can work together as one
giant computer. The delay is due to difficulties in manufacturing a
key circuit board at the heart of the system. It is a sign that
Nvidia's breakneck annual release cadence is colliding with
manufacturing limits.

 The iPhone Ultra will be announced in September together with the

The foldable phone is estimated to be priced at around $2,400. It is
likely that the phone will sell out immediately after pre-orders open,
and then there will be delays running through December.

 THE ROBOTS ARE HERE (32 MINUTE READ) [6]

 EGYPT IS BUILDING A NEW NILE (20 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Egypt is attempting to create a new agricultural landscape to relieve
mounting pressure on the Nile Delta. The New Delta product is a plan
that involves recycling water, moving it across the desert, and
bringing it to new land for cultivation. Early satellite images show
rapid progress, but questions remain about how sustainable the
transformation will be. Much of the water currently used to irrigate
the new fields is coming from underground aquifers, which are not
easily replenished.

 BIG TECH HAS SUDDENLY FLIPPED ON THE AI JOBS WIPEOUT SCENARIO (9
MINUTE READ) [11]
