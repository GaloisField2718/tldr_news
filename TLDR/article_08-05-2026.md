(6 MINUTE READ) [5]

cameras. The prototypes feature a near-final design and capabilities.

AI elements, which could further hold back a launch if the quality of

energy supply. This means that the country will have to rely on much

speech needs to be converted into structured text during the

 CLOUDFLARE TO SLASH 1,100 JOBS DUE TO AI-DRIVEN RESTRUCTURING PLAN (3
MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Cloudflare plans to slash 1,100 jobs as part of a restructuring plan
that it claims will define how a world-class, high-growth company
operates and creates value in an agentic AI era. The company says it
will become even faster and more innovative by embracing an agentic
AI-first operating model. The layoffs are expected to be substantially
complete by the end of the third quarter. Cloudflare expects to incur
charges between $140 million and $150 million for the layoffs. 

 ELON MUSK TRIED TO HIRE OPENAI FOUNDERS TO START AI UNIT INSIDE TESLA
(4 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 OpenAI claims that Elon Musk was always happy to commercialize the
lab as long as he remained in charge. Musk had tried to hire OpenAI's
founding team to lead a new AI lab within Tesla in 2018. The deal
would have brought Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, and Ilya Sutskever to
the company, with Altman becoming a board member or OpenAI becoming a
Tesla subsidiary. The claim contradicts Musk's accusations of Altman
stealing the charity by converting the company into a for-profit.
