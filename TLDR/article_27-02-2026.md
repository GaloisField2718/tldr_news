 OPEN SOURCE IN THE AGE OF AI (3 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 The dynamics of the software industry are rapidly changing. Some of
the foundations that open source was built on are looking increasingly
shaky. Open source maintainers have been fighting companies taking
their work and creating competing products for years by using
increasingly restrictive licenses or keeping certain parts of the
development stack private. However, AI is making it easier to clone
codebases into entirely new products. Software licenses may become
meaningless as AI becomes adept enough to rewrite open source
codebases from scratch without using any of the original code.
