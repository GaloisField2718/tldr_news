 AMAZON ROLLS OUT 1-HOUR, 3-HOUR DELIVERY AS ULTRAFAST SHIPPING TREND
GROWS IN THE US (3 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Amazon is rolling out one-hour and three-hour deliveries in parts of
the US. Three-hour delivery will be available in about 2,000 cities
and towns, while one-hour delivery will be available in hundreds of
areas. More than 90,000 products are eligible for the service.
Shoppers will be able to filter search for products that can be
delivered in three hours or less. 

 OPENAI LAUNCHES GPT-5.4 MINI AND GPT-5.4 NANO ON APIS (2 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 OpenAI has released GPT-5.4 mini and GPT-5.4 nano. GPT-5.4 mini is
live on the API, Codex, and ChatGPT, while the nano model is reserved
for API users only. Both models are designed for workflows where
response time shapes the product. GPT-5.4 mini is priced at $0.75 per
million input tokens and $4.50 per million output tokens, while
GPT-5.4 nano lands at $0.20 and $1.25.

 NVIDIA PUSHES FURTHER INTO THE AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE SPACE TO COMPETE
WITH TESLA AND WAYMO (2 MINUTE READ) [8]

 WATCH DONUT LAB'S MYSTERIOUS SOLID-STATE BATTERY CHARGE IN AN ACTUAL
EV (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Donut Lab will supply its production solid-state battery pack to
Estonian company Verge Motorcycles this spring. The companies recently
showcased a Verge TS Pro motorcycle charging at a public DC
fast-charging station. The motorcycle was equipped with an 18 kWh
(nominal) solid-state battery pack with a manufacturer-estimated range
of 217 miles. The battery charged to 80% in 12 minutes.
