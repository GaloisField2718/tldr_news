 THE CRUMBLING WORKFLOW MOAT: AGGREGATION THEORY'S FINAL CHAPTER (8
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 LLMs absorb the interface layer of vertical software, collapsing
workflow-based moats and turning products into interchangeable APIs.
When users interact only through chat, suppliers lose brand, UX, and
switching costs, forcing competition on data quality, coverage, and
price. This completes Aggregation Theory by eliminating interface
defensibility, triggering valuation compression for software companies
without truly proprietary data. 

 FRIENDLY ADVICE FOR MY STARTUP FOUNDER FRIENDS (2 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Solve for customers and your market, not for investors and media.
Help customers understand what you do for them. This will help
investors understand you better as well. Frame what you do from the
perspective of customers - investors usually don't know what the
current hot buzzwords mean either.
