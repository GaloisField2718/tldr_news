HEADLINES & TRENDS

 OPEN SOURCE, APIS, AND THE RISE OF AGENT-LED GROWTH (11 MINUTE READ)
[4] 

 AI agents are changing how software gets discovered, recommended, and
implemented. They are increasingly influencing implementation and
recommendations, giving open-source and API companies a stronger
distribution advantage. Agents need software they can understand,
test, and use. Open source provides context, and APIs, SDKs, and
integrations give them a way to act. 

 THE BIFURCATION OF CAPITAL IS INEVITABLE (15 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 As venture firms grow into massive asset managers, they become
structurally incapable of pursuing smaller opportunities, creating a
split between capital agglomerators chasing trillion-dollar outcomes
and smaller investors serving the increasingly neglected long tail.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 WHAT PEOPLE MISUNDERSTAND ABOUT BUILDING IN PUBLIC (9 MINUTE READ)
[6] 

 People want to see the human behind the product. They want to see
their faces, how they think, and the choices they agonized over.
People are hesitant to build in public but this is usually due to
misconceptions about what that actually means. This post provides four
examples of what 'good' building in public looks like. It also dispels
some of the misconceptions that may be stopping founders from building
in public. 

 WE BOOKED 614 MEETINGS WITH ONE INBOUND AGENT. YOUR “CONTACT US”
FORM IS COSTING YOU DEALS (11 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Replacing a traditional contact form with an AI inbound agent helped
book 614 qualified meetings across 2.25 million website sessions and
402,000 interactions without adding sales headcount. Beyond answering
questions, the agent routes leads based on close-rate data, runs
automated re-engagement campaigns, and manages discounting within
predefined guardrails to capture revenue that would otherwise be lost.

 NOTION DEV TOOLS (7 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Notion Database allows developers to continuously upsert external
records. Notion Agents can use custom tools to generate assets, query
live data, and hit any API. Notion workflows can be triggered from
anywhere using webhooks from any app. Notion Workers are hosted on
isolated sandboxes managed by Notion. Developers can use external
agents or custom agents within Notion. 

 OPENTAG (GITHUB REPO) [10] 

 OpenTag enables users to run AI agents inside Slack. It is like
having Claude in the workspace, except open-source and self-hosted.
OpenTag is built on CopilotKit's open SDK for chat-platform agents. A
video of the tool in action is available in the repository.

MISCELLANEOUS

 WHAT IF THERE IS NO MOAT YET? (2 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Not all companies have a moat straight away. Some moats need to be
earned. It can take years to build scale, brand, networks, and
infrastructure. The moat is still real, even if it shows up later. 

 THE AI INDUSTRY AS YOU KNOW IT DIED TODAY (7 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 The US government now effectively decides who gets access to the
newest frontier model. OpenAI's GPT-5.6 is rolling out consumer by
consumer under government approval, right after Anthropic was forced
to pull a model entirely. This quietly turns access and governance
into the scarce thing, just as Chinese labs ship their best models
openly to anyone. If it holds, where you can even get capability
starts to matter as much as how good it is.
