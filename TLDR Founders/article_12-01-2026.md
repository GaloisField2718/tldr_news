 HOW PERSONIO'S CRO BUILT AN AI-POWERED GO-TO-MARKET IN JUST 6 MONTHS
(12 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Personio is a $3B+ HR platform with 400 salespeople. In May 2024, it
ran an "AI Surge Week" and felt good about adoption metrics. Then its
CRO noticed that high usage isn't transformation. Six months later, it
had 400+ AI assistants, cut SDR research time from 2 hours to 15
minutes, and booked 140 meetings in a week through an AI agent. 

 SALES PROBLEMS VS. PRODUCT-MARKET FIT PROBLEMS (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 If you misdiagnose a product-market fit problem as a sales problem,
you'll hire a sales coach, and it won't help. The distinction comes
down to force and fit. Force is demand that exists independently of
your product. Is this customer actively trying to accomplish
something, and are their existing options not good enough? Fit only
matters after you've established force. The framework for diagnosing
which problem you actually have is surprisingly simple once you see
it.
