 WHAT'S WORKING IN SAAS PRICING RIGHT NOW (15 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Credit-based pricing exploded 126% in 2025 because it splits the
difference between predictable licenses and pure usage. Customers can
budget, while vendors protect margins on volatile AI costs. Meanwhile,
per-seat pricing isn't dying like everyone predicted. It's evolving.
OpenAI and Figma now tie seats to capability tiers and product access.
Salesforce lets customers swap unused licenses for AI credits. Nobody
knows what AI is actually worth yet, so the winners are offering
multiple ways to pay.

 Founders never ask how to build a local press strategy, and that's a
