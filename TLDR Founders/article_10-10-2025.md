 REFLECTION RAISES $2B TO BE AMERICA'S OPEN FRONTIER AI LAB,
CHALLENGING DEEPSEEK (5 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Reflection raised $2 billion at an $8 billion valuation to challenge
established AI labs by developing open-source frontier models. Founded
by ex-Google DeepMind researchers, the startup aims to release a
high-powered language model next year. The company will share model
weights publicly while capitalizing on enterprise and government
clients for revenue. 

 FIGMA PARTNERS WITH GOOGLE TO ADD GEMINI AI TO ITS DESIGN PLATFORM (3
MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Figma has partnered with Google to integrate Gemini AI models into
its design platform, enhancing image editing and generation. Gemini
2.5 Flash promises a 50% reduction in latency for Figma's "Make Image"
feature, improving workflows for 13 million users. Google also
introduced Gemini Enterprise to enhance AI capabilities for enterprise
clients, expanding its suite of integrations and partnerships.

investment, Altman remains confident that these strategic moves will

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 WE REBUILT OUR ONBOARDING AROUND MCP (5 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Most developer onboarding flows lose users before they ever see real
value. DevCycle found the problem wasn't motivation, it was location.
Developers don't want to leave their editors to try a demo app. So the
team rebuilt onboarding around the Model-Context-Protocol (MCP),
letting AI coding assistants like Cursor or VS Code install and
configure the SDK directly in-editor. Two prompts now replace an
entire tutorial, tripling install rates and shrinking time-to-value
from hours to minutes. 

 HOW TO BUILD (AND EXPLAIN) COHORT CHARTS (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Most founders can quote retention rates, but few can show them. This
guide breaks down the three cohort charts every operator should know -
range retention tables, spider charts, and stacked area charts - and
explains when to use each. You'll learn how to build them, interpret
the patterns, and turn a pile of numbers into a story about what's
really happening in your customer base. 

 GEN AI PRESENT AND FUTURE: A CONVERSATION WITH NAVEEN ZUTSHI, CIO AT
DATABRICKS (9 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 GenAI is transforming enterprise software by enhancing development
and intelligence systems, though challenges remain in areas like sales
and reliability. Databricks' CIO highlights improvements in AI-driven
tasks, such as automating manual processes and improving data handling
without needing additional staff. Startups benefit from GenAI by
focusing on automation, allowing them to scale rapidly and
efficiently.
