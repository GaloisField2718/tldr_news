 AGENT SKILLS ARE THE NEW SDK (AND YOU SHOULD BE BUILDING ONE) (16
MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Developer infrastructure companies need to adjust their product for
AI coding agents as a competitive necessity. Agent skills make it
possible to have a 10x solutions engineer for every single customer
account. The value of many infrastructure products scales with
coverage, and that was previously bottlenecked by developer memory and
discipline. Skills dislodge the bottleneck of developer bandwidth. 

 THE GROWTH METRIC SILICON VALLEY LOVES MOST IS ALSO ITS LEAST TRUSTED
(5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Cluely's CEO admitted to inflating their ARR figures, highlighting a
common issue among AI startups. ARR, a key growth metric, lacks
standardization, allowing for manipulation and misuse. This challenge
raises trust concerns among investors, though experts caution against
imposing heavy auditing on startups, advocating for transparency
instead. 

 2026 STATE OF AI AGENTS (15 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 The numbers in Databricks' annual State of AI Agents report are
striking. Drawing on telemetry from over 20,000 organizations
(including 60%+ of the Fortune 500), the report shows multi-agent
systems grew 327% in under four months, and more than 80% of databases
are now built by AI agents. Companies with AI governance frameworks
pushed 12x more projects to production. Of the top 15 enterprise use
cases, 40% center on customer experience and engagement.

 FROM SIGNUP TO VALUE: HOW AI IS CHANGING ACTIVATION IN SAAS (12
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 AI has changed what's possible in the first session. Products that
haven't adopted the technology are already behind. The companies that
compound growth over the next few years will be the ones that turn
early value into lasting workflow habits. Strong first outputs,
self-building setup, and onboarding that adapts to what users actually
say they need are real improvements. 

 VIBE в†’ ENVIRONMENT в†’ CULTURE: LEADERSHIP GETS IT BACKWARDS (3
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Leaders obsess over culture when they should start with their vibe.
That vibe shapes the environment (processes, approvals, and work
patterns), and culture naturally emerges from that environment.
Misaligned environments frustrate A-players, slow output, and create
politics. The solution is explicit leadership, environment design, and
clarity, not trying to force culture directly.

 WHICH CRM SHOULD YOU USE IN 2026/2027? FOLLOW THE AGENTS (9 MINUTE
READ) [14] 

 AI agents are often more important than the CRM itself. Businesses
should pick the platform where their agents can do the most work.
Every agent deployed deepens a company's commitment to the platform
they run on. This means the CRM decision is now also an AI
infrastructure decision. 

 SOFTWARE FINALLY GETS TO WORK: THE OPPORTUNITY IN VERTICAL AI (16
MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Vertical software has never escaped a persistent ceiling. It competes
for IT budgets rather than the far larger labor spend underneath it,
and often requires new headcount to manage. Vertical AI moves the ROI
calculation from software budgets to people budgets. This will open up
a category of business that the SaaS model structurally can't support.
