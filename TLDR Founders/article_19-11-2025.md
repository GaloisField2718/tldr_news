 BUILDING A 60% AI TEAM: EFFICIENT, PROFITABLE, AND SURPRISINGLY
LONELY (5 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 AI may be a necessity in 2025 to be competitive. The unit economics
alone make it almost impossible to justify hiring humans for many
functions. Tutoring an AI is much easier in many cases. However, an AI
team is a quiet one - it can be lonely when group meetings are gone

 I ANALYZED 1,000 FORWARD DEPLOYED ENGINEER JOBS: WHAT I LEARNEDâ€¦
(18 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) are software engineers who embed
directly with customers to build, deploy, and maintain complex
technical systems in production. They are like a hybrid of a senior
software engineer and a technical consultant. The role is exploding in
growth because AI deployment is hard - companies need engineers who
can ensure complex AI systems actually work in the real world. If you
want to become an FDE, learn Python, get comfortable with AI/ML, build
customer skills, be ready to travel, and target growth-stage startups.


 PLATFORM PRODUCTS & METRICS MYTHS (5 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Platform PM myths include believing all investments must have
immediate ROI and that metrics must be instantly measurable. Metrics
often need time to show value, and sharing ownership across teams can
drive better outcomes. Platform innovations, though less visible, play
crucial roles in long-term success by enhancing scalability,
efficiency, and competitive differentiation.
