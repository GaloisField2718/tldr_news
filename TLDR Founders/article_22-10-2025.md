its beta iOS app offering features like search and text interaction.

 ONLY 100 METRICS MATTER (2 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Companies track thousands of numbers, but almost none of them change
decisions. In reality, about 100 metrics explain 90% of what's
happening. Once you break the business into equations - growth,
engagement, revenue - you see which levers actually matter. Everything
else lives in the long tail: useful for edge cases, not for running
the company.

 HOW STRUGGLE BECOMES STRATEGY (3 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Pejman Nozad reflects on his 32-year journey from living above a
yogurt shop to becoming a top Silicon Valley investor, framing early
