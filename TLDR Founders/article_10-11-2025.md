 THE AI TALENT WAR: AI ACQUISITIONS (4 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 AI companies are aggressively acquiring startups to secure top talent
and technology. Over $10 billion was spent on 365 M&A deals in early
2025. Major players like Google and Microsoft use acquihires to gain
specialized expertise and proprietary technology rapidly. This
strategy, driven by intense talent competition and regulatory

competitive edge.

STRATEGIES & TACTICS

 HOW TO HELP YOUR HEAD OF MARKETING WIN EARLY (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 When a new Head of Marketing joins, everyone rushes in with ideas -
new deck, new brand, new campaign - and nothing tied to pipeline gets
finished. Slow the chaos early. Align on two or three measurable goals
for the first 90 days, make them public, and be explicit about what
marketing isn't touching yet. Then help them deliver something visible
that earns trust - refresh the homepage, fix demo follow-ups, or
launch one targeted campaign that actually brings in leads. 

 WHAT MAKES A ROUND EASY (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Every round falls into one of two buckets. Either you're an attention
spike - the kind of team, traction, or market that investors chase on
instinct - or you're an explainer, where belief builds through
conversation and time. This post breaks down how to tell which you are
and why it matters. 

 YOUR ROADMAP IS A PROTOTYPE FOR YOUR STRATEGY (12 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Product roadmaps should be treated as prototypes, focusing on
adaptability and learning, rather than rigid delivery contracts. A
flexible roadmap, like the Now-Next-Later model, encourages teams to
test assumptions, iterate based on feedback, and keep strategies
aligned with changing environments. By emphasizing the process over
the end product, roadmapping builds resilience and ensures strategies
remain relevant and effective.

MISCELLANEOUS

 FOUNDER IDEOLOGY (5 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Every founder builds from a different place. Some are missionaries
who believe deeply in a mission, others are mercenaries who love the
chase. There are performers too - the rebels who bend the rules and
the ones who fake it until it breaks. People move between these types
as they grow, fail, or win. What matters most isn't which one you
start as, but who you're becoming while you build. 

 ANGEL INVESTORS, A FIELD GUIDE (10 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Cap space is limited, and signalling matters a lot in VC funding.
Your network can go a long way in helping you signal. Choose your
angels well so they can help you a lot. This guide walks readers
through how to bring on angel investors. It will require a lot of
luck, but it's possible to learn how to create your own luck.
