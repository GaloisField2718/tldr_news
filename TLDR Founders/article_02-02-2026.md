 LOYALTY IS DEAD IN TECH (4 MINUTE READ) [17] 

 Big Tech has figured out how to acqui-hire without the acquisition.
License the IP, hire who you want, leave the rest behind. No
regulatory review, no shared outcomes. Microsoft did it with
Inflection, Amazon with Adept, and Google with Character AI. The
unwritten oath between founders, employees, and investors is dead.
When picking your next company, ask your VC who else they've backed in
your category and what happened to employees at the founder's last
company. 

 THE ORIGINS OF GENERATIONAL COMPANIES (8 MINUTE READ) [18]

selling, learn by iterating on what doesn't work, avoid trying to
predict the future or convince markets, and stay in the game long
enough to catch a demand wave when it hits.
