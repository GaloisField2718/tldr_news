 THE MOST HUMAN TECHNOLOGY EVER MADE (10 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 An electrician built a $12.99 load calculator that can replace a $500
service call. A plumber says one afternoon with OpenClaw got him
further than a $40,000 consulting project. The interesting shift is
the minimum market size. A tool this narrow never justified a
developer's salary, but when the domain expert builds it himself, a
few thousand electricians is a viable market. Pricing is the easy
part: the $500 service call already exists, so $12.99 reads as a
discount on money the customer was spending anyway. 

 YOU JUST HIRED A MILLION BAD EMPLOYEES (10 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 A badly managed AI agent can cost more than the employee it was meant
to replace. The money vanishes into loops: agents spending tokens
redoing work nobody clearly defined. This article warns founders
against giving AI agents vague jobs and assuming cheap tokens mean
cheap labor. A badly managed agent can cost more than the employee it
was meant to replace. It keeps retrying, replanning, and correcting
work nobody properly defined, and every loop costs money.

 THE DISCIPLINE PREMIUM (6 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Founders benefit from setting initial valuations below market rates,
enabling easier future fundraising and sustainable company growth.
This strategy avoids the pitfalls of inflated valuations and offers
opportunities to sell stock later at a higher price. The approach
aligns with maintaining disciplined capital management, preventing
overcapitalization, and ensuring long-term success. 

 THE STUFF NOBODY TELLS YOU ABOUT STARTUP MARKETING (8 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 Everyone seems to be reaching $1 million ARR in record time.
PostHog's account of its first 1,000 users is refreshing because it is
mostly repetition. Hacker News worked, so the founders kept posting
there. People liked candid stories, so they wrote about the launch,
the fundraise, and six ideas that failed. No secret growth loop.

 ZAPIER MCP (WEBSITE) [8] 

 Zapier MCP connects Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and other AI tools to
Gmail, Slack, Salesforce, and over 9,000 other apps. It can be set up
in minutes with no terminal, config files, or coding required. Zapier
MCP is included in existing Zapier plans. Guides on how to use the MCP
are available. 

 DOMAIN SDK (WEBSITE) [9] 

 The Domain SDK makes it easy to add, verify, monitor, and remove
customer domains with one TypeScript API. Users just add a hostname to
the platform they already run, and it returns the exact DNS records
customers need, tracks them until they are ready, and removes them
safely. The SDK features adapters for Vercel, Cloudflare for SaaS,
Railway, Render, and Netlify. The tool is server-side only and
requires Note 20+ or Bun.
