 HOW CLAUDE IS BUILDING A UNIQUE GROWTH ENGINE OUT OF USER-CREATED
APPS (8 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Claude launched AI-powered apps with a clever twist - users pay with
their own Claude subscription, not the creator's. A teacher can build
a SAT prep tool and share it with students, but each student must sign
up for Claude to use it. No API keys, no hosting costs, no
authentication headaches. The constraint becomes the opportunity. Each
app spawns its own growth loop as project tools drive team invites and
calculators spread through forums. While Cursor and Bolt chase
developers, Claude went the opposite direction - maximum constraints
for minimum friction, turning every creator into a distribution
channel. 

 THIS EMAIL STRATEGY CHANGED EVERYTHING (5 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Citizen, a real time safety alerts app, was losing users after it
muted push notifications. Julia Knight tackled the issue by
hand-collecting local emergencies, dropping headlines into a Figma
template, and blasting fifteen tailored emails daily. The clicks
proved so strong that the team automated triggers that now send forty
million incident emails each month, bringing dormant accounts back for
free, cutting paid acquisition, and even lowering iterable costs
through renegotiation.
