 SEC TO HALT REQUIRING CRYPTO FIRMS TO REGISTER AS TRADING SYSTEMS (2
MINUTE READ) [4]

systems, signaling a shift toward a more lenient regulatory stance
under Acting Chair Mark Uyeda. This move follows the agency's recent
trend of pausing lawsuits against major crypto firms and aligns with
the pro-crypto policies of the new administration.

 CONFESSIONS OF A CRYPTO MARKETER (4 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Traditional marketing strategies like SEO and paid ads largely fail
in crypto due to unique challenges like rapid market shifts, short
user attention spans, and strict advertising regulations. Effective
crypto marketing instead revolves around real-time community
engagement, continuous education, transparency, and an integrated
approach encompassing community management, product development, and
customer support. Crypto marketing is less about conventional
techniques and more about immersive community building, continuous
education, authenticity, and responsiveness to user needs.
