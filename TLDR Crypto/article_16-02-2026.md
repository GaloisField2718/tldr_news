 TOMASZ STANCZAK STEPS DOWN FROM ETHEREUM FOUNDATION (3 MINUTE READ)

 PREDICTION MARKETS FOR HEDGING (6 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Vitalik Buterin argues prediction markets are converging toward
unsustainable "corposlop" (short-term crypto price bets, sports
betting) and should prioritize hedging as the core use case.
Prediction markets on price indices for all major goods and services
could be used as a type of hedged currency, with local LLMs generating
personalized baskets representing each user's expected future
expenses. The system requires interest-bearing denominations like
wrapped stocks or ETH rather than fiat, and could incorporate
autonomous agents for more accurate and full-time information
gathering.

 EVERYTHING IS MARKET (6 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Markets are evolving from outputs into infrastructure, resembling
"markets as APIs" that produce real-time data through adversarial

permissionless markets as regulatory forcing functions, with the
vision extending to one billion traders plus AI agents consuming
