 POLYMARKET BETTORS THREATEN REPORTER OVER MISSILE REPORT (2 MINUTE

 Emanuel Fabian, a Times of Israel military correspondent, says he
received dozens of messages and explicit death threats after reporting

affected a Polymarket contract that had more than $14 million wagered.

prompting Polymarket to ban the accounts involved and lawmakers in
Washington to push legislation like the Death Bets Act to ban
contracts tied to war, assassinations, or fatalities.

 ORMR: FUNDING RATE TRADING VIA PENDLE BOROS (4 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 ORMR is an open-source automated trading bot that captures yield from
funding rate dislocations through Pendle's Boros protocol, using
Synth's AI forecasting network (200+ competing ML models on Bittensor)
for volatility predictions and liquidation cascade detection. The bot
runs three core strategies вЂ“ carry trades on fixed/floating rate
spreads, volatility regime plays positioning ahead of funding spikes,
and liquidation catalyst trades front-running overleveraged position
cascades. A 90-day backtest from December 2025 through March 2026
showed a 2,868% return on $1,000 starting capital across 399 trades
with a 64.7% win rate.

 WHY PUBLIC AI MODELS WON'T GIVE RETAIL AN EDGE (4 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Research replicating the NeurIPS best paper "Artificial Hivemind" in
a crypto trading context shows that GPT-5.4, Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.6,
Gemini 3.1 Pro, and Qwen3 Max all recommend identical trades (buy SOL)
across 150 experiments when given the same 30-second orderbook data
вЂ“ even at high temperature settings. The homogeneity implies that
unsophisticated retail traders using basic AI prompts will herd into
identical positions, with first-movers profiting and latecomers
serving as exit liquidity. Expert traders with richer context may
maintain an edge, but public models with basic inputs are structurally
unlikely to generate sustained alpha.

 AUTOMATING CRYPTO RESEARCH WITH AI (6 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 It is possible to build an AI-augmented crypto research workflow
using three layers: signal detection (Lookonchain, Nansen smart money
alerts), context investigation (Arkham wallet mapping, Dune SQL
queries with AI-generated queries), and LLM synthesis (Claude for deep
analysis over large data sets, Perplexity for cited sources). The
system reduces daily research from 4+ hours to ~30 minutes through
structured prompt templates for protocol deep dives, wallet behavior
analysis, and narrative-vs-reality checks. Expert tips include never
trusting AI outputs blindly, using AI for research rather than
execution speed, and matching tools to tasks based on context window
capabilities.
