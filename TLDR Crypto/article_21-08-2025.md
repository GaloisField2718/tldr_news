 BINANCE, COINBASE JOIN TRM LABS TO COMBAT CRYPTO CRIME (3 MINUTE
READ) [6]

2023, with the Beacon initiative targeting threats from ransomware,
North Korean hackers, and terrorist financiers.

 INTRODUCING ZKC COIN FROM BOUNDLESS (10 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Boundless's ZK Coin (ZKC) is the native token of its universal ZK
proving protocol. It enables any blockchain to access zero-knowledge
computation through independent prover nodes without requiring
infrastructure changes. The ZK proving protocol introduces Proof of
Verifiable Work (PoVW), a novel consensus mechanism where provers
stake ZKC as collateral (typically 10x the request fee) and earn
rewards by generating unique ZK proofs, with slashing penalties for
failed deliveries. ZKC has an initial supply of 1 billion tokens with
7% inflation in Year 1, tapering to 3% by Year 8, and the effective
circulating supply decreases as more proofs require collateral
staking. The system creates a scaling loop where increased proof
demand leads to more ZKC locked as collateral, positioning ZK Coin as
the foundational economic layer for ZK proving across the entire
blockchain ecosystem.

 COINBASE BETS ON AI PAYMENTS FUELED BY DIGITAL DOLLARS (7 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 Coinbase is positioning itself as infrastructure for the emerging AI
economy with x402, an open-source payments protocol that enables apps,
bots, and autonomous systems to charge and settle payments instantly
using stablecoins. This strategic pivot represents a deliberate shift
away from speculative trading toward building practical payment rails
for AI agents that need to make micro-transactions for computing costs
or services. Coinbase is betting that stablecoins will become the
default payment method for AI commerce, offering advantages like
instant settlement, programmability, and freedom from traditional
banking restrictions. 

 AN INTRO TO HYPERGAMBLIFIED MARKET DESIGN (11 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Successful crypto gaming requires "hypergamblification" â€“
integrating speculative mechanics directly into gameplay rather than
treating games as labor-based extraction systems like Play-to-Earn
models did. Play-to-Earn collapsed because it removed the fun and
speculation that makes games sticky, turning players into extractors
rather than participants in risk-based entertainment. Onchain
infrastructure makes speculation-based gaming inevitable by providing
instant liquidity, composable markets, transparent odds, and
programmable settlement that can turn any game into a micro-market.
Platforms like Multiplier are building this vision through gamified
capital markets that route attention directly into market demand,
representing a shift toward "an arcade wired into a financial system"
where speculation and entertainment are inseparably fused.
