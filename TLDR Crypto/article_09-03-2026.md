 TRUMP'S CYBER STRATEGY INTEGRATES CRYPTO AND BLOCKCHAIN SECURITY (3
MINUTE READ) [4] 

 The Trump administration has formally incorporated cryptocurrency and
blockchain security into its national "Cyber Strategy for America,"
placing digital asset infrastructure alongside AI and quantum
computing as pillars of US technological leadership. The document
commits the federal government to "support the security of
cryptocurrencies and blockchain technologies" with an emphasis on
privacy-by-design across supply chains, though it introduces no new
regulatory framework, functioning instead as a strategic posture
signal. Blockchain is evolving from a regulatory gray zone to an
explicitly recognized component of national economic and security
infrastructure. 

 FLORIDA PASSES FIRST STATE-LEVEL STABLECOIN FRAMEWORK (2 MINUTE READ)
[5] 

 Florida's Senate Bill 314 passed a State Senate vote mandating that
stablecoin issuers obtain licenses from the state's Office of
Financial Regulation while aligning with the federal GENIUS Act's
consumer protection standards. If signed by Governor DeSantis, Florida
would become the first US state to establish a standalone stablecoin
regulatory framework. The bill arrives as the broader stablecoin yield
debate intensifies at the federal level, with Coinbase lobbying to
allow interest-bearing stablecoin products, incumbent banks pushing
back over deposit migration risk, and President Trump publicly siding
with the crypto industry. State-level licensing regimes layered atop
federal frameworks could create compliance complexity for issuers
operating nationally, making Florida's model a closely watched
template.

 XSTOCKS LAUNCHES XCHANGE FOR ONCHAIN STOCK TRADING (3 MINUTE READ)
[6] 

 xChange is a platform that enables atomic onchain trading of
tokenized equities across Solana, Ethereum, and Ink, with additional
chain support planned. The goal is to bridge traditional stock market
liquidity directly into onchain execution environments. xStocks'
relationship with Kraken insinuates that the path to tokenization will
happen through existing exchange integrations and gradually move
onchain. 

 WESTERN UNION LAUNCHING USDPT STABLECOIN ON SOLANA (2 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 Western Union's USDPT is a Solana-based stablecoin backed by
Crossmint's wallet and payment API infrastructure, redeemable across
its 360,000 cash locations in 200+ countries. This is one of the most
significant TradFi entries into the stablecoin market. Western Union's
existing physical distribution network offers a rare real-world
redemption layer at scale.

 WHAT ETHENA IS TELLING US ABOUT THE CRYPTO MARKET (6 MINUTE READ) [8]


 Ethena's deployed capital has fallen to $791M вҖ“ 71% below the April
2025 low of $1.1B вҖ“ serving as a high-signal proxy for broader
crypto market risk appetite rather than a protocol-specific issue. The
protocol's basis position has contracted over 60% from north of $2B to
sub-$800M since BTC's drawdown to $60K, driven by negative funding
rates forcing basis trade unwinds, reduced long demand, and an influx
of hedging activity from VCs and crypto companies managing runway
through structured products that short correlated asset baskets.
Hundreds of small-cap projects represent a cohort of distressed
balance sheets actively cap-loss hedging, introducing a sustained
structural short pressure that is suppressing net long exposure across
the market. For arguably the first time in crypto's history,
directional longs and shorts are approaching parity. 

 AGENTIC COMMERCE WON'T KILL CARDS, BUT IT'LL OPEN A GAP (5 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 Traditional card networks are not under threat from stablecoins for
existing merchants, as evidenced by Visa's 16B+ tokenized credentials,
Mastercard's live Agent Pay for US cardholders, and the Stripe/OpenAI
Agentic Commerce Protocol already onboarding Etsy with 1M+ Shopify
merchants queued. The real displacement vector is a new merchant class
emerging from AI-assisted development: 36M developers joined GitHub
last year, 67% of Bolt.new's 5M users are non-developers, and 25% of
YC's W25 cohort had 95%+ AI-generated codebases вҖ“ producing API
tools and data services whose operators lack the entities and track
records to qualify for merchant accounts. Protocols like x402 address
this gap by embedding stablecoin payments natively into HTTP requests,
eliminating the merchant account requirement and making stablecoins
the default rail for this segment. The incumbent payment system will
eventually adapt, but the interim window represents a material
opportunity for stablecoin-native payment rails to capture an entire
generation of AI-born merchants.

 BLACKROCK PRIVATE CREDIT FUND IS LATEST TO CRACK (4 MINUTE READ) [10]


 BlackRock's $26 billion private credit fund restricted withdrawals,
joining Blue Owl in a sector-wide liquidity crunch. This stress
threatens the $5 billion tokenized credit market and crypto prices
through macro deleveraging. Recent defaults demonstrate how off-chain
credit failures trigger on-chain liquidations and collateral
volatility. 

 YOU THINK BLOCKCHAIN IS A SCAM BECAUSE YOUR BANK WORKS (7 MINUTE
READ) [11] 

 In emerging markets where traditional finance fails users,
stablecoins have become essential infrastructure rather than
speculative instruments. Sub-Saharan Africa pays ~8% on $200
remittances, Nigeria received $19.5B in remittances in 2023 and became
the world's second-largest crypto economy by transaction volume
despite a government ban, and Argentina's ~200% inflation has pushed
three-quarters of workers who receive crypto salaries to select
stablecoins over volatile assets. Stablecoins now represent 43% of all
crypto transaction volume in Sub-Saharan Africa, with USDT comprising
89% of activity on Yellow Card and 70% of that usage tied to personal
financial needs rather than trading.
