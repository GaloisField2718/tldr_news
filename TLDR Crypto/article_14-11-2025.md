 THE TRUSTLESS MANIFESTO (10 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Ethereum's decentralization erodes through convenience as hosted
RPCs, centralized sequencers, and upgrade keys create dependence on
intermediaries most users cannot realistically replace. The manifesto
defines trustlessness through three laws: no critical secrets held by
single actors, no indispensable intermediaries that only specialists
can run, and no unverifiable outcomes hidden from public data.
Builders must reject systems requiring faith in intermediaries and
measure success by trust reduced per transaction rather than
throughput.

 DEX WASH TRADING (5 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Wash trading inflates DEX volumes significantly, with Uniswap's
GOLDEN-WETH pool showing $5 billion in November volume from just 14
traders, including one address responsible for $1.8 billion on a
single day. Zero protocol fees previously allowed LPs to trade their
own pools without capital leakage, but Uniswap's fee switch will force
wash traders to migrate as value now flows to the protocol. Filtering
wash trading and memecoin pools reduces Uniswap Ethereum's projected
30-day revenue from inflated figures to around $6 million, with actual
buyback execution expected to reveal a stark gap between reported pool
accrual and genuine value capture.
