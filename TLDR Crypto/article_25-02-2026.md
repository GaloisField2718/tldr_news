 TERRAFORM LABS SUES JANE STREET FOR INSIDER TRADING PRIOR TO TERRA
COLLAPSE (4 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Terraform Labs' liquidation administrator has filed a lawsuit against
Jane Street and several employees, alleging they used non-public
information from Terraform insiders to front-run trades during the
Terra-Luna collapse in 2022 that destroyed over $40 billion in value.
The complaint cites a wallet linked to Jane Street that withdrew 85
million TerraUSD from Curve within 10 minutes of Terraform's
unannounced 150 million withdrawal on May 7, 2022, and claims Jane
Street planted a former Terraform employee to establish insider
communication channels. The lawsuit also connects to the
administrator's filing against Jump Trading for $4 billion in damages
in December, which alleges that Jump leaked information to Jane
Street. Jane Street disputes the claims as "opportunistic."
