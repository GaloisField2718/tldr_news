MARKETS & BUSINESS

 LAWMAKERS PROPOSE TAX RELIEF FOR SMALL STABLECOIN TRANSACTIONS (4
MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Representatives Max Miller and Steven Horsford proposed amending the
IRS Code to exempt stablecoin transactions under $200 from capital
gains taxes if issued by GENIUS Act-permitted issuers pegged to the
dollar with stable trading ranges, while excluding brokers and dealers
from the benefit. The draft allows taxpayers to defer income
recognition on staking and mining rewards for up to five years rather
than immediate taxation, addressing "phantom income" concerns, and
extends securities lending treatment to digital asset lending
arrangements. 

 COINBASE SUES THREE STATES OVER PREDICTION MARKET REGULATION (5
MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Coinbase filed federal lawsuits against Connecticut, Michigan, and
Illinois, arguing that prediction markets fall under the CFTC's
exclusive federal jurisdiction rather than state gaming boards. The
Illinois Gaming Board issued cease-and-desist letters to Kalshi,
Robinhood, and Crypto.com in April over sports event contracts,
claiming state law violations. Chief Legal Officer Paul Grewal argues
Congress deliberately excluded only specific commodities like onions
from CFTC jurisdiction, making all other subjects, including sports,
federally regulated.

 QUANTUM & BITCOIN (10 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Quantum computing is transitioning from ‚Äúremote theory‚Äù to a
plausible engineering timeline, and Bitcoin's slow governance means
the network needs to start post-quantum prep now, not when a
‚ÄúQ-day‚Äù headline hits. The core fears include keys getting
cracked, panic, contentious forks, and institutional confidence
breaking if the community has to decide under duress. Furthermore,
there's a world where millions of BTC in old ‚Äúpay-to-public-key‚Äù
outputs (including Satoshi-era coins) that can't be proactively

 JPMORGAN'S TOKENIZED DOLLARS ARE REWIRING MONEY (7 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 JPMorgan launched its JPM Coin tokenized deposits on Coinbase's Base
blockchain, driven by institutional demand for bank deposit products
on public chains that can be interest-bearing, unlike stablecoins
restricted by the GENIUS Act. The permissioned, whitelisted JPMD
serves clients using Coinbase for crypto transactions as collateral
and margin payments, offering different risk profiles than stablecoins
or off-chain bank accounts with cutoff time issues. Tokenized deposits
are the "cousin of stablecoins" as banks face interoperability
challenges exporting products beyond their four walls versus
stablecoins' broader distribution. The bank maintains sole control
over the smart contract with proper key storage and role separation,
positioning public blockchain infrastructure as the destination for
innovation and customer activity.

 STABLECOINS' REAL PRODUCT IS BETTER SETTLEMENT (4 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Stablecoin transaction volume roughly doubled YoY, but the growth
isn't mainly consumers buying coffee. The biggest slices are Internal
transfers and B2B flows, with retail-like categories (P2P/P2B/B2P)
much smaller in comparison. There's also a clear weekday greater than
weekend pattern, which is exactly what you'd expect if stablecoins are
increasingly used as business settlement plumbing. 

 AI X CRYPTO IS DEAD, LONG LIVE AI X CRYPTO (5 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 The AI x crypto theme evolved from 2023's decentralized data
marketplaces and compute sharing to 2025's autonomous agents managing
assets and executing trades. Bittensor surpassed $10 billion market

development, allowing non-technical founders to launch businesses in

centralization risks while AI enhances blockchain usability.
