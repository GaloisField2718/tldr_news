[5]

 CREATOR COINS ARE FAILING FOR A REASON NO ONE WANTS TO ADMIT (5
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Creator Coins on Zora and Base are failing because platforms frame
them as investment opportunities through trading contests and
"green/red" categories rather than fan support tools, creating
pump-and-dump cycles where early snipers and bots profit while late
participants absorb losses, destroying community trust. Most creators
lack audiences that sustain ongoing demand beyond initial Jesse Pollak
amplification and platform features, with trading fees peaking in
hours then vanishing as prices fall when temporary attention fades.
Web2's sustainable model shows fans spending on merch, donations, and
virtual gifts without profit expectations, suggesting Creator Coins
should function as loyalty tokens and Content Coins as onchain tip
jars where value flows from appreciation, not speculation. 

 STATE OF FINTECH 2026 (4 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Fintech hyperscalers Nubank, Klarna, and Revolut reached 127 million,
114 million, and 65 million customers, respectively, as the sector
bifurcated between neobanks targeting mass affluent, stablecoins
attacking corporate cross-border rails, and AI unbundling asset
manager workflows, though only Stripe deployed industry-specific
foundation models driving earnings despite AI assisting one in six
Black Friday purchases. Banks posted record years through M&A
tailwinds as prediction markets had their breakout with Wall Street
making strategic bets, positioning platforms as price discovery rather
than gambling. Financial nihilism emerged as a political force as
consumers abandoned 60/40 portfolio retirement belief due to housing
costs breaking the social contract, while open finance quietly
succeeded as cash flow data was integrated into Experian and FICO
scoring.

 AAVE LABS BRAND ACQUISITION PROPOSAL (6 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Aave Labs advanced a brand-rights proposal to Snapshot vote without
author Ernesto Boado's consent, prompting accusations of a "hostile
takeover attempt" from Marc Zeller, who criticized the holiday timing
and recent wave of new delegations with sizable voting power. Boado
called the action "disgraceful" and urged abstention as the proposal
seeks to give AAVE token holders explicit control of domains, social
handles, GitHub organizations, and naming rights currently stewarded
by Aave Labs and BGD Labs. Aave Labs defended the move as following
documented governance rules requiring a five-day ARFC minimum before
voting, arguing prolonged debate was "hurting the Aave brand" and
critics were trying to rewrite rules because they lacked votes. The
dispute escalated earlier tensions over CoW Swap revenue allegedly
diverted from the DAO treasury and a "poison pill" proposal suggesting
the DAO absorb Aave Labs entirely, as the AAVE token slumped over 10%
in 24 hours.
