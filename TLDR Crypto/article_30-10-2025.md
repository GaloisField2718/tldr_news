MARKETS & BUSINESS

 STABLECOIN INFLOWS RISE BEFORE FED RATE DECISION (7 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Bitcoin held around $113,000 as traders rotated into stablecoins
ahead of the Federal Reserve's expected 25 basis point rate cut, with
stablecoin supply rising for the first time since September, while
exchange order book depth collapsed to 40% of pre-liquidation levels.
BTC futures open interest remained at $26.8 billion with highly
divergent funding rates amid $514 million in 24-hour liquidations
split 69-31 between longs and shorts. Spot BTC ETFs recorded $202.4
million in daily inflows while ETH ETFs saw $246 million, as
Grayscale's Solana Trust converted to a spot ETF (GSOL) on NYSE Arca
and World Liberty Financial prepared to airdrop 8.4 million WLFI
tokens to early USD1 users following a points program that drove USD1
to become the sixth-largest stablecoin. 

 CONSENSYS PREPARING IPO WITH JPMORGAN AND GOLDMAN SACHS (3 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 Consensys has reportedly hired JPMorgan and Goldman Sachs to lead its
initial public offering. The move follows other successful crypto IPOs
this year, including Circle and Bullish, as firms capitalize on a
renewed wave of institutional and regulatory openness under the
current administration's pro-crypto stance. Consensys also operates
Infura, Linea, and backs ETH treasury firm SharpLink, positioning
itself as a core pillar of Ethereum's infrastructure ahead of the IPO.

INNOVATION & LAUNCHES

 WESTERN UNION TO LAUNCH USD-BACKED STABLECOIN ON SOLANA IN 2026 (3
MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Western Union announced plans to launch the US Dollar Payment Token
(USDPT), a Solana-based stablecoin issued by Anchorage Digital Bank,
in the first half of 2026. The token will allow Western Union's 100
million customers to send money internationally without relying on
local currencies, expanding its 175-year legacy of global payments
into the digital asset era. 

 BRALE LAUNCHES API BRIDGING BANK RAILS AND STABLECOINS (2 MINUTE
READ) [7] 

 Brale has launched a powerful new API that bridges traditional
banking rails with onchain payments, enabling users to ACH debit via
Plaid and mint stablecoins instantly. The API supports custodial and
non-custodial wallets, ~20 blockchains, and ~50 stablecoins, all at
bank-level pricing (as low as $0.01 per transaction). It also includes
RTP support, customizable transfer branding, and the ability to fund
any off-chain payment with stablecoins, creating a low-cost link
between legacy finance and crypto-native systems.

 LOOPING TO ZERO: WHAT'S ABOUT TO HAPPEN (8 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Crypto is repeating the 1980s-90s hedge fund era when blowups like
LTCM led to the creation of funds of funds as a middle layer for
allocation and risk management. Vaults now require the same onchain
infrastructure that traditional finance developed after massive
failures. Onchain funds of funds would handle full risk management
systems tracking positions across vaults, quantitative risk checks,
manager due diligence, protocol-level fundamental research, and
dynamic rebalancing to maintain concentration limits and risk-adjusted
returns. Crypto users lack the expertise to properly size vault
positions or track underlying risks, creating demand for professional
fund-of-funds services that will reach billions in AUM as the winners
filter for best strategies and publish public research to justify
allocations. 

 WHAT IS N1? (6 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 N1 is a permissioned high-performance L1 launching with sub-1ms
latency, 100,000+ TPS per application through dedicated compute
environments, and gasless transactions where protocols pay less than a
cent in fees instead of users. The chain supports Solidity, Rust, and
TypeScript development with in-house collaboration on flagship apps to
avoid ecosystem fragmentation, using Jito (Re)staking for validation
and NordVM for asynchronous virtual machine execution.

 THE ULTIMATE SUPERCYCLE (10 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 The 2020s mark the start of an 80-year civilizational reinvention
cycle with AI, clean energy, and bioengineering converging for the
first time as solar and wind costs drop 20% per doubling of output
while CRISPR makes biology programmable. Crypto serves as the
coordination layer through blockchains and zero-knowledge proofs,
enabling a shift from financial capitalism optimized for extraction to
sustainable capitalism based on transparent protocols and
abundance-driven cooperation. This author's investment thesis
expresses this supercycle view by going long crypto (trust layer), AI
(intelligence layer), and abundance plays in energy storage and
bioengineering. They predict a 25-year boom despite current political
division and institutional turbulence that mirrors crisis periods
preceding past reinventions. 

 A YEAR ONE PROJECTION OF HIP-3 REVENUE (18 MINUTE READ) [13]

requiring 500,000 HYPE stake (~$20 million) and charging double the

$16 million in auction revenue, and 5 million HYPE staked across 10
markets in year one at 5% of total Hyperliquid volume. The upgrade
responds to $3.3 billion bridging to competing alt-perp DEXs like
Lighter, Aster, and EdgeX, with nearly 20 teams announcing HIP-3
markets. Equity perps remain unproven with weak demand â€“ Ostium
