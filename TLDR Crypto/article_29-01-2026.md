 PREDICTION CAPITAL MARKETS (5 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Despite generating $150B+ in volume and $800M in revenue through
platforms like Pump since January 2024, creator tokens suffer from a
fundamental structural flaw: perpetual onchain instruments tracking
ephemeral attention create perpetual bagholders by design. The lack of
objective resolution mechanisms means winners are determined by exit
timing rather than correctness, enabling manipulation while forcing
creators to absorb direct reputation damage whenever they withdraw
fees or token prices decline. The proposed solution replaces perpetual
creator tokens with time-bounded markets featuring defined speculation
windows and resolution endpoints that match the ephemeral nature of
attention cycles. 

 EIGENCLOUD: VERIFIABLE OFF-CHAIN COMPUTATION (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 EigenCloud introduces a hybrid verification model that combines
Trusted Execution Environments (TEE) with restaking-based economic
security to enable verifiable off-chain computation, addressing the
fundamental lack of transparency in traditional cloud services while
circumventing blockchain's computational limitations. The platform
allows complex operations like AI inference and GPU-intensive
workloads to execute off-chain with cryptographic proof of correct
execution, bridging the gap between Web2 scalability and Web3
verifiability. Its architecture supports familiar developer tools,
including Docker containers and external API calls, requiring minimal
smart contract expertise to implement verifiable applications. Target
use cases span prediction markets, institutional finance, cross-chain
security infrastructure, and AI agent ecosystems where result
verification and trustless coordination are critical.
