GUIDES & TUTORIALS

 DEPIN - A NEW PARADIGM IN CRYPTO (11 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 This article gives an overview of the DePIN space, a new sector in
cryptocurrency that pertains to Decentralized Physical Infrastructure
Networks. After an initial surge in prices, DePIN projects seem primed
for promising future growth. The broad category encompasses both
physical and digital resources. Its benefits include resource
efficiency, lower barriers to entry, and decentralization. This
article highlights several DePIN projects, including Filecoin, Helium,
and Shadow. 

 WHAT HAPPENS TO ALTCOINS AFTER THE BITCOIN HALVING (3 MINUTE READ)
[10] 

 The Bitcoin halving is usually a positive catalyst for altcoins and
this is expected to be the case again this cycle. While the extent to
which this happens as it did in prior cycles is uncertain, it is
highly unlikely the market will go into an extended cooldown phase
after the halving.

 THE TOKEN IS THE PRODUCT (9 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Tokenization plays a pivotal role in the distribution strategy for
blockchain-based products, often being more significant than the
product itself. Founders who focus on attracting attention and
liquidity to their tokens can create successful crypto projects and
substantial network effects. Examples include Uniswap and the Helium
Network, which used tokens to incentivize network participation and
subsequently gained great traction because of that. 

 NEW BLACKROCK TOKENIZED FUND GARNERS $160 MILLION IN DEPOSITS (2
MINUTE READ) [12] 

 BlackRock's first tokenized investment fund, the BlackRock USD
Institutional Digital Liquidity Fund (BUIDL), has attracted $160
million in inflows within the first week of its launch, boosted by
contributions from tokenized real-world asset platform, Ondo Finance.
The fund, which invests in U.S. Treasury bills, repurchase agreements,
and cash, provides dividends to qualified investors in the form of
tokens representing U.S. dollar yield.
