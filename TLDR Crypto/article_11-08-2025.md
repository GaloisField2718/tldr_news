 WHITE HOUSE CRYPTO COUNCIL HEAD BO HINES TO STEP DOWN (2 MINUTE READ)
[4]

stepping down and returning to the private sector. His deputy, Patrick
Witt, will take over. Hines, appointed by President Trump in late
2024, met with over 150 crypto leaders during his tenure and will
continue advising on AI initiatives under David Sacks.

competitors like Helium Mobile and Starlink by focusing on areas with
higher mobile user density.
