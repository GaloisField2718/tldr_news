 BANKS' CRYPTO RULES MUST BE REWORKED (5 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Basel Committee chair Erik ThedÃ©en acknowledged crypto capital rules
need reworking after the US and UK refused to implement the 2021
framework requiring banks to hold large capital reserves against

framework's planned January implementation.

GUIDES & TUTORIALS

 STABLECOIN CARDS VS BANKS (7 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Today's stablecoin cards ride on Visa and issuing banks'
infrastructure, taking a slice of interchange while offering better
UX, global access, DeFi yield on idle balances, and token-subsidized
cashback, which makes them look insanely attractive but effectively
eats into banks' margins. This won't last forever because, as crypto
cards start replacing primary bank accounts rather than adding extra
volume, banks and regulators will fight back by squeezing interchange
and reclaiming economics, forcing stablecoin card players either to
own more of the payment stack (licenses, rails, maybe bank-to-bank or
wallet-to-wallet) or accept a hard cap on their upside. 

 THE GREAT CONSOLIDATION (16 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 The Great Consolidation is accelerating as $70 billion in altcoin
unlocks approach and fragmented liquidity across 25,000+ daily token
launches becomes unsustainable, with most alts down 50-70% from highs
as Bitcoin dominance rises and institutional flows bypass alt season
entirely. The industry must consolidate memes toward lindy names,
eliminate copycat Alt-L1 ecosystems offering no unique value
proposition, and push apps toward superapp models as solo farming
becomes unprofitable and retail rejects low float, high FDV exit
liquidity games. Ethereum faces existential challenges, capturing only
1% of Base fees while ETH/BTC hits multi-year lows with no clear value
accrual narrative as L2 fragmentation and stablecoin adoption
undermine the moneyness thesis, though blobs and potential L2
"tariffs" offer long-term scalability paths. Winners will be
fee-generating protocols like Aave, Ethena, Uniswap, and Hyperliquid
that established product-market fit before token launch as
fundamentals replace speculation and supply overhangs crush
hype-driven projects.
