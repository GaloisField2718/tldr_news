 BINANCE INTEGRATES CIRCLE'S USYC AS YIELD-BEARING COLLATERAL (2
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Binance has integrated Circle's tokenized money market fund, USYC, as
off-exchange collateral for institutional derivatives trading,
deepening the partnership between two of crypto's biggest players.
USYC, comparable to BlackRock's BUIDL, offers yield exposure to U.S.
Treasurys and near-instant convertibility with USDC, making it a
capital-efficient bridge between tokenized dollars and real-world
assets. The integration, which includes native issuance on BNB Chain,
marks a major move for Circle's RWA strategy and comes shortly after
its IPO and acquisition of tokenization startup Hashnote.
