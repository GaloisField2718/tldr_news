 IF YOU LIKE PREDICTION MARKETS YOU SHOULD LEARN ABOUT FUTARCHY (4
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Futarchy is a governance model that combines democratic voting on
desired outcomes with prediction markets to determine which policies
are most likely to achieve those goals, following the principle "vote
on values, bet on beliefs." Traditional democracy aggregates
preferences but not knowledge since being wrong carries no cost and
policies are chosen by popularity rather than accuracy, while
prediction markets require participants to risk capital and reward
correct predictions. Citizens would vote on outcomes they care about,
like GDP growth or employment levels, then prediction markets would
compete to identify which specific policies are most likely to deliver
those results, creating real-time probabilities based on collective
intelligence rather than ideology. Crypto infrastructure now enables
futarchy to be tested at scale through open participation, transparent
settlement, and programmable incentives, with DAOs already
experimenting with prediction market governance for resource
allocation and policy evaluation.

 SOLANA'S PROPRIETARY AMM REVOLUTION (24 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Solana has witnessed the emergence of proprietary AMMs, a new
market-making primitive that embeds trading strategies directly
onchain rather than relying on off-chain execution, with daily volumes
consistently exceeding $1 billion and capturing over 60% of SOL/USDC
trading volume. These venues, operated by professional market makers
like SolFi, Tessera, and HumidiFi, provide "active liquidity" through
continuously adjusted pricing strategies anchored to oracle feeds,
fundamentally differing from traditional "passive liquidity" models of
constant-product DEXs. The model is uniquely enabled by Solana's
structural advantages, including high retail participation, low-cost
oracle updates, and Jupiter's aggregator dominance, which provides
single-integration access to non-toxic retail order flow. 

 RETHINKING TOKEN SUPPLY (6 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Outstanding Supply is a new token supply metric that mirrors
Outstanding Shares on the stock market to fix inconsistencies in
crypto valuations. It offers a clearer picture of true market cap
versus FDV (overstated) or Circulating Supply (inconsistent). The
framework also refines Circulating Supply into a smarter version that
excludes locked or insider tokens, giving investors apples-to-apples
comparability, transparency, and more accurate valuation multiples.
Outstanding Supply equals Total Supply minus Protocol Holdings.
