 ETHEREUM'S BLOCKCHAIN TRILEMMA SOLUTION (3 MINUTE READ) [6]

 ETHEREUM'S PURPOSE: RESILIENCE, NOT CONVENIENCE (2 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Ethereum's core mission is to maximize resilience and digital
sovereignty, not to chase marginal efficiency or convenience gains. It
prioritizes these factors to ensure users retain access and control
even if cloud providers, developers, or corporations fail or censor,
making decentralized, permissionless blockspace a safeguard against
catastrophic loss and unequal dependence.

 OPEN BANKING VS. CARD PAYMENTS: CAN PISPS DISRUPT THE CARD STACK? (3
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Traditional card payments rely on a complex four-party model that
routes transactions through acquirers, card schemes, and issuers, with
fees distributed across each layer. Open banking proposes a simpler
flow, where Payment Initiation Service Providers (PISPs) can initiate
payments directly from a user's bank account with consent. While this
could bypass card schemes and reshape the payment value chain, card
networks still provide large-scale clearing and settlement, meaning
open banking payments may ultimately replicate similar
batch-settlement dynamics rather than fully replacing cards.
