 EULEREARN: PASSIVE YIELD, PROFESSIONALLY CURATED (6 MINUTE READ) [6]

deploy and manage ERC-4626 vault strategies without gatekeeping. It

community-driven control.

 THE INVISIBLE UPGRADE: WHY INSTITUTIONS SHOULD CARE ABOUT CRYPTO
RAILS (7 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Institutional finance is undergoing a fundamental infrastructure
upgrade through crypto rails, moving beyond speculative use cases to
address core operational inefficiencies in traditional systems like
T+2 settlement and fragmented reconciliation processes. Zero-knowledge
proofs enable institutions to maintain privacy while proving
compliance, solvency, or creditworthiness without exposing sensitive
portfolio data to centralized gatekeepers who create security
vulnerabilities. Ethereum's L2 scaling solutions offer real-time
settlement and near-zero cost transactions with built-in execution
guarantees, while smart contracts can enforce compliance, portfolio
limits, and trading mandates natively rather than through retrofitted
oversight systems.
