 AAVE EMERGES AS DEFI'S BENCHMARK RATE STANDARD (4 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Just as SOFR and Fed Funds rates anchor TradFi, DeFi now needs
standardized benchmark rates - and Aave is becoming that foundation.
With $58B in net deposits and over 50% of DeFi lending market share,
Aave's rates reflect true market-wide credit conditions. Protocols
like CoinDesk's Overnight Rates (CDOR), Cap, Level USD, and Plasma
already use Aave's rates to price capital, route idle assets, or set
hurdle yields. Its stability (e.g., only 20bps movement on a $100M
deposit) enables floating-rate lending, structured products, and
institutional risk management. Aave's dominance makes it DeFi's
reference layer for rate discovery, liquidity, and scalability. 

 ZORA TURNS POSTS INTO MARKETS, POWERED BY PRIVY WALLETS (4 MINUTE

social app, where every post and profile is a tradable ERC20. Creator

users to onboard via content instead of crypto complexity. With over
4M wallets and 2M+ monthly actives, Zora's model blurs the line
between social media and financial protocol, turning followers into
participants and content into capital.
