NEWS & TRENDS

 SCARY TIMES: HOW TO LEAD THROUGH LAYOFF FEAR (10 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Product leaders are now expected to guide teams through not just
layoffs, but the constant fear of them. Honest communication, focusing
on controllable actions, and coaching teams toward meaningful impact -
not survival - can preserve trust and resilience when stability
disappears. 

 THE BROWSER BECOMES AN AGENT (5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Browsers are evolving from passive tools into intelligent agent
platforms that understand, reason, and act for users. This shift
transforms browsing into proactive collaboration, redefining how
humans and AI interact online.

 8 HABITS OF HIGHLY EFFECTIVE PRODUCT LEADERS (6 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Effective product leadership centers on empowering teams, fostering
clarity, and leading by example. Great leaders stay close to the work,
coach over command, adapt their style, and prioritize people above
process. 

 13 PRODUCT-BUILDING LESSONS FROM PALANTIR (9 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 High-impact products come from trusted product-engineering
partnerships grounded in accountability, field immersion, and shared
ownership. Prioritize strengths, build feedback-rich, empathetic
cultures, and design for scalability and speed without sacrificing
trust.
