OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 THE PRODUCT MODEL AT GOOGLE (10 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Google's success comes from a product model that empowers teams to
solve hard problems through continuous discovery, strong technical
leadership, and outcome-driven execution. This approach has allowed
the company to scale, dominate mature markets, and adapt effectively
to major shifts like mobile and AI. 

 THE ODDS ARE AGAINST FINANCIAL SUPERAPPS (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Financial superapps fail when they optimize for short-term
speculation instead of long-term trust. Products that increase churn
may look profitable early, but weaken the business over time.
