 HOW PRODUCT MANAGEMENT CAN FIX YOUR AI INTEGRATION PROBLEMS (6 MINUTE
READ) [14] 

 Most enterprise AI pilots fail to produce ROI by focusing on tool
deployment rather than redesigning workflows. Since pilot teams often
lack the authority to change work processes, these integrations often
become expensive demos. Instead, teams should have the autonomy to
redesign workflows and measure success based on outcomes. 

 TIMESCALES OF PLAY (6 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Effective product collaboration requires recognizing that different
stakeholders perceive the same situation through different
“backgrounds,” which are shaped by timescales. This piece proposes
three timescales to consider and recommends using visual models to
expose shared context and bridge timescale barriers.
