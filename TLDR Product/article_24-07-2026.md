TLDR PRODUCT MANAGEMENT 2026-07-24

NEWS & TRENDS

 WHAT SYSTEMS THINKING LOOKS LIKE FOR PM'ING AI PRODUCTS (5 MINUTE
READ) [4]

 AI products must be cultivated as evolving systems rather than
designed as isolated features. Product teams can create lasting
differentiation by building reusable interfaces, shared contracts, and
feedback loops that help agents improve over time.

 WHAT COMES AFTER "BECOME A BUILDER"? (61 MINUTE PODCAST) [5]

 As AI makes building easier, product managers must differentiate
themselves through judgment, conviction, and customer impact. The best
PMs build systems that scale their expertise and help teams make
better decisions independently.

🚀

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 MOST AI ROLLOUTS FAIL DUE TO CHANGE MANAGEMENT, NOT TECHNOLOGY (4
MINUTE READ) [6]

 Enterprise AI succeeds when companies treat it as a people
transformation, not simply a software rollout. Leaders must create
safe conditions for experimentation while helping effective
employee-led workflows spread across the organization.

 WHY YOUR AI PRODUCT HAS NO MEMORY (4 MINUTE READ) [7]

 AI products improve retention by remembering relevant user context
instead of making every session start over.

🧑‍💻

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 WANT USERS TO STICK AROUND? THESE 5 ONBOARDING TEARDOWNS SHOW YOU HOW
(5 MINUTE READ) [8]

 Effective onboarding helps users experience value by guiding them
toward one important behavior. The best flows combine timely
instruction, purposeful questions, and immediate action to build trust
and encourage repeat use.

 FIVE PRODUCT MANAGEMENT PRINCIPLES FOR INTERNAL DEVELOPER PLATFORMS
(6 MINUTE READ) [9]

 Successful developer platforms make the right workflows easier and
continuously improve based on developer outcomes.

🎁

MISCELLANEOUS

 HOW TO BECOME SO CREATIVE IT FEELS ILLEGAL (11 MINUTE READ) [10]

 Creativity thrives when the mind has space to wander, process
experiences, and notice unexpected connections. Reducing stimulation
and pursuing a meaningful project can restore curiosity and make
original ideas easier to find.

 WHY HASN'T AI INCREASED UNEMPLOYMENT? (8 MINUTE READ) [11]

 AI is beginning to improve productivity without significantly
increasing unemployment because it still depends on human expertise,
direction, and oversight. That balance could change as models become
more capable and automate longer, more complex tasks.

QUICK LINKS

 THE AI PRODUCTIVITY PARADOX (3 MINUTE READ) [12]

 AI improves outcomes when teams use it to validate the right
solutions, not simply build faster.

 DEFENSIBLE, BUT NOT GOOD (2 MINUTE READ) [13]

 Strong teams pursue work that creates real value, not ideas that are
merely defensible.
