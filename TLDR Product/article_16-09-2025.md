 76% OF PRODUCT TEAM MEMBERS ARE EXCITED ABOUT AI. 54% AREN'T SURE HOW
TO USE IT (SPONSOR) [9] 

 Miro surveyed 2,000+ developers, PMs, and designers on AI adoption.
Engineers lead in AI usage - but for others, excitement is not always
keeping pace with confidence. The issue isn't technical skills, but
leadership support for safe experimentation. Read the report [9]

 5 STEPS FROM FEATURES TO OUTCOME ROADMAP (9 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Most roadmaps are plans, not strategy. A real roadmap builds from
narratives, focuses on outcomes, and aligns with company goals. 

 THE MESSAGING MATRIX: HOW TO ALIGN YOUR PRODUCT MESSAGING (17 MINUTE
READ) [11] 

 A messaging matrix aligns product positioning, benefits, and features
across teams to ensure consistent, resonant communication. Create it
cross-functionally, anchor in strategy, tailor by persona, and keep it
current.
