 SUBSCRIPTIONS WILL SURVIVE IN EXACTLY TWO PLACES (18 MINUTE READ) [5]


 Subscriptions thrived because markets rewarded recurring revenue over
ownership. Income pressure, context exhaustion, and AI cost
compression are now breaking the model, leaving only utilities and
continuously fresh context as durable subscriptions. 

 ZILLOW'S CEO ON GROWING THE COMPANY DURING A HOUSING CRISIS (60
MINUTE PODCAST) [6] 

 Zillow's CEO argues the company's future is transaction software and
workflow that makes buying and selling easier, not just a listings
app. In an AI-driven world, Zillow's advantage is execution and
integration across agents and financing, while pushing for
transparent, broadly marketed listings.

 TAKING STOCK OF THE SAASPOCALYPSE (5 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 The SaaS selloff reflects a belief that AI is breaking seat-based
pricing and eroding point-solution moats. Software will keep growing,
but value will shift to AI-native, outcome-driven layers that capture
context and orchestrate work across systems.

 16 PIECES OF DESIGN WISDOM (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Great design comes from love, courage, and consistency more than
tools or process. Ideas travel through polish and alignment, and
designers thrive by treating feedback as dialogue while balancing
reliability with bold experimentation. 

 ENTERPRISE SAAS TRUISMS, REVISITED (8 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 AI is breaking classic SaaS assumptions by shifting moats from
interfaces to context and compressing timelines for ROI and adoption.
The winners will design for agents, governance, and organizational
intelligence rather than traditional seat-based workflows.
