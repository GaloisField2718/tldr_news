 AI AND PRODUCT MANAGEMENT: BECOMING MORE EVIDENCE-GUIDED (9 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 GenAI is often portrayed as a way for product managers to crank out
documents and prototypes faster, but its true power is in enabling
evidence-driven discovery. Used well, it can lighten the cognitive
load of research and analysis while leaving empathy, collaboration,
and human judgment where they belong - with people. 

 IS THE INNOVATOR'S DILEMMA OUTDATED? (12 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Big tech firms don't usually crush startups by copying them - small
markets just aren't worth their effort. However, with AI labs, every
model update spawns accidental products, collapsing the boundary
between platform and application and changing how software, media, and
even financial reporting get made and framed.

 Polished roadmaps create false certainty, waste time, and discourage
change. Useful roadmaps stay messy, flexible, and outcome-focused,

 Advancing into management requires proving that added resources under
your leadership will drive greater business impact. Success comes from
expanding scope, building a strong business case, and cultivating
strategic relationships.
