 CAN ANALYSIS EVER BE AUTOMATED? (8 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 AI can automate analysis, but not trust. Ultimately, users still need
to verify the work. In today's markets, acting boldly or bizarrely may
be more valuable than actual business performance. 

 HOW CAN YOU STREAMLINE THE UNSTOPPABLE THUNDERSTORM OF REQUESTS? (4
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 PMs face constant overload and must streamline prioritization to
avoid becoming overwhelmed by unstructured requests. Successful PMs
standardize idea intake, establish clear goals to enable ruthless
prioritization, and focus on maximizing product value instead of
pleasing everyone.
