 PRODUCT-MARKET FIT IS A TRAP (5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 In AI, product-market fit can become a trap when companies
over-optimize around what worked last quarter while customer
expectations keep changing. Winning teams will stay lean, close to
customers, and willing to replace their own product before the market
moves past them. 

 WHY AI INITIATIVES BREAK NORMAL PRODUCT MANAGER INSTINCTS (6 MINUTE
READ) [7] 

 AI initiatives quickly turn normal product work into transformation
work by exposing messy workflows, unclear ownership, and weak
governance. The best PMs keep momentum by starting with narrow,
low-risk pilots, learning quickly, and adding structure only once the
workflow proves repeatable.
