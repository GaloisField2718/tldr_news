 YOUR PRODUCT HAS A NEW USER. IT'S NOT HUMAN (6 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 AI agents are becoming the real users of products, not humans.
Companies must shift from optimizing for user experience to delivering
reliable, structured outputs that machines can access and evaluate.

 CLAUDE CODE IS NOT MAKING YOUR PRODUCT BETTER (7 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 AI coding agents boost speed but not product quality. The real
constraints are complexity, judgment, and taste, not how fast code
gets written.

 Winbacks are strongest early and signal real product value. Acting
quickly preserves both return rates and revenue.

 AI works best when it strengthens team collaboration, not replaces
