 Software is shifting from human operators to AI agents, making Agent
Experience more important than traditional User Experience. Metrics,
pricing, and product design must move from seat-based human logins to
programmatic, agent-driven integration.

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 WHAT DO ENGINEERS MEAN WHEN WE SAY "TASTE"? (9 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Engineering taste is the ability to calibrate product, system, and
quality decisions based on a deep understanding of what is possible.
As AI automates coding, this judgment becomes the key differentiator
of great engineers, even though it is rarely taught or formalized. 

 FRANCHISE THINKING AS A BUILDER (4 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Product builders should think like franchisors by building systems
that enable others to deliver value consistently. Real scale comes
from clear processes, focused pilots, and disciplined growth rather
than individual effort.

 MOATS (3 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 As AI makes software easier to clone, durable advantages will come
from non-code moats such as scale, proprietary data, and trust.
Long-term value depends on what you uniquely know and who relies on
you, not just how fast you ship.

 Vibe PMing connects AI agents to analytics and workflow tools so PMs

 YOUR SYSTEM IS FINE. YOUR USERS AREN'T (4 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Technical SLOs show whether systems are running, but business SLOs
show whether users are getting value. Teams should define and measure
outcome-based metrics that reflect real user success. 

 YOUR PRODUCT IS NO LONGER THE CENTER OF GRAVITY (3 MINUTE READ) [17] 

 AI-native B2B companies are shifting from standardized products to
systems tailored to each customer's workflow. Scale now comes from
quickly modeling and encoding real business structures instead of
imposing shared abstractions.
