TLDR PRODUCT MANAGEMENT 2026-03-10

 85% SAY AGENTIC AI WILL BE TABLE STAKES WITHIN THREE YEARS. PRODUCT
TEAMS ARE ALREADY ADJUSTING ROADMAPS (SPONSOR) [4] 

 Agentic AI is quickly moving from experiment to roadmap priority. In
a survey of 1,000+ developers and product leaders, most said agentic
capabilities will soon be a baseline expectation across modern
software.

The State of Agentic AI 2026 report [4] explores what that shift looks
like in practice:

 	* 64% of product roadmaps already include agentic AI
 	* 67% of teams are already building or shipping agentic workflows
 	* 94% would consider switching SaaS vendors for stronger agentic AI
functionality

NEWS & TRENDS

 HOW GENERATIVE UI CUT OUR DEVELOPMENT TIME FROM MONTHS TO WEEKS (10
MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Generative UI builds interfaces dynamically using approved
components, contextual data, and a trained language model instead of
fixed layouts. When supported by strong design systems and guardrails,
it can dramatically speed development and tailor software to each
user's situation. 

 FORWARD DEPLOYED DESIGNER (6 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 AI enables designers to embed with teams and rapidly prototype real
solutions, creating a new “Forward Deployed Designer” role. Small
squads that combine design, engineering, and research can dramatically
accelerate learning and product discovery. 

🚀 

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 AI MADE RELATIONSHIPS, NOT FEATURES, THE LAST DEFENSIBLE ADVANTAGE (3
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 As AI commoditizes features, competitive advantage shifts to the
relationship products build with users. Context, history, and trust
create switching costs that features alone no longer can. 

 THE INTERFACE WAS THE PRODUCT (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 AI removes interface learning but also removes the natural discovery
that shows users a product's value. The new challenge is making
capabilities visible and surfacing the right value at the right
moment.

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 HOW GSK USES AI AGENTS TO TURN DATA INTO DECISIONS — NOT DASHBOARDS
(SPONSOR) [9] 

 BI tools were built to report. Not to act. GSK and Huel's data
leaders join ThoughtSpot at a free virtual event to show how AI
analytics agents are replacing static dashboards with answers you can
actually use. Watch the Spotlight Series [10]. Also grab the Embedded
AI Agents Ebook [9] for the full playbook. 

 ORGANIZING PRODUCTIVE PLATFORM TEAMS (4 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Platform systems reflect the structure of the organizations that
build them. Effective platform teams reduce developer complexity by
aligning teams to capabilities, defining clear interfaces, and
evolving with the architecture. 

 SUMMONING THE COURAGE TO FOCUS (2 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Strategy requires the courage to focus by rejecting many good ideas.
Teams must build confidence through testing assumptions and gathering
evidence to prioritize what matters most. 

🎁 

MISCELLANEOUS

 MACHINE LEARNING AT SCALE: MANAGING MORE THAN ONE MODEL IN PRODUCTION
(7 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 At scale, machine learning shifts from optimizing a single model to
managing many models reliably. The key is designing systems that stay
available, fail safely, avoid data leakage, and include human
oversight before models reach users. 

 THE AI-ENABLED ORG IS A VENN DIAGRAM (4 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 AI-driven development increases overlap between product, engineering,
and other roles rather than replacing them. The future likely belongs
to small, highly collaborative teams where disciplines intersect to
deliver products faster.
