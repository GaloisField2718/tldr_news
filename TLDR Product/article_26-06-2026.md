NEWS & TRENDS

 THE AI ERA REQUIRES A DIFFERENT KIND OF EXPERIMENTATION (7 MINUTE
READ) [4] 

 Product experimentation must evolve in the AI era, as traditional
assumptions no longer apply. Shifts include collapsing UI surfaces,
faster development cycles, diminished value in minor copy tweaks due
to AI personalization, and increased inference costs that raise the
stakes for monetization experiments. 

 HOW TO MEASURE DEVELOPMENT PRODUCTIVITY? (6 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Developer activity metrics like code, commits, tokens, and features
measure output, not value. Measure cross-functional teams by the
business and user outcomes they create instead. 

🚀 

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 WHAT BEING "INSPIRING" ACTUALLY MEANS (4 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Inspiring at work means presenting a clear point of view and showing
people, concretely, why taking action will make their work better. 

 THE NEW INNER GAME (7 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 As AI makes knowledge and execution abundant, emotional clarity
becomes a critical advantage. The people and teams that thrive will
make discerning decisions, confront conflict, embrace failure, and
manage fear without letting it dictate their actions. 

🧑‍💻 

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 IS AI FOOLING YOU? (4 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 AI accelerates execution, but without shared vision and
collaboration, it only magnifies misalignment. Strong product teams
use AI to speed up iteration while keeping humans focused on judgment,
alignment, and solving problems that matter. 

 STOP WRITING BLOG POSTS AND CALLING IT SEO (5 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Publishing blog posts for SEO is often just content marketing
masquerading as SEO, which frequently fails to drive business
outcomes. A true product-led SEO strategy prioritizes making your
product discoverable at the moment of intent, routing users directly
into action-oriented conversion flows rather than passive reading. 

🎁 

MISCELLANEOUS

 BUILD THE AGENT OR POWER THE AGENT? (6 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Founders must choose between "building the agent" and "powering the
agent”. This decision hinges on where the customer works, whether
the company owns the full workflow or a slice, and where the product's
value resides: in data/context or capabilities/actions. 

 RICK AND MORTY'S STORYTELLING SECRET TO HOOK ANY READER (4 MINUTE
READ) [11] 

 Dan Harmon's Story Circle turns the Hero's Journey into eight
repeatable steps that move a character from comfort through conflict,
transformation, and consequences. Its strict structure creates room
for creativity, helping writers turn absurd ideas into accessible
stories rooted in meaningful human struggles.

 PRODUCT MANAGER IS THE OLD TITLE (2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 As AI reduces shipping costs, the traditional PM role is shifting
toward Complexity Management: prioritizing strategic restraint, value
clarity, ecosystem-level thinking, and the judgment to recalibrate.
