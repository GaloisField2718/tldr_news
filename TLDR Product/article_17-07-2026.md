NEWS & TRENDS

 IS AI GOING TO TAKE PM JOBS? (7 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 AI will reshape product management, not eliminate it. A broader
technology downturn poses a greater threat to PM jobs than automation.


 THE PROTOTYPE IS A QUESTION, NOT A PRODUCT (8 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 AI makes convincing prototypes easier to build, but polish can create
a false sense of product readiness. A clear decision framework helps
teams define what each prototype can prove, gather the right evidence,
and prevent experimental code from becoming an accidental production
commitment. 

🚀 

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 THE FARABI'S GUIDE TO BUILDING PRODUCTS THAT MATTER (3 MINUTE READ)
[6] 

 Great products help users flourish rather than merely capture
attention. Shared purpose, careful research, and continuous iteration
keep teams focused on that goal. 

 ARE PMS BECOMING IRRELEVANT? (5 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 AI is replacing routine PM work, not strategic product judgment. The
PMs who thrive will focus on choosing the right problems and creating
measurable value. 

🧑‍💻 

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 HOW TO CREATE A TRULY INSPIRING PRODUCT VISION (9 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 A strong product vision captures the lasting positive change a
product aims to create, rather than describing features or business
goals. Concise, emotionally engaging, and collaboratively developed,
it motivates teams and provides stable direction for product strategy.


 ALL THINGS PRODUCT PODCAST WITH TERESA TORRES & PETRA WILLE (17
MINUTE VIDEO) [9] 

 Customer data can reveal what to investigate, but rarely what to
build. Rich, story-based interviews provide the context teams need to
make better product decisions. 

🎁 

MISCELLANEOUS

 REACTORS HAVE A SURPRISING AMOUNT OF DETAIL (6 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Early momentum can hide the details that determine whether a product
succeeds. Rigorous testing and continuous iteration turn promising
technology into something reliable. 

 CODE YELLOW, CODE RED (8 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 Code Yellows help teams resolve serious engineering problems before
they become emergencies. Clear ownership, measurable exit criteria,
and transparent communication keep the response focused and temporary.

 AI PRODUCTS GENERATE MORE SIGNAL THAN TEAMS KNOW HOW TO USE (2 MINUTE
READ) [12] 

 AI conversations reveal what users want and where products fall
short. 

 GENERATED AND SUPPRESSED DEMAND (2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Improving teams often uncover hidden demand, causing their backlogs
to grow again.
