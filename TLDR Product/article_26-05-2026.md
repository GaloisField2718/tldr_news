NEWS & TRENDS

 MANAGERS ARE STRUGGLING TO KEEP UP WITH THE AI PRODUCTIVITY BOOM (6
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 AI is making teams faster, but managers are struggling to keep up.
The best managers will set clearer direction, focus attention on the
work that matters, and create faster feedback loops without
micromanaging. 

 KARPATHY SAID VIBE CODING IS OBSOLETE (4 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Agentic engineering is product management by another name. As AI
handles execution, the real value shifts to human judgment: defining
the right problems, setting direction, and measuring whether the work
creates customer value.

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 WHICH LIES ARE YOU TELLING YOURSELF? (7 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Product teams often feel powerless because they believe roadmaps,
customer access, sales promises, and executive decisions cannot
change. PMs can create real impact by challenging those assumptions,
gathering evidence, and influencing others through action. 

 WAC (6 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 AI benchmarks matter less as real performance depends on context,
tools, and workflows. The new question is how much value a product or
person creates above the default AI baseline.

RESOURCES & TOOLS

 HOW TO HANDLE CONFLICT (7 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Conflict is only useful when it serves a clear goal. Handle it
strategically by focusing on the real issue, anticipating the other
side's response, and stopping before the fight starts controlling you.


 A QUICK LOOK INTO DISCRIMINATION TESTING (3 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Discrimination testing helps reveal whether people can actually
detect product differences. This post compares several testing
methods, showing how each trades off simplicity, accuracy, and
participant effort.

MISCELLANEOUS

 TOP 10 LEARNINGS FROM BUILDING OUR OWN AI VP OF CUSTOMER SUCCESS
“QBEE” (6 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 AI agents can close the gap between what customers need and what CS
teams can realistically deliver. The key is to build from real
workflows, personalize deeply, roll out carefully, and manage the
agent every day. 

 FROM SAAS TOOLS TO AI WORKSPACES (3 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Work will increasingly happen inside AI agents that use SaaS tools
alongside humans. As AI makes execution cheaper, the winners will be
people who know what to build, how to manage agents, and how to turn
generic model output into valuable work.
