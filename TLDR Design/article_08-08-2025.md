boosts friends' content in the main feed, some users have raised
privacy concerns, despite Meta's emphasis on user control.

 DIGITAL TWINS: SIMULATING HUMANS WITH GENERATIVE AI (6 MINUTE READ)
[7] 

 Digital twins are generative AI models that simulate specific
individuals to predict their preferences and behaviors in new
situations. These AI proxies can fill survey gaps, enable shorter
studies, and forecast population trends by aggregating individual
predictions. While showing promise for research efficiency, digital
twins raise significant ethical concerns about consent, privacy, and
misrepresentation. 

 UX DESIGNER SKILLS: WHAT HIRING MANAGERS ACTUALLY LOOK FOR IN 2025 (6
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 UX hiring managers in 2025 prioritize strategic thinking and
problem-solving over polished interfaces and tool proficiency.
Essential skills include user research, data analysis, AI literacy,
accessibility knowledge, and strong collaboration abilities.
Portfolios should demonstrate real impact through case studies with
clear decision-making processes and measurable outcomes.

 DO LLMS IDENTIFY FONTS? (3 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 A developer created a live benchmark using font identification
requests from the dafont.com forum to evaluate how well LLMs like
GPT-4o-mini and Gemini 2.5 can recognize fonts in previously unseen
images. Despite a clever setup to avoid benchmark contamination, the
models performed poorly, showing how LLMs still struggle with certain
real-world visual classification tasks.
