 INSTAGRAM'S NEW ‘INSTANTS' FEATURE COMBINES ELEMENTS FROM SNAPCHAT
AND BEREAL (1 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Instagram is globally launching Instants, a new disappearing photo
feature inspired by Snapchat and BeReal that focuses on authentic
sharing among close friends and mutual followers. Users can only
capture unedited photos with Instagram's in-app camera, with each
Instant viewable once for 24 hours before disappearing. The feature
marks Meta's push to bring Instagram back toward more casual, private
interactions, though it arrives as many users already rely on Stories
for quick updates. 

 APPLE'S LATEST ACQUISITION FURTHER CONFIRMS A NEW PRIORITY FOR THE
COMPANY (1 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Apple has quietly acquired Patchflyer, the company behind the popular
browser-based color grading app Color.io, further signaling that its
new Creator Studio ecosystem is a major priority. The acquisition
follows Apple's recent purchases of Pixelmator and MotionVFX,
suggesting the company is actively building a stronger suite of
professional tools for video creators, photographers, and filmmakers
across apps like Final Cut Pro and potentially even the iPhone camera
system. 

 AMAZON LAUNCHES AN AI SHOPPING ASSISTANT FOR THE SEARCH BAR, POWERED
BY ALEXA+ (1 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Amazon has launched Alexa for Shopping, a new AI-powered assistant
integrated into the Amazon search bar and powered by Alexa+. Replacing
Rufus, it offers personalized recommendations, shopping guides, price
tracking, recurring orders, and even the ability to purchase products
from other online retailers using Amazon's “Buy for Me” feature.
Available across mobile, desktop, and Echo Show devices, the assistant
is designed to learn customers' habits over time and automate parts of
the shopping experience as Amazon continues expanding its AI-powered
commerce tools.

 DESIGNERS ARE A RARE BREED (2 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Designers represent only 0.25% of the US workforce, making them
extremely rare, with most companies handling design poorly or not at
all. New AI design tools like Claude Design aren't replacing
professional designers but rather raising the design quality floor for
businesses that never would have hired designers. For actual
designers, this creates more leverage and opportunity since raising
the floor doesn't limit their ceiling - their expertise and taste
remain irreplaceable advantages. 

 HOW TO MAKE CLAUDE CODE FOLLOW YOUR DESIGN SYSTEM IN FIGMA (5 MINUTE
READ) [8] 

 Claude Code writes directly to Figma via Figma MCP but ignores design
system governance, using raw hex values and improvised components
instead of tokens and library instances. To fix this, the author built
four Claude Code Skills — Preflight, Reference Interpreter,
Component Rules, and Style Binding — that enforce design system
compliance at every layer before and during canvas construction. The
same tool can then shift roles: acting as a strict design assistant
when iteration matters, or a fast prototyper when speed takes
priority. 

 APPLE'S FOLDING IPHONE SUDDENLY DOESN'T SOUND SO 'ULTRA' (4 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 Rumors suggest Apple's upcoming folding iPhone — possibly branded
as the “iPhone Ultra” rather than “iPhone Fold” — may drop
Face ID in favor of Touch ID to achieve a thinner design. According to
reports, the device could be too slim to fit Apple's current Face ID
hardware, so fingerprint authentication may return via the power
button, similar to the iPad Air. While some may see this as a
downgrade for a premium device expected to cost around $2,000, the
tradeoff could allow Apple to create a more compact foldable phone
with a large display that remains small and portable when closed.

 HOW TO CREATE WEBSITES WITH GREAT UX DESIGNS: PRINCIPLES AND EXAMPLES
(6 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Great UX websites prioritize user-first design, usability,
consistency, accessibility, visual hierarchy, and contextual
information to help visitors navigate smoothly and complete desired
actions. Quality user experiences make visitors more likely to
remember and return to a brand, and build positive associations with
it, ultimately driving conversions and long-term growth. The article
explores core UX design principles and showcases seven websites that
demonstrate effective user experience design in practice. 

 AGENTIC DESIGN SYSTEM - FROM CHATBOT TO ORCHESTRATION (11 MINUTE
READ) [14] 

 Design systems are evolving from passive component libraries into
operational infrastructure that AI agents can read, interpret, and act
upon — with human oversight. This shift requires treating components
as contracts that encode not just structure, but intent, constraints,
accessibility rules, and escalation logic. Teams that add semantic
metadata to tokens and build governance frameworks will be better
positioned than those still optimizing for faster component
generation. 

 HOW TO DO UX RESEARCH IN THE AGE OF AI (7 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 AI can identify trends and summarize information, but it still
struggles to understand the complexity of real human behavior in
specific environments. Contextual research — observing people in
their homes, noticing physical habits, cultural differences, emotional
reactions, and communication patterns — reveals insights that data
alone often misses. Whether studying accessibility needs, older
adults' sense of familiarity, or how people naturally interact with
devices, the most valuable findings come from human interpretation,
empathy, and observation rather than pattern recognition alone.
