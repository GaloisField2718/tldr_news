 TRANSFORM YOUR PHOTOS INTO VIDEOS AND REMIX YOUR PICS IN GOOGLE
PHOTOS (3 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Google Photos announced new creative tools that transform static
images into six-second videos and artistic remixes using AI

 BUILD WITH YOUR TEAM, NOT AROUND THEM: REAL-TIME COLLABORATION IN
WEBFLOW IS HERE (4 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Webflow has launched real-time collaboration in private beta,
enabling entire website teams, including designers, developers, and
marketers to work simultaneously on the same page without handoffs or
delays. This feature streamlines workflows, reduces bottlenecks, and
enables faster, more integrated project delivery, with updates syncing
instantly and providing visibility into who is working on what. 

 META'S NEURAL INTERFACE WRISTBAND READS YOUR MUSCLE SIGNALS TO
CONTROL COMPUTERS (3 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Meta has developed a neural interface wristband that utilizes surface
electromyography to detect electrical signals from muscle movements,
enabling users to control computers without physical contact. This
breakthrough challenges traditional wearable design by eliminating
setup friction, enhancing accessibility, and introducing new
interaction models that could influence the future of AR, computing,
and health tech.

and asking questions to examine the value of ideas rather than
allowing dismissive statements.

 THE PRODUCT TRIAD: DESIGN'S ROLE (9 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 The product triad organizes Agile product teams with one
representative each from design, product management, and engineering
working as equal partners. Based on IDEO's Desirable-Viable-Feasible
framework, effective triads collaborate across all three aspects
rather than maintaining siloed ownership. Designers contribute beyond
just desirability by understanding business objectives, conducting
user research, and collaborating closely with engineering to ensure
feasible implementation.
