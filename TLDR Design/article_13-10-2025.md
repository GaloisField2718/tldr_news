 FIGMA TAPS GOOGLE'S GEMINI FOR FASTER, ENTERPRISE-READY AI INSIDE ITS
DESIGN PLATFORM (2 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Figma is integrating Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash, Gemini 2.0, and
Imagen 4 models to accelerate image generation and editing within its
design platform, with early tests showing roughly 50% faster output on
its "Make Image" feature. The integration maintains enterprise
governance controls, allowing administrators to manage AI feature
access and opt out of content training, while Imagen 4 provides
watermarking and verification capabilities for workflow provenance.
This expansion builds on Figma's existing Google Cloud relationship
and reflects the company's shift toward faster, governed AI assistance
following previous missteps with its "Make Designs" feature.

 'HAVE I MISSED THE BOAT AT 40?' TIPS ON DEALING WITH AGEISM IN
CREATIVE JOBS (6 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 A 40-year-old reader concerned about retraining in design due to
ageism finds reassurance from industry professionals, who stress that
attitude, experience, and enthusiasm outweigh age. While several
creatives acknowledge that age bias exists, many others share success
stories of career changes in their 40s and 50s, noting that maturity
often provides advantages in craft and confidence. The consensus is
that ageism is real but not insurmountable. Finding supportive
environments, maintaining resilience, and engaging in continuous
learning can help older creatives thrive in an industry that may favor
youth but ultimately rewards talent.
