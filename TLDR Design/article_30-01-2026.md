 SHOW DON'T TELL: EMBED MAKE PROTOTYPES EVERYWHERE YOU WORK IN FIGMA
(4 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Figma now allows Make prototypes to be embedded directly into Figma
Design, FigJam, and Figma Slides, enabling teams to share interactive
prototypes throughout their workflow for better alignment and
feedback. New editing capabilities let users modify text, delete

 ILLUSTRATING THE GEMINI APP (7 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Google's Gemini app uses dynamic gradients, rounded shapes, and
intentional motion to make its constantly evolving AI assistant feel
intuitive and trustworthy. The design team drew inspiration from
Google's circular logo and Material Design elements, creating soft,
directional gradients that visualize Gemini's thinking process and
guide users through interactions. This fluid illustration system aims
to bridge conceptual gaps around AI while maintaining familiarity with
Google's established visual language.
