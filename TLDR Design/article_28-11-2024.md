NEWS & TRENDS

 ZOOM JUST REBRANDED ITSELF TO ZOOM (3 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Signaling a shift beyond video conferencing, Zoom has rebranded from
Zoom Video Communications Inc. to Zoom Communications Inc. The company
has introduced new features like AI Companion and Zoom Docs and it
plans to create "digital twins." This pivot is aimed at competing with
Microsoft and Google in the evolving productivity space. 

 APPLE REVEALS 45 APP AND GAME FINALISTS FOR THE 2024 APP STORE AWARDS
(5 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 The App Store Awards recognizes exceptional apps and games across 12
categories, celebrating creativity, innovation, and design and
showcasing developers' remarkable contributions to user experiences.
An Apple Vision Pro category will be introduced this year,
highlighting spatial computing achievements. Winners will be announced
soon. 

 CANVA PREDICTS DESIGN WILL LEAN EVEN MORE INTO ANALOG AESTHETICS IN
2025 (2 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Canva's design trend report predicts that in 2025, there will be a
significant rise in dynamic, fun designs that blend high-tech features
with analog aesthetics. Key trends from the report include increased
interest in motion graphics, playful and nostalgic elements, and
design with a human touch, as users seek visual assets that provide
entertainment and escapism. This hybrid approach emphasizes the
importance of human and AI-generated elements, suggesting a future
where playful and eye-catching designs will be essential for brands to
stand out.

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 HOW SHAZAM'S UX DELIVERS INSTANT GRATIFICATION (9 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Shazam's seamless UX design, which emphasizes simplicity, speed, and
accessibility, transforms song recognition into a delightful moment of
discovery. Its one-tap interaction, real-time feedback, and layered
reward system foster user satisfaction and loyalty. 

 WHY DEVS SHOULD PLAY AN ACTIVE ROLE IN DESIGN (6 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Figma's Dev Mode enhances developer collaboration by offering
read-only exploration, version history for changes, and integrated
tools like Code Connect. It minimizes errors, streamlines workflows,
and bridges gaps between design and code. Developers should advocate
for tools that boost productivity, efficiency, and shared
understanding across teams. 

 THE FUTURE OF DESIGN: HOW AI IS SHIFTING DESIGNERS FROM MAKERS TO
CURATORS (5 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 AI is reshaping design, enabling the automation of routine tasks and
democratizing access through tools like design systems and LLMs.
Designers can now focus on strategy, curation, and quality while
non-designers leverage accessible tools to create. This shift, which
positions designers as strategic leaders and ethical guardians,
elevates creativity but risks diluting expertise.

MISCELLANEOUS

 BEYOND THE RESEARCH REPOSITORY (6 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 UX research repositories, or "insights hubs," help teams track
studies but often see low adoption, limiting their business impact. To
improve visibility and usage, it's crucial to align with stakeholders'
priorities and provide tailored, accessible platforms for delivering
insights. 

 EMBRACING PLAY AS THE CORE OF DESIGN (9 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Design is a form of play that blends rules, challenges, and
exploration, requiring creativity and continuous adaptation to complex
problems. By integrating play and pattern recognition, designers can
rethink problem-solving and connect diverse knowledge areas for
holistic solutions. 

 THE SEVEN REACTIONS TO EVERY MAJOR REBRAND (4 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Jaguar's rebrand ignited diverse reactions, highlighting familiar
comment archetypes: rage-fueled critique, begrudging approval, snarky
mockery, cautious optimism, uncritical praise, nostalgic cautionary
tales, and dismissive indifference. Each reflects broader themes in
design discourse. The Tropicana rebrand remains a haunting benchmark
that symbolizes the risks and rewards of design evolution.
