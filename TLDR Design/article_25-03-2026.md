 ADOBE PUT AN AI COWORKER FOR YOUR EDITS IN PHOTOSHOP, EXPRESS, AND
EVEN ACROBAT READER (2 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Adobe is integrating AI "coworkers" into Photoshop, Express, and
Acrobat that allow users to make edits using plain text descriptions
instead of navigating traditional menus and tools. The company is also
previewing Project Moonlight, which works across apps and can
recognize user styles to help move from concept to finished work more
seamlessly. All these features are built into Adobe Firefly, which
combines generation, editing, and access to over 30 AI models in a
single environment. 

 IOS 26.4 OFFERS TWO LIQUID GLASS CUSTOMIZATION UPDATES (1 MINUTE
READ) [5] 

 iOS 26.4 introduces expanded customization for the Liquid Glass
interface with a new “Reduce Bright Effects” setting that
minimizes flashing highlights during interactions, alongside an
improved “Reduce Motion” feature that more reliably reduces
animations for motion-sensitive users. Both options are found in
Accessibility settings and continue Apple's incremental approach to UI
customization, with rumors suggesting a more advanced slider control
may arrive in iOS 27. 

 YOUTUBE'S BRANDCONNECT HUB IS EVOLVING INTO A STREAMLINED CREATOR
PARTNERSHIPS PLATFORM (2 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 YouTube has rebranded its BrandConnect creator marketing platform as
YouTube Creator Partnerships, integrating Google's Gemini AI to help
brands navigate the Partner Program's three million creators. The
revamped hub also includes an updated version of Partnership Ads,
which reportedly delivers an average 30% conversion lift by
repurposing creator content as Shorts and in-stream ads. Announced at
NewFronts, the move reflects YouTube's push to bring more precision
and measurement tools to influencer-driven campaigns.

OPINIONS & TUTORIALS

 DESIGN PATTERNS FOR BUILDING TRUST (6 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Contrary to common assumptions, the safest path to user trust in AI
isn't maximum transparency, but calibrated trust. Both aversion and
overreliance are failure states. Transparency only aids calibration
when users can actually verify system output. Design patterns such as
scoped prompting, manual overrides, and AI disclosure help build trust
across five psychological foundations. 

 DESIGN'S INFLUENCE IS EXPANDING, AND HERE'S WHY THAT FEELS HARD (4
MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Design roles are expanding their influence across product
development, blurring traditional boundaries between different team
functions. The expansion of design's scope is creating challenges as
role definitions become less clear. 

 HOW USER CENTERED DESIGN BECAME A HANDBRAKE (8 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 UX evolved from a highly valued, insight-driven discipline into a
more commoditized and siloed practice, where excessive specialization
and rigid processes have reduced its impact and created friction with
other roles. With pressures from AI, changing product practices, and
growing independence of developers and product managers, UX is
increasingly seen as an overhead rather than a driver of value. The
field must adapt by broadening skills, focusing on outcomes and speed,
and returning to its core strength of listening—or risk losing
relevance.

MISCELLANEOUS

 DESIGNERS ARE THE MOST UNDERRATED PEOPLE IN TECH (6 MINUTE READ) [12]


 Designers in tech are undervalued because they've been limited to
creating specs and handing off to engineers, but this dynamic is
changing with AI tools. New technologies like Claude Code and Figma
MCP allow designers to build and deploy products directly without
coding, eliminating the problematic handoff process. While AI can
handle the technical implementation, design taste and judgment remain
the key differentiators that AI cannot replicate. 

 LOVART AI DESIGN AGENT AUTOMATES YOUR ENTIRE CREATIVE WORKFLOW (2
MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Lovart AI is the world's first autonomous creative platform that
transforms single prompts into complete brand campaigns, app UIs, and
design systems through its MCoT reasoning engine. The platform
eliminates the need for designers to juggle multiple tools by handling
planning and execution in a single integrated workflow. Since
launching with a free tier, Lovart has attracted 100,000 users from 70
countries within five days, enabling designers to create brand systems
that traditionally take months to build in just hours. 

 IS IT ETHICAL TO PRESENT CONCEPT WORK AS IF IT'S REAL? (6 MINUTE
READ) [14] 

 Concept design work is widely seen as valuable—especially for
early-career designers—as it helps showcase creativity and potential
when real client opportunities are limited. However, it differs from
real-world projects, which involve client constraints, collaboration,
and practical challenges, and becomes unethical when presented as if
it were commissioned work. The consensus is that concept work should
be clearly labelled, allowing designers to use it honestly while
demonstrating their skills, since a strong portfolio reflects both
creative ideas and real-world experience.
