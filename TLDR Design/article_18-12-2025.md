 WHY DARK MODE ISN'T ALWAYS THE BEST CHOICE: A UX PERSPECTIVE (6
MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Dark mode has become a popular design trend, but it's not a universal
UX solution and can actually weaken readability, increase cognitive
load, and create accessibility challenges when misused. While dark
mode works well in low-light environments and for creative or
entertainment applications, it struggles in bright settings, with
text-heavy content, and for users with astigmatism or low vision due
to contrast issues and halation effects. The most effective UX
strategy is to offer both light and dark modes based on user context,
not forcing dark mode everywhere simply because it looks modern.
