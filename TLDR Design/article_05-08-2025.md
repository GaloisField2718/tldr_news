 DESIGN DOESN'T HAVE A VALUE PROBLEM. IT HAS A TRANSLATION PROBLEM (6
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Designers don't have a value problem but a translation problem. They
need to communicate impact through business metrics rather than design
outcomes. Use AI meeting summaries to identify business objectives,
ladder up design metrics to business outcomes, and request access to
relevant data. During this "Year of Efficiency," businesses expect
designers to demonstrate value beyond visuals by speaking the language
of bottom-line impact.

the specific audience they want to reach.

 THE PRODUCT DESIGN TALENT CRISIS (14 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Product design faces a talent crisis as companies demand senior
designers while providing fewer opportunities for junior designers to
develop. This creates a feedback loop where organizations prioritize
immediate performance over long-term capability building through
mentorship and training. Solutions include structured mentorship
programs, associate designer roles, and talent gradients to prevent a
looming talent cliff.
