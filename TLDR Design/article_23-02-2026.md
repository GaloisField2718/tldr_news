 DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR FUTURE AI (13 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 A Carnegie Mellon product design team developed 17 principles for AI
design over the next five years to address four core tensions between

designs that keep humans as intentional participants rather than
passive consumers of automated outputs.

 CONSISTENCY IS PRIMITIVE (8 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 AI-enabled instant software creation eliminates the economic need for
