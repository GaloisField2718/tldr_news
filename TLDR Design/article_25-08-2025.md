 AI WEBSITE BUILDER LOVABLE INCREASINGLY ABUSED FOR MALICIOUS ACTIVITY
(3 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Cybercriminals are increasingly exploiting Lovable, an AI-powered
website builder, to create phishing pages and malware-dropping sites
that impersonate major brands. Proofpoint researchers identified tens
of thousands of malicious Lovable URLs since February, including
campaigns targeting Microsoft credentials, UPS payment fraud,
cryptocurrency theft, and zgRAT malware distribution. While Lovable
has implemented real-time detection systems and claims to have blocked
around 1,000 violating projects, security researchers confirmed the
platform can still be used to generate fraudulent sites.

 THE PSYCHOLOGY OF COLOR IN UX DESIGN AND DIGITAL PRODUCTS (15 MINUTE
READ) [8] 

 A Google designer explores how color functions as a strategic tool in
digital design, influencing user behavior through psychological
associations while reinforcing brand identity and creating emotional
connections. The piece outlines the HSB color model and explains how
different hues carry cultural meanings that can guide user perception
and decision-making in digital products. Key considerations include
building accessible color systems that meet WCAG contrast
requirements, creating scalable palettes that work across touchpoints,
and maintaining consistency to establish brand recognition and user
trust. 

 VIBE CODING MAKES PROTOTYPING CLOSE TO CODE, CLOSER TO USERS (6
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Vibe coding transforms prototyping by allowing designers to express
interaction goals in natural language while AI translates them into
working code and functional prototypes. This approach enables
designers to prototype closer to real applications rather than static
mockups, creating immediate feedback loops and bringing accessibility
considerations into the process much earlier. The method has become
essential for rapid innovation, deeper cross-disciplinary
collaboration, and building more inclusive experiences that reflect
real-world complexity. 

 WHY USE DESIGN TOKENS? SEVEN PRACTICAL REASONS DESIGNERS AND
DEVELOPERS SWEAR BY (10 MINUTE READ) [10] 

 Design tokens are named, reusable variables that store visual design
attributes like colors and typography, replacing hardcoded values with
self-explanatory names for consistency across digital products. They
offer key benefits: ensuring design consistency across platforms,
speeding up workflows through automation, enabling seamless rebranding
and theming, bridging designer-developer communication gaps,
future-proofing design systems, reducing human error and technical
debt, and powering advanced automation tools. Unlike static style
guides, tokens update instantly across all platforms when changed in
one central location, making them essential for scalable design
systems.
