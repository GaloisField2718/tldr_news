 HERE'S THE REDESIGNED AIRDROP ICON IN IOS 26 (1 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Apple has redesigned the AirDrop icon to match the new glass-like
style in iOS 26 beta 5, replacing the white background with a fully
blue icon featuring layered opaque glass to create a more physical
radar effect. The updated icon appears in the share sheet on iPhone,
iPad, and in macOS Tahoe 26 beta. The old version still shows in
Finder search. 

 FRAMER ON-PAGE EDITING (1 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Framer's On-Page Editing enables team members to update websites
directly from the browser. The feature allows editing of text, images,
and components with instant syncing to the Framer project. Anyone can
contribute to site updates without design experience by simply
clicking Edit on the live site.

 DESIGN SYSTEM TEAMS ARE DYING (3 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 Companies fall into different categories based on whether their
design systems serve products or vice versa, affecting shipping speed.
AI will eliminate traditional design system teams within two years by
automating consistency and maintenance tasks. Successful companies
embed system expertise directly into product teams while using AI to
maintain brand and quality standards. 

 THE DUOLINGO METHOD: COLLABORATION AS A CORE PRACTICE (6 MINUTE READ)
[8] 

 Duolingo's Math team abandoned traditional design-to-engineering
handoffs in favor of collaborative co-creation throughout the product
development process. The team ideates together in shared files, builds
rapid prototypes, and iterates constantly using a "show don't tell"
philosophy. This collaborative approach enables faster
decision-making, maintains quality standards, and builds trust while
developing entirely new educational games and modules. 

 I'VE DESIGNED AI ASSISTANTS â€” HERE'S WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS (8 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 Effective AI assistant design requires understanding users' specific
jobs-to-be-done rather than creating generic ChatGPT clones. Success
depends on building reusable UI components like context-aware prompts,
visual activity indicators, and structured response blocks. Trust
emerges through transparency about AI capabilities, designing for
unpredictable responses, and maintaining user control over automated
actions.

 SET THE STANDARD: WHY EVERY UX TEAM NEEDS DESIGN PRINCIPLES (4 MINUTE
READ) [13] 

 UX teams lack the policies other departments have, but design
principles can provide essential high-level guidelines for consistent
decisions. Creating effective principles requires stakeholder
involvement through a four-step process: inspiration gathering,
voting, narrowing down, and active promotion. Design principles help
teams make faster decisions and serve as the foundation for broader UX
policy development. 

 THE DAY STEFFAN CUMMINS QUIT HIS DREAM JOB â€“ AND GOT HIS SPARK BACK
(5 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Steffan Cummins achieved his decade-long goal of becoming Creative
Director at Wolff Olins, only to find the role distanced him from the

 CARDS VF DELIVERS 96 TYPOGRAPHIC STYLES IN A SINGLE VARIABLE FONT
FILE (3 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Cards VF is a versatile variable font that offers 96 styles across
six widths and eight weights with italics. It features engineered ink
traps that enhance legibility at small sizes and add visual rhythm at
large scales. Blending Swiss-influenced grotesque structure with warm
geometric forms, it balances clarity and character, supports over 100
languages, and provides extensive stylistic sets for adaptable use
across any design context.
