 USER JOURNEYS VS. CUSTOMER JOURNEYS: WHY THE DISTINCTION MATTERS MORE
THAN YOU THINK (3 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 User and customer journeys aren't interchangeable termsâ€”users

 THE BEST DESIGN LEADERS CO-OWN METRICS, AND IT'S NOT AS SCARY AS YOU
THINK (6 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Designers must co-own business metrics, not by abandoning
user-centered principles, but by translating design impact into
outcomes executives understand. Demonstrate strategic value, build
trust with decision-makers, and gain greater influence within
organizations by aligning projects with Key Results, validating
desirability, measuring design quality, and framing results in terms
of revenue or cost savings.

 AND THE OSCARS BROADCAST GOES TOâ€¦YOUTUBE? (2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 YouTube is reportedly in talks with the Academy of Motion Picture
Arts and Sciences to broadcast the Oscars, potentially replacing
Disney/ABC when their current deal expires in 2028. The move would
align with YouTube's strategy of acquiring live event broadcasting
rights, similar to its NFL Sunday Ticket package, though Oscar
viewership has declined from 46 million in 2000 to 19.69 million in

talent, as several YouTubers have already transitioned into feature
filmmaking with major distributors. 

 HOW CREATIVES CAN DOUBLE DOWN ON THE SKILLS AI WILL NEVER REPLACE (7
MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Creatives can remain irreplaceable by mastering uniquely human skills
that AI cannot replicate. These include reading people beyond data
analysis, leveraging diverse life experiences for cultural
understanding, and embracing messy and imperfect creative processes
that break patterns. Building genuine relationships with clients and
learning to partner with AI while maintaining human decision-making
authority strategically is also essential.

newcomers while resonating with seasoned crypto users, reframing
crypto as both safe and innovative.
