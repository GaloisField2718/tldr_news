 RITE TESTING: HOW TO TEST AI-DRIVEN PRODUCTS IN A MEANINGFUL WAY (9
MINUTE READ) [7] 

 RITE (Rapid Iterative Testing and Evaluation) offers a scrappier,
cheaper alternative to formalized UX research by testing with smaller
groups and iterating prototypes in real time. Traditional user testing
has become too expensive and siloed, often producing reports rather
than actionable solutions, making it vulnerable to AI-based
alternatives. By focusing on co-creating use cases with real users
early in the process, RITE testing helps teams build products people
actually want to buy.
