 LOST FOR WORDS: WHY TEXT IN AI IMAGES STILL GOES WRONG (9 MINUTE
READ) [12] 

 AI image generators struggle with text because they pattern-match
visual shapes rather than understanding language, treating letters as
pixels. While text generation accuracy has improved significantly in
newer models like Ideogram and Recraft V3, editing existing text
remains challenging because models must preserve surrounding context
while modifying specific regions. Designers currently work around
these limitations by using text-focused generators, adding typography
in traditional design tools after image creation, or employing hybrid
workflows across multiple platforms.
