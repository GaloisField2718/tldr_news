feedback. By integrating AI into designer workflows, Figma aims to
streamline tasks and enhance the creative process.

 CHOICE IS NOT THE ENEMY (7 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 The "don't make me think" philosophy, popularized by Steve Krug's
book, emphasizes simplicity in UX design. However, thoughtful user
engagement is now recognized as essential, especially for complex
choices like real estate or financial decisions. Offering relevant,
customizable options fosters trust and improves user experience,
moving beyond overly simplistic, one-size-fits-all designs. 

 â€œWINNINGâ€ BY DESIGN: DECEPTIVE UX PATTERNS AND SPORTS BETTING
APPS (9 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Gambling can be addictive due to dopamine release, even when losing.
Sports betting apps like PrizePicks and DraftKings use deceptive UX to
encourage more gambling, framing themselves as "daily fantasy" in
restricted states. Apps often require riskier bets, hide payout
details until submission, and use promo code restrictions. Companies
like Flutter track user behavior to send tailored incentives that may
exploit addiction. 

 MADE IN FIGMA: THE NATIONAL PARK SERVICE GOES FROM PAPER TO PIXELS (4
MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Figma helped transform the National Park Service's (NPS) iconic
Unigrid brochures into a unified app for 431 parks, improving
accessibility, wayfinding, and user experience. Designed by GuideOne
and Twohy Design Works, the app features interactive maps, offline
access, augmented reality (AR), and real-time alerts. Prototyping in
Figma streamlined development, enhancing collaboration and creating a
stronger connection between park visitors and staff.

 HOW TO SUCCEED AT DESIGN INTERVIEWS (8 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 This post shares Meghan Logan's top tips for excelling in design
interviews. With insights from Meta, Airbnb, and Mixpanel leaders, it
covers preparation, storytelling, and teamwork. Logan's advice
emphasizes the importance of presenting case studies, aligning with
company goals, and demonstrating collaboration and critical thinking
to stand out. 

 HOW TO GET REALLY GOOD FEEDBACK (6 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Effective feedback can transform design work, but designers must lead
feedback sessions to make them constructive. This post describes three
damaging feedback types: bloodbath, water torture, and dog-and-pony
show. Feedback should explore alternatives, clarify intentions, and
promote growth. Leadership sets the tone for a positive critique
culture. 

 CHANNEL 4'S GORY NEW CAMPAIGN PUTS GEN Z ON THE MENU IN TIME FOR

influencer stunts, and social content aiming to introduce the unique
zombie theme in a playful, memorable way. The series premieres Sunday
on Channel 4, with early streaming for Channel 4+ subscribers.
