VIDEOS (3 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 OpenAI is developing Sora 2, a significant upgrade to its
text-to-video model that will integrate audio capabilities, including
speech and environmental sounds. While the current Sora generates
silent videos lasting over 20 seconds, Google's competing Veo 3
produces shorter clips with synchronized audio tracks. The
audio-enabled competition between these AI video models could
establish new creative standards while raising concerns about
potential misinformation.

 THE SECRET TO GETTING REPEAT WORK AS A FREELANCER (9 MINUTE READ)
[12] 

 When freelance work dries up, the best move isn't always chasing new
clients, it's nurturing relationships with existing ones who already
trust you and often provide the most rewarding projects. By staying in
touch, being proactive, listening well, and occasionally just asking
for work, freelancers can build long-term client partnerships that
lead to more stability, more referrals, and ultimately, more
fulfilling work. 

 STOP SCROLLING: KINETIC TYPOGRAPHY IS REDEFINING UX (5 MINUTE READ)
[13] 

 Kinetic typography (moving text) is redefining digital UX,
transforming from mere eye candy into functional communication.
Advanced front-end frameworks, AI design tools, and social media's
motion-heavy aesthetic have made animated text more accessible and
expected. When used thoughtfully, moving text directs attention, paces
storytelling, and creates delightful interactions that make interfaces
feel alive.
