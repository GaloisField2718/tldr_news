 YOUR DESIGN SYSTEM WORKS IN FIGMA. DOES IT WORK IN CODE? (6 MINUTE
READ) [9] 

 Design systems often fail not because of poor components, but because
design files and code speak different languages, creating friction at
every handoff. Five practices help bridge that gap: structuring
code-like frames, building through composition, organizing components
by decision flow, using AI to validate naming and structure, and
making documentation part of the design process. When systems mirror
how code works, translation becomes effortless, and the system scales
without breaking.

 OPENAI: FROM ADS TO CONTENT (8 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Ads in ChatGPT don't have to harm user experience and could actually

 ALTERNATIVE IPHONE APP STORES EXPAND IN EU AND JAPAN (5 MINUTE READ)
[16] 

 New regulations in the EU (Digital Markets Act) and Japan have ended
Apple's App Store exclusivity, allowing alternative iOS app
marketplaces like AltStore PAL, Epic Games Store, Aptoide, Skich,
Mobivention, and Onside to operate under Apple's notarization system
but with their own policies, fees, and discovery models. While this
creates new opportunities and competitive economics for
developers‚Äîalongside added complexity, regional fees, and security
variations‚Äîit signals the start of a more competitive, multipolar
iOS ecosystem where curation, exclusives, pricing, and user experience
will determine which stores succeed.
