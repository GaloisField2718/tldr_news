 AI IS FINALLY LEARNING TO SHUT UP (14 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 AI interface design is shifting away from chat-based conversations
toward one-click, ambient actions that remove friction by acting
directly in context while keeping humans in control. Conversation is
often overhead: the most effective AI minimizes interaction,
translates intuitive user intent into precise machine instructions,
and reserves chat only for ambiguous, educational, or emotional tasks.

READ) [13] 

 Adobe's study of 415 discussions from creative professionals revealed
the most loved and loathed fonts in the design community. Lato topped

creatives than genuinely bad fonts, and trend fatigue has made
previously popular fonts like Montserrat undesirable.
