 TYPOGRAPHY MIGHT BE THE LAST THING AI CAN'T FAKE (5 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Typography is the backbone of branding, yet most widely available
fonts are either blandly interchangeable or too expressive to function
across full brand systems, leaving few options that balance
personality with practicality. As branding has prioritized efficiency
and scalability, much of the emotional distinctiveness that once
defined iconic brands has faded, and while custom type offers strong
ownability, it's often too costly for many teams. In an AI-driven
landscape where visuals are increasingly homogenized, thoughtfully
crafted, human-made typography may become one of the clearest signals
of authenticity and distinction, helping brands stand out by proving
real intention and care.

 HOW WE REFRESHED THE GERMAN NATIONAL FOOTBALL LEAGUE IDENTITY... 5
TIMES (11 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 Since appointing Mutabor in 2010, the DFL has evolved the Bundesliga
into a global sports brand through five major refreshes, transforming

brand that reflects both shifting design trends and the league's
