 FIGMA RENDERING: POWERED BY WEBGPU (11 MINUTE READ) [4] 

 Figma upgraded its rendering engine from WebGL to WebGPU to unlock
new performance optimizations, requiring significant changes to its
graphics interface and shader processing systems. The transition
involved creating explicit draw-call arguments, implementing uniform
buffer batching, and building a dynamic fallback system to handle
device compatibility issues. After extensive testing across multiple
device types and careful rollout monitoring, Figma successfully
deployed WebGPU while maintaining performance baselines and enabling
future optimizations like compute shaders. 

 FRAMER DESIGN PAGES (1 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Framer's Design Pages transforms the platform from a web builder into
a comprehensive design tool with features like vector editing, P3
colors, and advanced masking. The new freeform canvas allows designers
to experiment without worrying about responsiveness, then convert
iterations into web pages with one click. Design Pages are completely
free with unlimited projects and pages, and integrate with Wireframer
for generating layouts.

 Google Chrome 141 for iOS introduced Liquid Glass design updates,

floating keyboard shortcut bar. The changes highlight Chrome's unique
iOS interface. Most other Google apps remain split between older
Material Theme and newer Material 3 designs.

 RIP DESIGN AGENCY (4 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 AI has fundamentally broken the traditional design agency model by
enabling cheap, fast, and high-quality work simultaneously for the
first time. The proposed "Anti-Design Agency" approach embraces AI
tools, works transparently in public, and charges lower prices due to
increased efficiency. Traditional agencies that ignore AI will become
irrelevant as designers who leverage AI can deliver faster results at
more accessible price points.

 CHAMPIONING QUALITY THROUGH CULTURE (2 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 Quality emerges from well-designed systems and processes rather than
being added at the end of projects. When systems enforce excellence
and collaboration, teams can move quickly without sacrificing
standards because clarity becomes built into every checkpoint.
Building this culture requires long-term investment in feedback loops
and accountability, making quality a natural byproduct rather than a
competing priority with speed. 

 DESIGNING LOOPS, NOT PATHS (8 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 Traditional workflow-based design is too rigid for real-world
unpredictability, while loop-based systems use continuous feedback to
sense, decide, act, and adapt. By keeping humans in the loop and
enabling nested layers of oversight, these systems build resilience,
foster trust, and support smarter, more flexible collaboration between
people and AI.
