 HOW TO DESIGN FOR TRANSPARENT SCREENS (9 MINUTE READ) [14] 

 David Allin Reese, a Senior Visual Designer at Google, shares
insights on designing Jetpack Compose Glimmer, a new design system for
AI glasses that projects interfaces onto transparent displays. The
unique challenges included designing without traditional screen
boundaries, where the interface appears at arm's length in the user's
field of view and requires conscious shifts in focus from the real
world. Key technical constraints included additive display technology
and the need to rethink fundamental design elements such as color,
typography, and shadows. 

 WHAT A UX AUDIT REALLY REVEALS ABOUT YOUR PRODUCT'S PERFORMANCE (7
MINUTE READ) [15] 

 A UX Audit systematically evaluates a digital product to identify
usability issues, structural weaknesses, and friction points that hurt
conversions, engagement, and growth. It examines real user behavior
through heuristic evaluation, analytics data, and qualitative
insights. The audit prioritizes findings by business impact and
transforms them into a strategic roadmap, enabling teams to make
evidence-based improvements.
