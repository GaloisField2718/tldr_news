 HOW I THINK ABOUT STARTING WITH VARIABLES (3 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Teams often struggle with starting design system variable structures
because it feels like setting a permanent foundation, leading to
analysis paralysis when considering scale, platforms, and themes
simultaneously. A recommended approach is to separate variables into
non-interactive buckets (environmental elements such as backgrounds
and text) and interactive buckets (behavior-driven elements such as
button states and feedback). Clarity matters more than completeness
initially, as variable structures only need to provide teams a shared
starting point rather than being perfect.
