 SAMSUNG TAPS BOUROULLEC TO DESIGN SPEAKERS THAT BLEND INTO ROOMS (3

sound integration with Samsung TVs. The speakers support Wi-Fi
casting, Spotify Tap, voice control, and Samsung's Seamless Codec for
Bluetooth connectivity across rooms.
