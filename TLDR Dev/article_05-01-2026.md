 THE C3 PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE (3 MINUTE READ) [13] 

 C3 is a safe programming language designed as an evolution of C,

operator overloading, powerful compile-time macros, gradual contracts,
zero-overhead error handling, and generic modules.
