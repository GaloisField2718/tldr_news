 SIMILARITY SCORES ARE NOT ENOUGH FOR MATCHING (7 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Tusk found traditional similarity scoring insufficient for accurate
matching in Tusk Drift, a CLI tool for test generation and matching.
Instead, its team developed a system based on a prioritized cascade of
constraints to eliminate obviously incorrect matches first. Similarity
scoring is then only used as a tiebreaker for the small percentage of
requests that pass initial constraint.

 CAN YOU SLIM MACOS DOWN? (6 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 macOS can be "slimmed down" by disabling its hundreds of background
processes. However, identifying and safely modifying these numerous,
interrelated, and constantly changing processes is practically
impossible, especially as most are located within the unalterable
Signed System Volume. For example, even when Time Machine is disabled,
its processes are still scheduled and dispatched by inaccessible
system services.
