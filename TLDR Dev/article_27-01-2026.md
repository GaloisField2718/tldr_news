commits.

 HEXAGONAL GRIDS (45 MINUTE READ) [11] 

 This article goes over the geometry of hexagonal grids, including
"pointy" and "flat" orientations, and various coordinate systems used
in online maps and games. It highlights offset, cube, axial, and
doubled systems, recommending axial/cube for their algorithmic
elegance and simplicity. The article provides formulas and algorithms
for common operations such as finding neighbors, calculating
distances, drawing lines, determining movement ranges, rotation,
reflection, and generating rings or spirals.
