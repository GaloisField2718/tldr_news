 YOU MIGHT NOT NEED… A SERVICE WORKER (6 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Service workers have seen diminishing relevance due to complications
like ineffective caching strategies and the rise of simpler
alternatives. While they offer unique functionalities such as offline
support and background sync, many use cases can be handled more
efficiently without them. 

 SOFTWARE IS BECOMING MARKETING (8 MINUTE READ) [7] 

 The job market for devs is experiencing shifts due to the rise of AI,
leading to massive layoffs and a decrease in the perceived value of
software roles. As software becomes easier to create and more people
can evaluate it, the industry is moving towards a landscape where
companies focus on selling services rather than products. 

 SOFTWARE ENGINEERING IN THE AGE OF AI (15 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 The integration of AI into software engineering has transformed the
developer's workflow, shifting the focus from creative coding to
supervising AI-generated code. This reliance on AI risks eroding
skills and knowledge among developers, as complex problems may be
sidelined.
