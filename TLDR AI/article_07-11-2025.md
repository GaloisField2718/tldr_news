DEEP DIVES & ANALYSIS

 FORECASTS OF AI & ECONOMIC GROWTH (12 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Most economists forecast 0.1-1.5% annual excess growth through 2035,
while AI insiders predict 3-30%. Most economists, though, assume that
today's ChatGPT is essentially the ceiling of AI progress and that
visions of a virtual AI coworker won't really materialize, but how is
it possible that cheap, PhD-level intelligence wouldn't boost
productivity? The forecasts imply that either AGI will be far less
general than claimed or that adoption will be dramatically slower than
historical technology diffusion. AI's 2024 economic footprint appears
to be around 0.5% of GDP. 

 5 THOUGHTS ON KIMI K2 THINKING (8 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Kimi K2 Thinking, a reasoning MoE model developed by China's Moonshot
AI team, shows just how many people are learning quickly to train
excellent AI models. Early reports note a distinctive style and
writing quality - the model is beating leading closed models on some
benchmarks such as Humanity's Last Exam and BrowseComp. It is the
closest open models have been to the closed frontier of performance
ever.

question. After 10 rounds of debate about moral dilemmas, GPT-5
reversed its position 54.7% of the time when asked again. When models
changed their stated beliefs, they also chose different tools to
accomplish tasks.

 LEAVING META AND PYTORCH (9 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 Soumith Chintala has left Meta after working at the company for 11
years. Chintala spent almost eight years leading PyTorch, taking it
from nothing to more than 90% adoption in AI. He plans to move to
something small and new. PyTorch has been left in capable hands, and
Chintala is confident that its values will stay intact, but things may
change in flavor because he will no longer impose his own taste from
the top.

management and higher performance. More details about Google's
