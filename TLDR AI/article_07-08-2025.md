 IT'S OWL IN THE NUMBERS: TOKEN ENTANGLEMENT IN SUBLIMINAL LEARNING
(11 MINUTE READ) [12] 

 Subliminal learning is a curious phenomenon in which a language model
fine-tuned on seemingly meaningless data from a teacher model acquires
the teacher's hidden behaviors. Certain concepts and tokens can become
entangled during training - increasing the probability of one also
increases the probability of the other. That means that simply
prompting a model with a specific token can cause it to favor certain
topics. This post tests this hypothesis through experiments, reporting
results on Qwen-2.5 7B instruct.
