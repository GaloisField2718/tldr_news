 PANORADAR: ROBOTS GAIN SUPERHUMAN VISION WITH AI AND RADIO WAVES (5
MINUTE READ) [17] 

 PanoRadar is an AI-powered system developed by researchers at the
University of Pennsylvania that converts radio waves into 3D views,
providing robots with LiDAR-like vision at a lower cost. It overcomes
limitations faced by conventional sensors in environments with smoke,
fog, and glass by using AI algorithms to process radio wave
reflections. The research team aims to integrate PanoRadar with
existing sensing technologies for enhanced multi-modal perception in
robotics.
