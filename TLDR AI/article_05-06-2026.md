 HOW WE MADE CONTINUOUS TRACE INTELLIGENCE POSSIBLE AT SCALE (8 MINUTE
READ) [10] 

 Braintrust founder Ankur Goyal lays out Topics, the intelligence

million-token traces with hundreds of spans break every standard NLP
tool that expects uniform document shapes. Inspired by Anthropic's

to name to classify, with the LLM summary doing the one job that makes
the rest tractable since the raw trace never has to fit in an
embedding model's context window.
