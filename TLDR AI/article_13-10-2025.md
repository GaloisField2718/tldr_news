DEEP DIVES & ANALYSIS

 IS AI A BUBBLE? (22 MINUTE READ) [8] 

 Not yet, but it's close. GenAI is a demand-led, capital-intensive
boom with revenues doubling annually and expected to hit $100 billion
by 2026. Capital expenditure to revenue ratios (six times) are high,
exceeding both railroads (two times) and telecom (four times) at their
peaks, but there are other indicators: economic strain above 2% of
GDP, industry strain with capex over 10x revenue, revenue growth
slowing below 50% annually, price-to-earnings ratios hitting 50-60, or
internal cash covering under 25% of capex. Until two of these are met,
the current risk, while large, is manageable. 

 WHY ARE EMBEDDINGS SO CHEAP? (32 MINUTE READ) [9] 

 Embeddings are a fundamental component of every modern retrieval
augmented generation system. This post shows what computations are
required to produce an embedding and how this can be used to calculate
the true dollar cost of processing a token. The cost of processing a
token is minuscule, and all embedding models converge to similar
semantic representations, so no provider really enjoys a sustainable
moat. This results in prices racing to the bottom as the underlying
low costs are being passed directly on to consumers.

 REASONINGBANK: SCALING AGENT SELF-EVOLVING WITH REASONING MEMORY (1
MINUTE READ) [13] 

 ReasoningBank is a novel memory framework that distills generalizable
reasoning strategies from an agent's self-judged successful and failed
experiences. Agents can store and retrieve memories from ReasoningBank
to inform their decisions. They can integrate new learnings and become
more capable over time. The better memory enables more effective
scaling. ReasoningBank consistently outperforms existing memory
mechanisms that store raw trajectories or only successful task
routines across web browsing and software engineering benchmarks.

 WHY AMERICA BUILDS AI GIRLFRIENDS AND CHINA BUILDS AI BOYFRIENDS (15
MINUTE READ) [15] 

 A market scan of 110 AI companion platforms reveals stark
differences: 52% are US-based, targeting young men with

features. Chinese platforms target adult women aged 25-40 with dynamic
AI boyfriends featuring card-collecting mechanics and WeChat

 THE CHATGPT APP STORE MOMENT (2 MINUTE READ) [16] 

 OpenAI just launched apps inside ChatGPT where you can say "Spotify,
make me a playlist" or "DoorDash, order my usual" and it handles
everything conversationally. This looks like early iPhone web apps,
not true native experiences yet, but the trajectory is clear. As
OpenAI expands its SDK to give developers access to user memory,
persistent state, and action execution, we'll see actual native
ChatGPT applications designed for conversational interfaces.
