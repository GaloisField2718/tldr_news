 ANTHROPIC IS ON TRACK TO TURN A PROFIT MUCH FASTER THAN OPENAI (6
MINUTE READ) [6] 

 OpenAI forecasts that it won't turn a profit until 2030, while
Anthropic expects to break even for the first time in 2027. OpenAI is
also expected to burn roughly 14 times as much cash as Anthropic
before turning a profit. The two most valuable AI startups are taking
vastly different approaches to growth. OpenAI is investing heavily in
chips and data centers. Anthropic is taking a more cautious approach,
making sure that costs grow more in line with revenue.

 OMNILINGUAL ASR (GITHUB REPO) [12] 

 Omnilingual ASR is a speech recognition system that supports over
1,600 languages, with hundreds never before covered by other ASR
technology. New languages can be added with just a few paired examples
without specialized expertise or large datasets. Omnilingual ASR is
designed for broad accessibility - it is aimed at making speech
technology more inclusive and adaptable for communities and
researchers worldwide. 

 EGOCENTRIC-10K (HUGGING FACE REPO) [13] 

 Egocentric-10K is a hand visibility and active manipulation data set
with 10,000 hours of video. It contains 192,900 video clips of 2,138
workers completing tasks in real factories. A video showing examples
of some of the clips is available in the repository. 

 AMA WITH MOONSHOT AI, THE OPEN-SOURCE FRONTIER LAB BEHIND KIMI K2
THINKING MODEL (REDDIT THREAD) [14] 

 Moonshot AI's Kimi K2 is a Mixture-of-Experts model with 32 billion
activated parameters and 1 trillion total parameters. It achieves
state-of-the-art performance in frontier knowledge, math, and coding
among non-thinking models and also has agentic abilities. Kimi-K2-Base
and Kimi-K2-Instruct were recently open sourced. This thread contains
a discussion with the team at Moonshot AI.

 EVERY AI APPLICATION STARTUP IS LIKELY TO BE CRUSHED BY RAPID
EXPANSION OF THE FOUNDATIONAL MODEL PROVIDERS (4 MINUTE READ) [15] 

 There is now almost no time to build a company and scale it. There
are two ways an AI application founder can make money: make a
flash-in-the-pan app that generates a ton of cash over a short period,
or make a good enough app to get acquired by one of the big players.
The situation is highly unstable. The best way to play is to find an
application niche in a highly specialized field with extremely unique
and specific data barriers.
