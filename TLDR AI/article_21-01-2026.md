 THE TRAGEDY OF THE AGENTIC COMMONS (14 MINUTE READ) [10]

 ANTHROPIC CEO: SELLING H200S TO CHINA IS LIKE GIVING NUKES TO NORTH

authorities have yet to allow local buyers to acquire the GPUs.
Anthropic wants stricter controls on AI exports, saying access to the
chips would put Chinese model developers in a better position to
compete with the West.
