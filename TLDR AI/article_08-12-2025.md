 META ACQUIRES AI DEVICE STARTUP LIMITLESS (3 MINUTE READ) [5] 

 Meta has acquired Limitless, a startup known for its AI pendant that
records conversations. Limitless will cease hardware sales but
continue to offer support for a year. Its team will focus on
supporting Meta's existing AR and AI wearables rather than launching
new products. The acquisition comes amid rising competition in AI
hardware as major players like Meta expand their device offerings. 

 GOOGLE SET TO LAUNCH GEMINI NANO BANANA 2 FLASH (2 MINUTE READ) [6] 

 Google plans to launch the Gemini Nano Banana 2 Flash model, which
matches the performance of the Nano Banana 2 Pro, at a lower cost. The
move targets a broader user base by providing high-quality outputs
suitable for large-scale deployments. Code references suggest an
announcement is imminent, aligning with Google's strategy of model
tiering for cost optimization.

 ANNOUNCING RNJ-1: BUILDING INSTRUMENTS OF INTELLIGENCE (14 MINUTE
READ) [10] 

 Rnj-1 is a world-class pair of base and instruction-tuned large
language models. The 8B model roughly follows the open-source Gemma 3
architecture. The Rnj-1 Base and Instruct models compare favorably
against similarly sized open weight models. This post summarizes the
key capabilities of the models and covers the background behind their
development. 

 TITANS + MIRAS: HELPING AI HAVE LONG-TERM MEMORY (12 MINUTE READ)
[11] 

 Google Research has released two new papers introducing an
architecture (Titans) and a theoretical blueprint (MIRAS) that combine
the speed of recurrent neural networks with the accuracy of
transformers. Together, Titans and MIRAS advance the concept of
test-time memorization, the ability of an AI model to maintain
long-term memory. They introduce a meaningful shift toward real-time
adaptation. The architecture actively learns and updates its own
parameters as data streams in, enabling the model to incorporate new,
specific details into its core knowledge instantly. 

 CUTILE PYTHON (GITHUB REPO) [12] 

 cuTile Python is a programming language for writing parallel kernels
for NVIDIA GPUs. It automatically leverages advanced hardware
capabilities while providing portability across different NVIDIA GPU
architectures. cuTile enables the latest hardware features without
requiring code changes.

 COMPUTER-SCIENCE REINFORCEMENT LEARNING GOT REWARDS WRONG (6 MINUTE
READ) [13] 

 In reinforcement learning, the reward is part of the agent, not part
of the environment. Once we start thinking in terms of the reward
translation as being part of the agent, we can also start thinking of
ways in which the agent can affect the reward translation mechanism.
It could be dynamic and change across time, be tied to policy, or even
learned. The shift in perspective creates many possible options, just
by changing the notation - because notation matters.
